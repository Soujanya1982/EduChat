TITLE: Visitor and Event Parking : University of Dayton, Ohio

Visitor and Event Parking
All vehicles parked on University of Dayton property must display a valid UD parking permit or visitor permit during these times:
Monday through Thursday: 6 a.m. - 7:30 p.m.
Friday: 6 a.m. - 4:30 p.m.
Permits are
not
required outside these hours, as long as you park in a visitor space or in a single letter lot (e.g., Lot A, Lot B, Lot C, etc.).
You
must
purchase a permit if you park on campus for more than two consecutive weeks, or more than three times per month.
Obtaining a Visitor Permit
A visitor permit is required to park in the visitor parking spaces. To obtain a visitor permit, either:
Complete the
request form for guest/event parking
.
Stop by one of our visitors centers, which are located on
University Circle
and in
Lot C
.
The visitors centers are open:
Monday through Thursday: 7:30 a.m. - 7:30 p.m.
Friday: 7:30 a.m. - 4:30 p.m.
Group Events
For group events, contact Parking Services at
parking@udayton.edu
or 937-229-2128 to inquire about space availability and obtain permits.
Delivery Spaces
15- and 30-minute delivery spaces are available in certain locations. No permit is needed for these short-term spots, but emergency flashers are required.
Motorist Assistance Program
If you are parked on University property and experience a problem with your vehicle, we can provide the following assistance free-of-charge:
Battery booster service
Escort to obtain gas
Vehicle lock-out service
Tire inflation service
To request services, contact the Motorist Assistance Program at 937-229-2121.
