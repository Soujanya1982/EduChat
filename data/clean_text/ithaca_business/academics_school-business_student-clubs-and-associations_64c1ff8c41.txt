TITLE: Student Clubs and Associations | Ithaca College

The School of Business offers student professional organizations related to all types of business disciplines.
You are encouraged to take an active role in the school's student clubs and associations, as they provide opportunities to develop your managerial and leadership skills and connect your classroom experiences to real-world issues.
Whether you're interested in portfolio research, accounting, marketing, sustainability, or any number of other business topics, you'll be able to find a student club that helps you pursue your passions. Click on any organization name in the left-hand navigation to read more about each club's mission and activities.
Officers of each student organization serve on the Dean's Student Advisory Council (DSAC), which meets monthly with the dean and faculty advisers to share information and coordinate activities.
