MBA Public Relations
Learn to steer and manage public communications on behalf of companies and organizations by adding coursework in PR campaigns and management, persuasive messaging, strategic communication and branding to your Lerner MBA. Courses in this specialization are taught by full-time faculty and successful PR practitioners.
Online Format
The courses for the public relations major or concentration are offered online. You may take MBA courses outside of the specialization in person or online.
Program Requirements
The
Lerner MBA’s
core business courses and professional development classes give you a solid base of business management knowledge. Then choose a public relations specialization option.
Major in Public Relations
The major requires 5 courses and offers an in-depth study of public relations.
Major Courses (15 credits)
Required course:
COMM 702 – Principles of Strategic Communication
Choose 4 from:
COMM 719 – Reputation Management
COMM 721 – Public Relations Campaigns
COMM 722 – Crisis Communication
COMM 723 – Strategic Communication Campaigns
COMM 725 – Branding
COMM 727 – PR Management
COMM 728 – Strategic Communication and Persuasion
COMM 729 – Topics in Public Relations
COMM 756 – Advanced Strategic Communication Writing
Concentration in Public Relations
The concentration requires 3 courses and offers a broad view of public relations.
Concentration Courses (9 credits)
Required course:
COMM 702 – Principles of Strategic Communication
Choose 2 from:
COMM 719 – Reputation Management
COMM 721 – Public Relations Campaigns
COMM 722 – Crisis Communication
COMM 723 – Strategic Communication Campaigns
COMM 725 – Branding
COMM 727 – PR Management
COMM 728 – Strategic Communication and Persuasion
