Bauer Ambassadors
Bauer Ambassadors (BA) is a program for an elite group of undergraduate business students who represent Bauer College, its spirit and values, to the campus and community. Bauer Ambassadors represent the college at key events and activities for the Dean's Office, Undergraduate Business Programs (UBP), and the Rockwell Career Center (RCC), and serve as Ethics Advocates for their fellow students. Those events and activities include college tours, new student orientations, career events, dean’s events, commencement, and presentations to students on the importance of academic achievement and honesty, and being engaged in the college.

Leadership
Bauer Ambassadors offers a comprehensive approach to student development. Through professional development workshops covering practical skills, engaging general meetings for shared experiences, and insightful guest speakers, students are equipped with the knowledge and unity needed to navigate the dynamic business landscape successfully.
Service
As a Bauer Ambassador, you will be presented with the invaluable opportunity to actively serve and represent your community through a diverse range of engaging activities. These initiatives include community service projects, connecting with high school students to share the college experience. By embracing these opportunities you contribute to the positive impact and social responsibility that defines the Bauer Ambassador experience.
Culture
The Bauer Ambassadors includes mentorship programs that create a supportive network for personal and career development. The ambassadors emphasize a family-oriented environment that builds strong interpersonal relationships. Fostering a positive and collaborative atmosphere that facilitates overall growth and well-being within the group.
Membership Requirements
Bauer Ambassador Membership
*BA Membership is limited to 45 students*
A currently enrolled Pre-Business or declared Business major with a cumulative GPA of 3.0 or higher
(Must have completed a semester)
Must be enrolled full-time and maintain at least a 3.0 GPA
Commit to serving as an Ambassador for 1 year (fall and spring semesters)
Attend all mandatory training sessions and meetings
Fulfill the established point requirement by volunteering for a certain number of events each semester
Agree to all terms and conditions listed on the Bauer Ambassador Contract
Application Process
Applications for the 2026-2027 academic year will be available on July 15, 2026.

Interview invitations will be sent to applicants by September 11, 2026.
Final selections will be made by September 25, 2026.
