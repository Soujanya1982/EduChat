Math Requirements
All PC Business students are required to demonstrate proficiency in finite mathematics by completing the following requirements:
Successful completion of MTH 108 Mathematics for Business Analysis II (minimum requirement for all business majors); or, MTH 109 Calculus I, MTH 131 Calculus and Analytical Geometry I, or MTH 132 Calculus and Analytical Geometry II. Math requirements for PCSB students are linked to intended career paths and majors. Students will complete a math placement exam prior to orientation – they will be registered for a math course (pending availability) based on their placement score. The chart below provides details on math requirements, to be used as a guide for students and advisors to determine, with math placement scores, the appropriate math course.
