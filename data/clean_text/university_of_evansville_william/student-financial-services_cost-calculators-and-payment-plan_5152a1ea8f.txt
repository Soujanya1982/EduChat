Cost Calculators
The University of Evansville's Cost Calculators will help you calculate an estimate of your directly-billed charges and any remaining balance you may owe the University. The calculator also serves as the UE Monthly Payment Plan application. To use this customizable tool, select from the options below.

Net Price Calculator
at CollegeCosts.com
(NPC). This tool combines institutional data from the 2023-24 academic year along with information about yourself and family finances to provide an estimated net price to attend UE.
