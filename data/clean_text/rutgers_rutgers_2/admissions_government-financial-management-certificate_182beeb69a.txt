Online Graduate Certificate in Governmental Financial Management
Our online Graduate Certificate in Government Financial Management is a great alternative if you aren't quite ready to pursue a complete master's program.
By completing only four online courses as a non-matriculated student, you can earn a Graduate Certificate in Government Financial Management from Rutgers Business School. These courses will also prepare you to sit for the
Certified Government Financial Manager (CGFM) exam
sponsored by the Association of Government Accountants. If you decide to matriculate later on, these courses can be applied toward the 10-course master’s degree program.
Required certificate courses:
Governmental Accounting and Auditing
Public Sector Auditing
Governmental Budgeting Systems
Design and Development of Information Systems
