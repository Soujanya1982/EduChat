Tuition
Class fees for Univ+ session classes are charged per credit, at $495 per credit. Full-time students, taking an estimate of 30 credits, would pay $14,850 per year. Part-time students, taking an estimate of 15 credits, would pay $7,425 per year.  Additional fees include a $175 one-time undergraduate entering fee and an $90 registration fee per semester.
Financial Aid
To apply for financial aid at UMass Amherst you only need to complete the Free Application for Federal Student Aid, commonly called
FAFSA
. Students enrolled in at least six credits during a semester may be eligible for a subsidized or unsubsidized loan.
