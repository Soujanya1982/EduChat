Tuition and Costs
Instead of a traditional 16-week semester, you can earn the same college credit in half the time—and at half the price of our traditional day program—through classes built for eight-week terms.
Tuition for our online degree completion programs is
$574 per credit hour
.
In addition, you can earn up to 30 credit hours in one day through a behavioral assessment in our
CORE Competency Assessment Center
.
Financial Aid
Lipscomb offers the same high-quality degree as our traditional on-campus programs with the flexibility to fit your life at a cost that’s half the price. Earn a Lipscomb education in a way that fits your budget through financial aid opportunities like scholarships, grants, loans and part-time employment.
Grants
You may qualify for federal grants, such as the Pell Grant, or grants from other sources specific to your program of study and personal background.
Grants
Loans
Finance your education through one or more loans, either from the federal government or a private lender, that you can repay after college.
Loans
Scholarships
Depending on your credentials, you may qualify for scholarships from federal, state and other sources.
Scholarships
Tuition Discounts
If your employer offers tuition assistance or has a corporate partnership with Lipscomb University, you may be eligible for an application fee waiver and reduced tuition.
Partners
Military Benefits
Veterans, military members and their families attending Lipscomb may qualify for a variety of aid, including scholarships, stipends and tuition assistance.
Military Benefits
