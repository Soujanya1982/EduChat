Transfer Students By the Numbers
Students enter the Poole College of Management through one of three methods:
Direct admission as a first-time, first-year student
Off-campus transfer
On-campus transfer (Change of Degree Application)
Through each process, prospective students gain access to a wealth of streamlined application resources, and receive a thorough understanding of the requirements for successfully completing our degree programs.
