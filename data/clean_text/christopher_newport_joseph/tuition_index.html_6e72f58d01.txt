2025-26 Comprehensive Rate
The comprehensive rate is a mandatory student charge used to support non-instructional activities and programs in auxiliary enterprises, such as intercollegiate athletics, recreational and intramural activities, student and cultural activities, health services, student union, facility maintenance, and debt service. It is assessed to all students, does not distinguish between in-state and out-of-state students, and is considered mandatory.

Books and supplies are additional out-of-pocket expenses. These expenses should not be included with payments for tuition and fees. Such expenses depending on your major and class load can be estimated.  In addition to tuition and fees, there are other costs that should be considered when planning for college. Christopher Newport has developed an estimated Cost of Attendance that factors in direct costs like tuition and fees, as well as other indirect costs that won't be paid directly to the university.
