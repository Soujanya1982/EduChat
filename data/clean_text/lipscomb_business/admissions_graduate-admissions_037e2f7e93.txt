TITLE: Graduate Admission | Lipscomb University

Whether you’ve just earned your undergraduate degree, wish to advance your career or simply want to explore a new field, Lipscomb University offers an innovative range of graduate programs that will help you take the best next step for your future.
With more than 85 graduate degrees, certificates and endorsements offered, Lipscomb is one of the largest resources in middle Tennessee for post-baccalaureate education. There are a range of flexible schedules and locations, along with online options, ensuring you don’t have to put your life on hold to continue your education.
Our programs are built for people who are professionally driven and passionate about learning. Engage in rigorous and rewarding scholarship that will build on your existing skills and knowledge, giving you confidence to emerge at the forefront of your field.

Affording a Graduate Education
Lipscomb University offers a variety of financial aid avenues from assistantships to veteran aid. We aim to invest in every student as they invest in us, and we’re glad to be a top-performing institution for return on investment and below-average student debt. Feel free to contact the Financial Aid Office to learn more about how we can help you achieve your goals.
