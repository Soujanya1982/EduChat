Cost
Below are the estimated tuition and fees for CAP. Please remember that costs vary depending on the number of hours taken per semester and the status of the student.
Residents
3 hours per semester: $2,708
6 hours per semester: $4,620
9 hours per semester: $6,532
Non-Residents
3 hours per semester: $4,306
6 hours per semester: $7,815
9 hours per semester: $11,325
International Students
3 hours per semester: $5,589
*
9 hours per semester: $12,608
*
12 hours per semester: $16,115
*
*
For international students, medical insurance or proof of acceptable insurance is also required.
A $650 CAP fee is assessed on all 5000-level courses to offset the fact that CAP, as a non-degree seeking program, is not funded by the State of Texas.
This fee is included in the estimates listed above.
These costs are based on
Fiscal Year 2026
, which includes the Fall 2025, Spring 2026 and Summer 2026 semesters. The cost per credit hour for residents is $420.57 and non-residents is $953.11. For a complete list of optional and special fees, including fees for international students, please visit the
university fees webpage
.
Program costs are subject to change without prior notice. Complete estimated cost breakdown information is available upon request.
Financial Aid
Students in certificate programs do not qualify for
financial aid
unless they are taking specific classes in preparation for the
Master of Science in Accountancy Program
, in which case funding is limited to a 12-month period. Such financial aid only covers undergraduate tuition and fees, but not the additional CAP fee.
Scholarships
To be considered for scholarships awarded by the Department of Accountancy & Taxation’s scholarship committee, please submit Bauer’s
scholarship application
. Scholarships awarded by the committee include those offered through the college, department, and potentially external scholarships available through one of our business partners or organizations. Eligibility requirements and deadlines vary for each external scholarship.
For questions regarding scholarship eligibility and the awarding process, please view
our frequently asked questions
.
If you have questions about the scholarship application form, please email
bauer_scholar@central.uh.edu
.
