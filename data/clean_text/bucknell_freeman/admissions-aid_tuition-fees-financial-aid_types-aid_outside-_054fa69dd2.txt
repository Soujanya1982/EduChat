TITLE: Outside Scholarships

Outside Scholarships
Federal regulations and Bucknell policy stipulate that the total gift aid from any source (grants, scholarships, stipends, educational awards, tuition benefits, VA benefits and any other funding for educational expenses that is not required to be repaid)
cannot exceed Bucknell's cost of attendance
.
Included in our cost of attendance are tuition, a student activity fee, housing, a meal plan and allowances for books, course materials, supplies and equipment. If this cost is reached with total gift aid, Bucknell aid (need-based and/or merit-based) will be reduced dollar for dollar to prevent an award surplus. Based on actual determined financial need, federal/state/other aid may need to be adjusted as well.
If a student is selected to receive Alston funding and is only receiving athletic funding along with a Pell Grant, the Alston funding will be excluded from our gift aid policy.

Examples
Example 1
Plus Icon (Large)
2025-2026 cost of attendance: $91,836. This includes:
$69,976 Tuition
$600 Activity fee
$10,846 Housing
$6,714 Food
$3,700 (Our estimate of books, course materials, supplies and personal expenses)
If the initial financial aid package is this:
$77,446 Total Bucknell Aid (of any kind, need-based and/or merit)
$6,890 Federal Pell Grant
$5,500 Federal Direct Loan
$2,000 Federal Work-study
$91,836 Total financial aid package
And if the student receives $10,000 in outside scholarships, the financial aid package would be revised to this:
$74,946 Total Bucknell Aid (of any kind, need-based and/or merit)
$6,890 Federal Pell Grant
$10,000 Outside Scholarships
$0 Federal Direct Loan (reduced to 0)
$0 Federal Work-study (reduced to 0)
$91,836 Total financial aid package
Due to the outside scholarships, Bucknell Aid had to be reduced to ensure the total financial aid package did not exceed the cost of attendance. Additionally, loan and work-study amounts were adjusted.

Other Key Points
Total gift aid cannot exceed our cost of attendance.
Total aid package cannot exceed our cost of attendance. However, if a student is selected to receive Alston funding and is only receiving athletic funding along with a Pell Grant, the Alston funding will be excluded from our gift aid policy.
Specific federal need levels may require federal aid adjustments as well.
Outside scholarships count as student resources and are part of the financial aid package.
Outside scholarships are typically applied in equal amounts to the fall and spring semesters, unless the awarding organization specifies otherwise. If an agency instructs that the full amount be applied to one term, we will follow those instructions.
Students need to be sure and send the Office of Financial Aid copies of any outside funding letters that they receive. If an outside agency should remit their funds after the semester bill is due, please be advised that late fees will incur as a result.
