TITLE: Business Administration: Entrepreneurial Studies, B.S. - PAUL, UNH | University of New Hampshire

What is entrepreneurial studies?
Entrepreneurial studies is not just marketing, management, or finance — it’s all of that and more. If you are creative and want to bring that creativity and innovation into your profession — whether you are interested in starting your own business, joining a family business, working for a new venture, investing in start-ups, or becoming involved in new venture creation within an established organization — then entrepreneurial studies is a great choice.
