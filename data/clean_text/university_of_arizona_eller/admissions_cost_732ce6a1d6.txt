PhD Program Funding
Program and Funding
Learn More
PhD in Accounting
All students in Eller’s Accounting PhD program are awarded a generous financial package that covers full tuition. In addition, all students receive a stipend for living expenses.
Accounting Doctoral Admissions »
PhD in Economics
Tuition is waived for Economics PhD students. Most students are also funded with a living stipend of approximately $21,000 for serving as a graduate associate.
Economics Doctoral Admissions »
PhD in Finance
Finance PhD students receive full funding and have tuition waived and a living stipend of approximately $27,500 for serving as a graduate teaching assistant. Students are responsible for miscellaneous university fees of about $650 per semester.
Finance Doctoral Admissions »
PhD in Management
Management PhD students receive funding to cover the cost of tuition. Students also typically receive a research assistantship stipend which starts around $20,000 per year.
Management Doctoral Admissions »
PhD in Management Information Systems
Doctoral students in the MIS program receive full funding through research or teaching assistantships.
MIS Doctoral Admissions »
PhD in Marketing
Most Marketing PhD students are offered full funding through research or teaching associateships.
Marketing Doctoral Admissions »
