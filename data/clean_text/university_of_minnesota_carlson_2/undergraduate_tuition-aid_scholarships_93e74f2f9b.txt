For Incoming Students:
Minnesota Carlson works to provide as much support as possible to incoming and current students. New freshman and transfer students are given consideration for awards at the time of admission. All scholarship recipients are awarded on a rolling basis following admission and notified by April.
Beyond Minnesota Carlson support, there are numerous scholarship opportunities available through external programs. A wide list of external scholarships can be found on the resources below.

For Current Students:
Each spring Minnesota Carlson opens the application process for a wide variety of scholarships that support currently enrolled students. Most awards have specific criteria including financial need, year of study and/or major, and are awarded for a single year ranging from $1,000 - $7,500. In most cases, preference will be given to students who have not already received an award for the upcoming academic year.
