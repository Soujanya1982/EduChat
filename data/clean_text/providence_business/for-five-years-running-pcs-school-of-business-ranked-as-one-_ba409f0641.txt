TITLE: PC Business ranked "top 50 best undergrad business schools"

For five years running, PC’s School of Business ranked as one of the “top 50 best undergraduate business schools”
Posted on
January 22, 2024
For the fifth year in a row, the Providence College School of Business (PCSB) has been ranked in the top 50 by
Poets&Quants For Undergrads
,
reaching #37 in the 2024 ranking.
The PCSB placed exceptionally well in the academic experience category, where alumni feedback put the PCSB at number 28 among the 91 ranked business schools. “This ranking for academic experience puts the PCSB’s integrated liberal arts business school almost exclusively in the company of many larger, nationally recognized business-only programs,” comments Dean Sylvia Maxfield.
The PCSB’s academic experience and career outcomes categories are proof of the return on investment in a PCSB education that comes from the PCSB’s unique experiential learning opportunities – both inside and outside the classroom, strong relationships between faculty and students, study abroad internships, and superlative alumni support for career discovery and preparation.
Poets&Quants
utilizes a comprehensive methodology for its ranking, looking at factors like academic experience, employment outcomes, and admissions standards. Results are based on school-reported data and a survey of recent graduates.
The PC School of Business ranked #3 among New England schools and is the top ranked liberal arts school in the region.
