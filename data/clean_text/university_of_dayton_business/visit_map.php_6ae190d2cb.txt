Explore Campus
We invite you to explore the University of Dayton's campus, which blends historic halls with state-of-the-art facilities. Please note that no firearms or weapons are allowed on campus
*
.
*
Consistent with applicable law, individuals who lawfully own or possess a concealed handgun or any other weapon may only have it on campus if it is stored in a locked motor vehicle or if the individual is in the immediate process of placing the weapon in a locked motor vehicle. Such individuals may not have such a weapon anywhere else on campus.
