Eligibility
To be eligible for admission to the graduate programs at Marian University, you must meet all of the following requirements:
Hold a minimum of baccalaureate degree or its equivalent from a regionally accredited college or university;
Have maintained a minimum of B grade point average in your undergraduate major courses and demonstrate sufficient academic achievement to be successful in advanced scholarship and research;
Complete and submit the Graduate Studies online application form;
Submit all required test scores and other supplemental materials by specified departmental deadlines;
Remit all required fees for admission, both to the Office of Graduate Admission and to specific departments, if applicable.
Apply Now
Specific graduate programs may have supplemental admissions requirements that exceed general acceptance into our graduate studies. You will be required to submit a separate application and be accepted into the specific graduate program you intend to pursue.
If you have questions about specific programs, departments or majors, please go to the specific link on the Graduate Studies website.
