Civic Engagement & Community Service
enhance the lives of others & your own
Whether you’re building homes for families, helping feed community members, or supporting afterschool programs, your service strengthens communities while shaping your own sense of purpose and leadership.
At Susquehanna University, education and community go hand in hand. The Community Engagement Office connects students, faculty and staff with real-world opportunities that put classroom learning into action and make a tangible difference in the region. You’ll discover countless ways to get involved — beginning with a summer service opportunity before your first year and continuing through our annual day of service.
Recognizing that civic engagement is a vital part of a liberal arts education, the David ’69 and Sharon Johnson Center for Civic Engagement offers pathways for every student to reflect on their roles as active, informed citizens. Serving as both a hub and a catalyst, the Johnson Center supports experiences that foster leadership, social responsibility and meaningful community impact.
Hands-On Experience in Service and Leadership
AMPLIFY Internship Program
The Amplify program connects you with local nonprofit and public service organizations through paid, experiential-learning internships. You’ll apply your academic knowledge to real-world challenges while supporting initiatives that strengthen communities. By building professional skills and creating meaningful impact, Amplify prepares you to lead and serve beyond the classroom.
Kickstart Your Service Journey
SU GIVE: First-Year Day of Service
SU GIVE brings you together with fellow students, faculty and staff for a hands-on day of community service during orientation. Working alongside local organizations, you’ll begin building connections while making a meaningful difference. This shared experience launches your commitment to civic engagement and service at Susquehanna.
Voter Engagement & Civic Leadership Opportunities
ALL IN Campus Democracy Challenge
The ALL IN Challenge advances civic learning, voter engagement and democratic participation among college students. At Susquehanna, you’ll find support to register, vote and take an active role in shaping your community. Through inclusive initiatives, the program empowers you to become an informed and engaged citizen.

Community Partnerships & Regional Initiatives
Strengthening Our Region Together
Your impact extends beyond campus through partnerships with local businesses, nonprofits and public service organizations. These collaborations support initiatives in education, sustainability, public health and more — strengthening the social and economic fabric of the region. Through collaborative relationships, you’ll help create meaningful outcomes for communities while deepening your understanding of civic responsibility.
