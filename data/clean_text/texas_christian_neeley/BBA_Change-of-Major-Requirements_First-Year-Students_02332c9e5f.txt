First Year Students
TCU first-year students that would like to change their major to business,
MUST meet the following requirements:
Have a 3.25 TCU cumulative GPA or higher
Be enrolled in their second semester at TCU or later
Submit essay with application
Required completion with a 2.75 GPA in the courses below:
ENGL 10803 – Composition I
ECON 10223 – Microeconomics
ECON 10233 – Macroeconomics
MATH 10283 – Applied Calculus
*
NOTE: Each of these courses must be taken for a grade. Pass/No Credit is not allowed.
Changing your major may result in delaying graduation.
