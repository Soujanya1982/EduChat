MBA Accounting
Hone your skills and knowledge in financial or managerial accounting, information systems, auditing and/or taxation with advanced level classes in accounting and MIS.
Your specialized accounting skills, combined with Lerner’s well-rounded MBA, will benefit you in positions that interact with accounting, business and strategic planning.
Format
The Lerner MBA with specialization in accounting is an on-campus program. You can also take several courses online, giving you more flexibility and allowing you to complete your degree faster.
