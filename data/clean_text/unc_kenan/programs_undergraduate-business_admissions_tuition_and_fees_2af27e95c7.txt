Business Program Enhancement Fee
Once admitted to the Undergraduate Business Program (UBP), you will be charged a Program Enhancement Fee (PEF) per semester, which covers many aspects of the undergraduate program: student programming and scholarships, expanded student advising, career, study abroad and wellness services, and club funding, to name a few. As of the Fall 2023 term, majors are charged $1,000 per term and minors (Business Administration, Business of Health, and Real Estate) are charged $500 per term. PEF will begin the semester you are enrolled as a major or minor within the UBP. The fee is administered each semester you are enrolled as an UBP student.
UNC Kenan-Flagler’s Undergraduate Business Program is the last public, top-25 ranked (by Bloomberg Businessweek)  undergraduate business program to implement a differential fee or tuition.  Private schools do not charge a similar, differential fee as the revenues from their much higher tuition negate the need. Even with the fee, the UBP remains the lowest cost undergraduate business program of all our peers.
What if I face financial hardship because of the fee?
If you have completed the financial aid process through UNC’s Office of Scholarship and Student Aid, and financial need has been determined, you will
automatically
receive an offset to cover the fee.
Other admitted students facing undue hardship as a result of the
fee may apply for a scholarship/grant to cover PEF via

.
Students will need to apply each semester to request relief.
