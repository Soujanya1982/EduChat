TITLE: Undergraduate Advisors | Drexel University's LeBow College of Business

Our staff of full-time developmental advisors serve as an important resource to our students as they choose and attain their educational, career, and life goals. Our advisors form on-going relationships with our students as they progress through their college years to assist them with goal-formation, selecting a degree program and making choices from the wide array of options offered by Drexel University.
The goal of our developmental advisors is to foster the empowerment of our students to become skillful problem solvers and experienced decision makers who are well-positioned to emerge as the business leaders of the future following graduation from LeBow College of Business.
