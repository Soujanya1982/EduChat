Career Peers
Career Peers
Career Peers are Isenberg juniors and seniors trained by the Office of Career Success professional staff to assist other students in their career development. In the 2024-2025 school year, Career Peers held over 2,300 appointments with first-year and sophomore students as well as scores of walk-in appointments. The Career Peer Program is supported by contributions from generous Isenberg alumni!
