Transfer Students
Our academic community will support, to the best of our ability, your efforts to complete your degree in a timely manner. We understand that you will have lots of questions, many of which can be answered by our
Academic Bulletin
. If you wish to participate in a Transfer-to-Business Information Session, please visit our
Virtual Reception Room
to schedule one.
Please take a moment to review the valuable resources below.
Transfer to Miami Herbert from another university
Are you attending another university or college? This video provides some important information about transitioning to Miami Herbert.
Transfer to Miami Herbert from another school or college at UM
If you are already a student at the U, the process for transitioning to Miami Herbert is quite straightforward. It is essential that you are enrolled in MTH 161 (Calculus 1) or have already earned college credit for an equivalent calculus course
before
you initiate the process to transition from your home school or college to Miami Herbert.
Take a few moments to learn more about the process by viewing this video. Please make sure to review the
Internal Transfer Process Guide
as well.
Miami Herbert Equivalencies
Transfer students who are newly admitted to the University of Miami must submit their transfer course work for evaluation to
Miami Academic Transfer Evaluation System (MATES)
.
Questions about the admission process or status of your application should be directed to UM’s
Undergraduate Admissions Office
.
