Apply to WPI: Everything You Need to Know
WPI Application Overview
All first-year students apply to WPI using the Common Application.
You have three options to choose from:
Early decision
When you know, you know. If WPI is your No. 1 choice, why wait? Students who apply early decision receive a minimum
$25,000 scholarship each year
when admitted—for a total of $100,000+ over four years.
By applying early decision, you enter a binding agreement to enroll at WPI upon acceptance.
Learn more →
Early action
Applying early action is a great way to show your interest in WPI without the full commitment of early decision. You’ll receive your admission decision by late January or late February.
Regular decision
Regular decision’s later application deadline gives you more time to perfect your application and show the Admissions team all you’ve achieved during your senior year.
