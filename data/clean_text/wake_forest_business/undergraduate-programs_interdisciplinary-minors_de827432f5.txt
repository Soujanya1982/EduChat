Minors offered within the Wake Forest College of Arts and Sciences can complement the degree programs offered within the School of Business, especially the Business and Enterprise Management degree. Although minors should be chosen based on an individual student’s goals and interests, the following interdisciplinary minors are highlighted because certain electives taken within the School of Business also qualify as electives for these minors.
Entrepreneurship and Social Enterprise
Global Trade and Commerce
International Studies
