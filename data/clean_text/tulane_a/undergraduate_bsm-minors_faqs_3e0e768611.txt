TITLE: Business Minor FAQs | Freeman School of Business Tulane University

Q: Who can help me figure out if I have room for the minor?
A:
Your Newcomb-Tulane College (NTC) academic advisor will help you understand how to integrate the minor into your degree.
Q: Is there a GPA requirement for the minor?
A:
Students must maintain a minimum 2.0 GPA to complete the minor.
Q: Do I have to complete the business minor to apply to a 4+1 program?
A:
The business minor is the optimal pathway for non-BSM students to take the fundamental business coursework needed to take full advantage of the MFIN, MME, or MANA 4+1 programs. While one could stop short of completing the business minor, after completing the necessary coursework needed for one of our 4+1 programs, it would make sense to earn the minor after putting in so much effort.
Q: When should I apply to the 4+1?
A:
You can apply beginning August of your senior year. Most students apply in the spring semester, but later applications (May–July) are also accepted.
Q: Can transfer courses count towards minor requirements?
A:
With the exception of INFO 1010, minor requirements must be completed at Tulane.
Q: Can students pursuing the Freeman Business Minor use the Career Management Center and other Freeman resources?
A:
We encourage students to use the Freeman Career Management Center  and join student clubs that support Freeman students. This will be your “Freeman Experience,” too!
