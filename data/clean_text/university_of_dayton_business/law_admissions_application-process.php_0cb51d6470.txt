TITLE: Application Process : University of Dayton, Ohio

J.D. Application Process
When you apply to Dayton Law, our admissions committee not only considers your GPA and LSAT performance (or GRE or JD-Next), but also, life experience, extracurricular activities, leadership, ability to overcome hardships, motivation and a breadth and depth of skills and interests. We believe a well-rounded entering class will contribute to an exciting, educational and dynamic law school experience.
