Majors & Programs
At Roger Williams University (RWU), we offer over 65 accredited undergraduate majors, more than 70 minors, comprehensive graduate programs, professional certificates, trade and technical training, and a highly respected Juris Doctorate (JD) law degree. Our diverse academic offerings are designed to help students achieve career success through hands-on learning, experiential education, and interdisciplinary studies.
