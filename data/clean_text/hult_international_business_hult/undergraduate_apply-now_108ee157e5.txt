A fully supported enrollment process
Your Hult journey is unique and will be personalized to you. This starts the day you get in touch with us and continues through graduation—and beyond.
Our team of international education specialists is here to make your journey to Hult as seamless and stress-free as possible.
From the moment you start your application, you’ll be paired with a dedicated enrollment advisor to guide you and your family every step of the way. They’ll provide personalized advice on finances, scholarships, campus options, and the application process. You’ll also have a visa coordinator to ensure a smooth and safe arrival on campus.
