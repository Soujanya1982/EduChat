UE is a private, liberal arts and sciences-based university affiliated with the United Methodist church.
It was founded in 1854. Renamed Evansville College in 1919 and then renamed to the University of Evansville in 1967.
Study abroad at Harlaxton. It is our 100-room Victorian manor in Grantham, England. It's the experience of a lifetime.
Aces athletics is part of the Missouri Valley Conference and participates in 17 Division 1 sports.
The UE campus has 75 acres, 31 major buildings and is home to more than 2,0-00 undergraduate, graduate and adult students.
Evansville Indiana has a population of 118,000. It is the third largets city in Indiana behind Indianapolis and Fort Wayne.
UE has three colleges: the College of Business and Engineering, the College of Education and Health Sciences, and the William L. Ridgway College of Arts and Sciences.
The average class size is 15.
The student-to-faculty ratio is 10 to 1.
Seven percent of students are international.
There are eleven graduate programs.
There are 75+ undergraduate programs.
The male-to-female ratio is 2:3.
The average UE, Federal, and State scholarship and grant package is $39,595.
There are over 100+ student organizations on campus.
97 percent of the Class of 2025 graduates were either employed or pursuing further education within six months of graduation.
UE represents 45 countries and 44 states.
