TITLE: Tuition & Financial Aid - Undergraduate

Penn is affordable.
College is a major investment, and the university is committed to working with families to make a Penn education affordable to all students. All financial aid is based on each student’s demonstrated financial need. As a member of the Ivy League, Penn does not offer merit-based financial aid or athletic scholarships.
