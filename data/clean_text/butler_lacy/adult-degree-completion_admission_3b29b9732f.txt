How to Apply
To apply, candidates should submit the following:
Butler Application
Official Transcripts or copies of official transcripts from all the colleges and universities where candidates:
Earned a degree
Earned more than 15 credits (undergraduate and graduate)
Completed any program prerequisite (regardless of number of credit hours)
Completed courses for which candidates are applying for transfer credit
International applicants must additionally submit the following:
TOEFL scores or equivalent proof of
English proficiency
A clear copy of your passport
Evidence of financial support
Transcripts
Copies of official transcripts may be uploaded in the online application for application review only. Applicants who receive an offer of admission will be required at that time to submit official final transcripts issued directly from the institution(s) for verification prior to enrollment. application for admission

Prior College Credit
All applicants to the Bachelor of Science in Organizational Leadership program must possess either an associate’s degree or a minimum of 60 credit hours from an accredited institution. Applicants who meet the eligibility requirements will be considered for admission.
Applicants with insufficient college credits may explore eligibility for applied Credit for Prior Learning (CPL) based on credentials, certificates, or relevant work experiences, up to the equivalent of 30 credit hours. The review committee will assess program eligibility for individuals falling below the 60-credit minimum, aligning with our commitment to recognizing diverse learning pathways.
