Leaders Unscripted Speaker Series
Real conversations. Real leaders. Real growth.
Leaders Unscripted
is a student-led speaker series that brings authentic leadership stories to the heart of the Neeley School. Each conversation goes beyond the highlight reel to explore real moments of failure, growth and pivotal choices – the experiences that shape how leaders show up in the world.
Built by students, for students,
Leaders Unscripted creates space for open dialogue, personal reflection and meaningful connection. Every event is designed and produced by a team of student ambassadors, who select the speaker, craft the format and guide the discussion from start to finish.
The goal is simple: help business students grow as values-based leaders who learn from one another, ask better questions and build the confidence to navigate what comes next.

Join us for an eye-opening opportunity to connect with an accomplished leader and hear the real story behind their success. Our team of student ambassadors moderates the discussion and creates an open space for meaningful dialogue and reflection. The event also includes food and casual networking, giving students the chance to continue the conversation, build authentic relationships, and walk away with practical insight they can apply to their own leadership journey.
Why Students Love It
Come Hear Stories
Leaders get real about the moments that shaped their careers – from failures and turning points to lessons learned the hard way.
Ask What Really Matters
Student moderators guide the conversation with candid, thoughtful questions that open the door to honest leadership insight.
Connect with Leaders
Each event ends with casual networking, giving students a chance to build relationships in a relaxed, come-as-you-are environment.
How It's Student-led
Student ambassadors drive the experience from beginning to end. They:
Choose themes and invite speakers.
Host and moderate conversations.
Coordinate logistics and the run-of-show.
Shape the atmosphere, from audience engagement to event flow.
If you’re a Neeley business student looking for hands-on experience and direct access to industry leaders, consider joining the team.
