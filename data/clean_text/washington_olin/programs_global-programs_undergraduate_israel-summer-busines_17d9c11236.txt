Israel Summer Business Academy Fees
I learned invaluable information on what makes entrepreneurship unique in Israel. In fact, the ISBA program has inspired me to pursue a career in venture capital.
—2015 ISBA Alum
The ISBA program fee is $7,230; it includes tuition, housing, insurance, ground transportation, excursion costs and some meals. You’re responsible for your airfare, food and additional expenses such as elective travel. Scholarships are available.
All admitted applicants must pay a short-term program administrative fee of $1,000. All admitted non-WashU students must pay an additional nonrefundable administrative fee of $500.
