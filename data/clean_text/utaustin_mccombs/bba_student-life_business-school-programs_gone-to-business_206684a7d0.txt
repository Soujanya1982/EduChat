Gone to Business
Gone to Business is our annual new student welcome hosted by the BBA Program Office.
Recap: Gone to Business 2024
Gone to Business 2024 introduced incoming students to our vibrant McCombs community. They had the opportunity to meet Dean Mills, engage with McCombs student organizations, learn about the variety of business majors we offer, and interact with professionals from event sponsor H-E-B. We can't wait to meet the newest wave of incoming McCombs students at Gone to Business 2025!
