Why JURS?
Advances Interdisciplinary Research
Provides a JSOM platform for interdisciplinary, interschool, and interuniversity business research that drives innovation and real‑world impact.
Builds Lasting Academic Networks
Creates long‑term bonds between faculty and undergraduate scholars through mentoring, referrals, recommendations, and collaborative research.
Develops Future Scholars and Innovators
Cultivates a pipeline for graduate study, entrepreneurial ventures, and faculty–student publications, including opportunities for non‑tenured faculty.
Consulting, investment banking and analyst positions require strong analytical and research capabilities. Often students who become successful in research realize that their career may be pursuing a PhD and joining the academic ranks of a prominent university.
