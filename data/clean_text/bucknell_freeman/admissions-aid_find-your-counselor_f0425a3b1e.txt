Find Your Counselor
Once you're ready to get in touch with Bucknell, your next step is to find your Admissions counselor. Regardless of where you live, there's a Bucknell counselor who specializes in the unique circumstances of your geographic area. We also have a counselor who works with international students.
Also worth noting: If you choose to apply to Bucknell, the counselor assigned to your area will be one of the people who will read your application.
Use the list below to contact your counselor.
Please note that the location of your high school determines who your counselor is — not the state you live in
. If there isn't a counselor assigned to your area, please contact us at
admissions@bucknell.edu
, and an admissions counselor will respond.
Please reach out. We'd love to hear from you!
