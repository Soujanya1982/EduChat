Peer Advising
The Peer Advisor Program is designed to help first-semester Fisher students become more acclimated not only to the Fisher campus but also to the university as a whole. Peer advisors assist business survey instructors in the classroom, providing first-year students with a peer's perspective and mentoring.
Our team consists of undergraduate business majors who are either in their third or fourth year of study at Ohio State. Students are encouraged to contact peer advisors with questions about scheduling, student organizations, time management, and study abroad opportunities. Students of any class standing can meet with a peer advisor during drop-in hours listed below.
