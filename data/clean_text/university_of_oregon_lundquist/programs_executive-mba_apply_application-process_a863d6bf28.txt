Step-by-Step Application Checklist
To be considered for admission, you must submit the following:
Online Application
Get Started
Current Resume
Organizational Chart
or Job Description
Two Short Essays
Essay 1
:
Considering your career, education, and personal goals, why is the Oregon Executive MBA the right fit for you and why is now the right time to start?
(400-600 words)
Essay 2
:
Working effectively on teams is essential for success in the Oregon Executive MBA. Describe an experience working through conflict on a team. What did you learn?
(400-600 words)
Two Professional References
Recommenders should be individuals who know you professionally, e.g., a supervisor, colleague, or mentor. (Letters are submitted via an online system.)
Unofficial Transcripts
Submit
unofficial
transcripts from all colleges/universities you have attended.
Official transcripts will be required upon admission.
TOEFL or DET Scores
(if applicable)
Required for applicants whose native language is not English. Applicants may use the TOEFL or Duolingo English Tests to provide proof of language proficiency.
