What is the Business Study Cooperative?
The BSC is an inclusive and supportive learning environment for students who need help with the mathematical tools and methods used in the core business courses.
Who is it for?
Anyone with questions about course material.
Students looking to form a study group.
How to use the BSC
No registration is required for group tutoring—drop in and stay as long as you need.
For one-on-one tutoring between 4:00–6:00 p.m.,
visit our Starfish Service Profile
to see our availability and register.
To sign up for one-on-one tutoring outside of our regular 4:00-6:00 p.m. hours,
schedule on Starfish
.
