Often, we have this HUGE focus on first year students without realizing that a large majority of our students are not traditional freshmen. This event will be on
Thursday, September 7
from
9am-4pm
in the Office of Undergraduate Programs (2150 Nelson) as well as the Undergraduate Career Suite (1333 Nelson).
