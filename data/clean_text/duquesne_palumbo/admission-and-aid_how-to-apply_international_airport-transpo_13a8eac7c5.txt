TITLE: Airport Transportation & Hotels

There are many convenient transportation options to reach Duquesne University from
the Pittsburgh International Airport. It takes approximately 20-30 minutes to arrive
to campus from the airport.
Pittsburgh Airport and the surrounding area are very safe. We can assure you that
all of our transportation options are reliable and safe, even if you arrive at night.
Pittsburgh is also relatively small compared to many international airports. You will
feel at ease as there is only terminal and it difficult to get lost. More information
about
Pittsburgh International Airport
