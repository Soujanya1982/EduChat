Take a Virtual Campus Tour
UT Virtual Tours offer a guided tour of the historic University of Texas at Austin campus. You'll see and learn the history and purpose of iconic buildings, beautiful landscapes, and hear plenty of the stories that make UT such an interesting place to be.
Register now
Attend a McCombs Virtual Info Session
At these sessions you'll learn about admissions, resources and experiences available to our business students such as career management, study abroad, student life and more. You can also register for a Business Honors Program session for more information about their admissions process and offerings.
Sign up now
In-Person Information Sessions
We hope you will join us for one of our upcoming in-person sessions. Please select a highlighted date on the calendar to see a list of available sessions. Note that all times are Central Standard Time. If no dates are highlighted on the calendar, no sessions are currently available. We encourage you to attend a general admissions session to learn about your major of interest.
