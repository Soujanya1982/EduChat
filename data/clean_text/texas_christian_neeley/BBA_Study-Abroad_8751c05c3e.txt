Study Abroad
Your future employers are looking for cultural understanding and a global outlook. TCU study abroad opportunities offer you firsthand insight into how the business world is intricately linked, while encouraging your curiosity and openness to cultural differences and similarities.
Our faculty lead a variety of business courses during short international trips to a variety of locations around the world.
Spring Break
Classes typically meet one day per week during an eight-week session (Spring 8 Week 1), then travel to a country during spring break. After final coursework is submitted, classes end. Tuition for spring break study abroad trips is included in your TCU spring semester tuition. Additional trip fees apply. BUSI 30383 will be offered in Madrid, Spain and Buenos Aires, Argentina. MARK 40213 will be offered in Madrid, Spain.

Summer 2026
Neeley is offering Neeley Core Abroad located in Prague, Czech Republic! Courses will be taught by TCU faculty and offered over six weeks starting in May. Students must sign up for two of the following courses: BUSI 30383 - Global Business with Ethical Lens, MARK 30153 - Marketing Management, INSC 30313 - Supply Chain Management, or INSC 20263 - Business Informtion Systems.
