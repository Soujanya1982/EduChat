Enrollment in the Broad College is limited, and admission to the Broad College is competitive.
This information applies to students seeking a degree in the following majors: accounting, finance, human resource management, management, marketing, and supply chain management;
hospitality business has separate requirements
.
There are three pathways to admission to the Broad College.
Admission Pathways
High school students who select a Broad major on the MSU first-year application will be considered for direct admission.
View Pathway
Current MSU students can apply to the Broad College through the standard admission process. Applicants must fulfill specific academic and non-academic requirements as part of the application.
View Pathway
Students at other colleges/universities who select a Broad major on the MSU Transfer application will be considered for admission. Students may be admitted directly to the Broad College if they meet specific academic requirements; if not, they can apply to Broad after matriculating to MSU through the Standard Admission process.
