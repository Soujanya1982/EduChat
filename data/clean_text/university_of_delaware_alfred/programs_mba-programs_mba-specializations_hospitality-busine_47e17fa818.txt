MBA Hospitality Business Management
The worldwide hospitality industry provides you the opportunity to make the meaningful events in clients’ lives more memorable. You’ll be prepared for a successful career in any facet of the hospitality industry.
Format
The Lerner MBA with specialization in hospitality business management is an on-campus program. You can also take several courses online, giving you more flexibility and allowing you to complete your degree faster.
