Transportation and Parking
For those who will be flying into New Jersey, Newark Liberty International Airport (EWR) is conveniently located within 5 miles of the Robert Treat Hotel.
Complimentary hotel transportation to and from the airport is available at the P4 airport station. Once you arrive at the airport, take the airport Airtrain to the P4 station. The hotel van runs every 30 minutes.
Additionally, the Robert Treat is just 2 blocks away (within walking distance) from the Rutgers Business School.
If you plan to drive, please let us know and we will provide you with parking pass at the IDT Corporate Parking garage, 20-32 Atlantic Street, Newark, New Jersey 07102. The parking garage is also within walking distance Rutgers Business School. As you come out of the lot walk back to Bridge St. and turn left heading back to Broad St.
Cross over Broad and you will see the new Rutgers Business School in front of you—tall brick colored building, with flashing Rutgers signs, impossible to miss. The main entrance has a silver awning and has glass doors.
