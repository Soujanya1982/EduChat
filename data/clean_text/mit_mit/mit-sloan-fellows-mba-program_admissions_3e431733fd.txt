Successful candidates are:
Motivated to lead in their organization or start a new venture
Exceptional leaders who collaborate and inspire others
Ready to push boundaries and tackle challenges others might think too difficult to solve
Globally oriented and excited to master a rigorous curriculum
Committed to a year of self-reflection and growth
Effective communicators in English, both orally and in writing
Undergraduate degree recipients with at least 10 years of relevant full-time work experience
