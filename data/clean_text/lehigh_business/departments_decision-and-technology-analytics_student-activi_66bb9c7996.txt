DATA Student Activities
Business Information Systems Club
(previously AITP), Lehigh Student Chapter provides BIS students with opportunities to meet and network with local IT professionals to learn more about current IS topics and trends. The AITP student chapter also host activities such as invited talks, field trips, and mock interviews. Students meet informally with each other and their professors, exchanging ideas on current IS issues and seeking advice on careers, job hunting, IS future, and many other topics.  For more information, contact Professor Nayar.
The Lehigh Supply Chain Management Club
brings in speakers on SCM topics and provides opportunities for SCM students to network with each other and with SCM professionals.  For more information,
contact Professor Zacharia
