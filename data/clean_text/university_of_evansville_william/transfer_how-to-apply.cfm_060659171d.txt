TITLE: Next Steps - Transfer Admission - University of Evansville

Next Steps
Apply for admission.
Our application is free and available on our
website
or via the
Common App
. View our
Important Dates
to ensure you submit yours on time. Your application for admission will also serve as your application for
scholarships
.
Submit official transcripts.
You will be required to submit
official transcripts
from all colleges attended. These must be sent directly to UE by email or mail from your institutions. “Issued to student” copies are not considered official.
Email:
admission@evansville.edu
(through a transcript service, like
Parchment
or
National Student Clearinghouse
)
Mail:
UE Office of Admission, 1800 Lincoln Avenue, Evansville, IN 47722
After completing these two important tasks, you can follow our
next steps
toward becoming an Ace, like filing the FAFSA, visiting campus, and connecting with your admission counselor.
