TITLE: International Business Major - McCombs School of Business

Make Moves, Major in International Business
With the global economy in flux, the importance of global skills and knowledge has never been greater. The International Business major addresses the  goals of students considering international careers by offering two ways to focus: on language and culture or on hard business skills. With additional support from the Center for Global Business, including mentorships and advising, IB majors are ready for the world.

See What You'll Learn
International Business students learn from leading faculty and gain in-demand skills. This major offers you an integrated knowledge of how business changes as it crosses international borders, practical coursework, international experience (study abroad or internship) to acquire skills sought by employers, and opportunities to customize coursework to your personal interests and goals.
See the Jobs You Can Get
International Business students excel across industries and often pursue careers such as Consultants, Attorneys, Financial/Business Analysts, Communications Specialists, Strategic Developers, Real Estate Agents, and Business Developers.
