Financial Aid Deadlines
Eligible students who file the
Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA)
will qualify for an annual
$2,000 FAFSA Grant
*. The FAFSA Grant is renewable if the FAFSA is submitted annually with Susquehanna’s school code: 003369.
For Students Starting Classes in Fall 2026
Oct. 1, 2025
— 2026–27 FAFSA opens.
March 15, 2026
— Priority deadline for
first-year students
filing the FAFSA.
May 1, 2026
— Deadline for Pennsylvania students (
first-year and transfer
) to complete the FAFSA and be considered for the
PA State Grant
.
June 1, 2026
— Priority deadline for
transfer students
filing the FAFSA.
For Students Starting Classes in Spring 2027
Dec. 15, 2026
— Priority deadline to file the 2026–27 FAFSA.
*Students who are awarded Tuition Exchange or Tuition Remission are not eligible for the FAFSA Grant.
