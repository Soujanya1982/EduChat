GSAS Standardized Test Scores for Admissions
GRE and English Language Proficiency Exams
Please note, the GRE requirements for future application years are subject to change.
Applicants whose native language is not English must meet the requirements and are required to submit scores from the
TOEFL
,
IELTS,
Duolingo English Test
(DET),
PTE Academic
, or
Cambridge English Qualifications
unless they qualify for an exemption.
Fordham’s GRE-ETS school code is
2259
. No department code is required. GRE scores are valid for five years from the testing date. Any questions about your GRE scores should be directed to
ETS
.
ETS is currently offering a version of the GRE that can be taken at home called the GRE
®
General Test at Home. For more information, please visit the
ETS GRE
web page.
Applicants should have official test scores sent by the testing service no later than three weeks before the deadline to ensure timely receipt by published
application deadlines
.
