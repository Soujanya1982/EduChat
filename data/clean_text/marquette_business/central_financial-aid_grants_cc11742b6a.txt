Grants
Grants, like scholarships, do not have to be repaid. Most of the grants listed below are gift assistance awarded based on
federal need
. Every student's application is reviewed for grant eligibility. One or more of the grants listed may be included in a financial aid package.
Some grants may
only be available
to undergraduate students.
