TITLE: Accelerated Engineering + MBA Program | RPI Lally School of Management

A Fast, Flexible Path from Engineering Discovery to Business Leadership
Rensselaer’s Accelerated Engineering + MBA program allows students to earn a Bachelor of Science in a School of Engineering discipline and a Master of Business Administration (MBA) in just five years or less. This innovative pathway is designed for high-achieving undergraduates who want to pair scientific or technical expertise with top-tier business, leadership, and management skills.
You’ll build a competitive edge through early exposure to business coursework, hands-on experiential learning, and mentorship from expert faculty and alumni—all while saving time and tuition.
Program Overview & Timeline
What makes this dual-degree program unique?
This is a streamlined, co-terminal pathway that starts as early as your freshman year or as late as your senior year, depending on your schedule. Students complete two MBA courses as undergraduates and finish the remainder in two graduate semesters or fewer.
At a glance:
Years 1–3: Complete your engineering major and explore business seminars.
Year 4 (Senior Year): Take 2 MBA courses—one in the fall and one in the spring. With RPI’s credit-sharing policy, these count as undergraduate free electives.
Year 5 (Graduate Year): Complete 5 MBA courses per semester to finish the 36-credit MBA, which is focused on technology, leadership, strategy, and innovation.
Finish with 2 degrees in about 5 years: a B.S. from the School of Engineering and an MBA from the Lally School of Management.
Conditional on academic performance, students may qualify for a significant merit-based tuition award during their MBA year.

Admissions & Application
Who is this program for?
RPI undergraduates in engineering who want to lead in biotech, life sciences, AI, software, sustainability, data analytics, and more.
When can I start?
You can join the pre-program as early as the end of your freshman year and officially apply to the MBA in your junior year.
Requirements:
Enrollment in the School of Engineering
Cumulative GPA of 3.0 or higher
Completion of 15 credits at the 4000 level before applying
Attendance at pre-MBA seminars
Application materials: essays, transcripts, and two letters of recommendation
FAQs
Can I really complete both degrees in five years or less?
Yes. Most students complete the B.S. + MBA within
five years
by taking two MBA courses senior year and finishing the rest in the fifth year.
Which engineering majors are eligible?
All majors in the
School of Engineering
are eligible—including biomedical engineering, chemical and biological engineering, civil and environmental engineering, industrial and systems engineering, materials science and engineering, and more.
What financial aid is available?
Qualified students may receive a
merit-based tuition award
during their MBA semesters. This helps reduce the cost of an additional degree.
