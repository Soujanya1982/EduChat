File Your FAFSA Early
The Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) is the first step toward unlocking need-based financial aid, including grants, loans, and work study opportunities. The sooner you file, the better your chances for priority aid.
CNU's FAFSA code is 003706.

In-State Tuition and Fees
If you're a Virginia resident, you’ll receive a significant cost advantage as part of our commitment to keeping high-quality education accessible. CNU’s in-state tuition and fees offer incredible value for the personalized, hands-on college experience you’ll receive. Estimated annual costs include tuition, housing, meals, and required fees, but remember, most students don’t pay full price thanks to financial aid and scholarships. Take the next step—see what your investment could look like.
View In-State Tuition and Fees
Out-of-State Tuition and Fees
At Christopher Newport, out-of-state students are welcomed with open arms and outstanding value. Even if you’re coming from beyond Virginia, you’ll benefit from small class sizes, renowned faculty, and a beautiful coastal campus that’s worth the investment. Tuition and fees are estimated based on a full academic year and include room and board, but don’t let the sticker price scare you. Our financial aid team is ready to help you bring your cost down. Explore how affordable a private-feel, public education can be.
