Dual Admit Financial Package Ranges
Undergraduate Scholarships & Financial Aid
Applicants who either audition or submit a portfolio for a major in art, dance, film (BFA), music or theatre are considered for artistic scholarship. To see how artistic scholarship and other grants and scholarships from SMU and Meadows were allocated to dually admitted applicants in each area of study, select the appropriate Meadows major below.
