Student Wellness
Wellness Wednesdays
At the McDonough School of Business, student well-being is a top priority. Through a combination of weekly wellness events and university-wide resources, we provide students with opportunities to recharge, reflect, and connect — supporting both academic success and personal growth.
Bagels With the Deans
Time:
Thursdays, 10 – 11 a.m. (during the academic year)
Location:
Shea Commons
Each Thursday, the McDonough Undergraduate Programs Office hosts
Bagels With the Deans
, an informal gathering that brings together students, staff, and leadership. Enjoy complimentary bagels and coffee while connecting with deans and peers in a relaxed setting. It’s a great way to build community and stay engaged with school leadership.
Additional Resources
Additional Wellness Resources
Georgetown University offers a range of wellness services and tools to support students throughout their academic journey, including:
Mental health counseling and support
Academic coaching and tutoring
Guided meditation and mindfulness sessions
Workshops on stress management, resilience, and self-care
These resources are available through
Student Health Services
and other campus partners to ensure every student has access to the care and support they need to thrive on the Hilltop.
