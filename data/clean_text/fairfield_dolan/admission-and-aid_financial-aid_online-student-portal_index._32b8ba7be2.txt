Online Student Portal
Online Financial Aid System
Welcome to Fairfield University's online financial aid system.
Admitted students enrolled for Fall 2023 and beyond can easily access all of their financial aid and scholarship information using this convenient online student portal.
Once logged into the system, you will have the ability to:
View a list of submitted and outstanding financial aid documents
View, accept, or decline your financial aid awards
View your student loan history (if you have borrowed in the past)
Check the status of your student loans for the current year
View important messages from the Office of Financial Aid
Review and accept financial aid terms and conditions
New Student First-Time User
In order to use the Online Financial Aid System you must have a valid Fairfield University ID number and an active financial aid record.
Your ID number can be located in your admission portal or found on the paper award notification sent to you.
You must have applied for financial aid or been awarded a scholarship at the time of admission to Fairfield University to have an active financial aid record.
