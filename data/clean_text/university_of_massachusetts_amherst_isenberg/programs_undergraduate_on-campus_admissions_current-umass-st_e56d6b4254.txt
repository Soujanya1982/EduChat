TITLE: Undergraduate Application Information for Current UMass Students : Isenberg School of Management : UMass Amherst

The Isenberg School of Management encourages current UMass Amherst students to learn more about the academic offerings within our school.
All
UMass Amherst
students
are eligible to apply to the Bachelor of Science (BS) degree in Hospitality & Tourism Management or Sport Management
. Students beginning their academic career at UMass Amherst
in fall
2022 or later are not eligible to apply to the Bachelor of Business Administration degree program unless they have been admitted to Hospitality & Tourism Management or Sport Management upon their admission to the university.
Isenberg offers two degree programs: Bachelor of Science (BS) degree in Hospitality & Tourism Management or Sport Management, and a Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) degree in the following majors: Accounting, Finance, Management, Marketing, Operations & Information Management.
