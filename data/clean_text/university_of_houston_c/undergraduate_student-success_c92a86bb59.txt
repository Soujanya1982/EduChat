Welcome to an exciting journey at the C. T. Bauer College of Business! As an undergraduate business student, you have access to a wealth of opportunities designed to help you thrive both academically and personally. The
HUB for Student Success
is your go-to resource, providing support and guidance as you navigate your college experience. Our first-year experience programs, such as ELASP for Freshmen and ELASP for Transfers, will help you build a strong foundation, connect with peers and mentors, and access valuable resources. There are also opportunities to get involved in leadership programs and events, such as the Ted Bauer Leadership Certificate Program, to develop your leadership skills, expand your network, and prepare for future success. With a wide variety of Bauer Student Organizations, you can find your community, explore your passions, and gain hands-on experience in business and beyond. No matter what your interests are, the HUB for Student Success is here to support you as you grow, lead, and make an impact! Navigate over on the left to find which opportunity excites you.
