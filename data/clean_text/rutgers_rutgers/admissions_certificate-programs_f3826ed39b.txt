Certificate Programs
Rutgers Business School offers alternative paths to your education through shorter certificate programs that allow you to learn targeted information or become certified in a specific area.
Our academic programs pull from current program courses to offer you a concentrated learning experience and degree credits that are often transferable into our matriculated degree programs. The research center certificate programs offer you training outside of our offered academic courses that range from small business certifications to training in ethics and leadership.
