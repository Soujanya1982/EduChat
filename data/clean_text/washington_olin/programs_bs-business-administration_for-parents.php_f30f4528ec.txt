Support Going Forward
For Parents
Consider these opportunities to engage with the WashU community.
Your son or daughter is about to enter a new phase of life—and so are you, especially if this is your first or only child leaving home for school. You want to do everything possible to ensure your student flourishes in the new environment and, at the same time, builds independence and self-reliance.
The BSBA team understands this time of transition. We're always here to direct you and your student to helpful information and support. In addition, you and your entire family are encouraged to engage with the Olin community. The business school sponsors activities during Spotlight Weekend, orientation and
Parent and Family Weekend
.
Get Involved
Volunteer for university organizations and events.
If you haven’t done so already, spend a few minutes on the
families website
. It introduces you to Parent & Family Engagement services, programs, and events for WashU parents and family members.
You may be interested in joining the
Alumni and Parents Admission Program
, a group that helps the university recruit and enroll talented students and connects these parents around the world.
You also can host receptions for prospective or current students and their families and contact new students’ parents to welcome them to the university community.
