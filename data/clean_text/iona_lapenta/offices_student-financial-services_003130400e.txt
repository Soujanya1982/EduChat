Student Financial Services
Welcome to Student Financial Services. Our team is here to guide you through all financial aspects of an outstanding education at Iona University.
We’ll help you through the process of applying for financial aid. We'll find scholarship opportunities and other financial assistance for you. And we'll provide you with a financial savvy that will help you during your time at Iona and beyond. We can't wait to work with you!
