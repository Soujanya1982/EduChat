**For students considering an MEng in Course 6 and wanting to complete their MEng in one year, you will want to plan your course road with two “extra” courses that you can apply toward your MEng. If you are planning on getting
funding through an RA-ship/TA-ship
for your MEng, you are restricted to two courses per MEng semester. Having two “extra” courses to apply toward your MEng will allow the possibility of graduating in a year (an MEng is typically six courses plus thesis).
These “extra” courses cannot be double-counted
, i.e., used towards fulfilling your undergraduate Course 6 requirements, your undergraduate Course 15 requirements, GIRs, or 180 units beyond GIRs. Please see the
Course 6 website
for a list of eligible courses and confirm with Course 6 in advance and prior to taking a specific course if you would like that course to count toward your MEng.
