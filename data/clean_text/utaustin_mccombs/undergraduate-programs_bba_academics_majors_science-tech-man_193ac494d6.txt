TITLE: Science & Tech Management Major - McCombs School of Business

Make Moves, Major in STM
The Science and Technology Management major focuses on taking new innovations from the idea stage to the marketplace. It is one of the only undergraduate programs of its kind and is best suited to students who are tech-savvy and excellent communicators. STM students learn how to manage effectively in highly technical business environments. They take business classes at McCombs and engineering classes at the Cockrell School of Engineering. Please note, starting Fall 2022, this major will no longer be available for BBA students.
Read the degree plan
SEE WHAT YOU’LL LEARN
STM students learn from leading faculty and gain in-demand skills, including: every step in the process of bringing a product to market, how to identify a promising new technology, management and marketing skills, and important communication and problem-solving techniques.
SEE THE JOBS YOU CAN GET
Science & Tech MGMT students excel across industries and often pursue these careers: Consultant, Financial Analyst, Information System Analyst, Production and Logistics Manager, and Technical Sales and Marketing.
