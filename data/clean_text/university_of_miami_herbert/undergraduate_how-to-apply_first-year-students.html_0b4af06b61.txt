First-Year Students
We know you worked hard during high school to achieve outstanding academic results. However, we recommend that you consider completing a pre-calculus and (if possible) calculus course as part of your high school math requirement. Obtaining strong skills in math are essential for success in our business curriculum.
If you and your family would like more details about Miami Herbert and our curriculum, we offer weekday information sessions to support your college search process. Connect with us in our Virtual Reception
Room to schedule a session or ask a general question about the business program.
