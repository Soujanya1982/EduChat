Discover Where Opportunity Meets Possibility
Feel the energy and excitement of Susquehanna University in person. Explore how our campus, programs and community can ignite your path to success.
A campus visit is your first step into Susquehanna’s vibrant, welcoming community. You’ll see our stunning campus, experience state-of-the-art facilities and connect with people dedicated to helping you shape your future. Whether you’re just beginning your college search or finalizing your decision,
scheduling a visit
is easy.
Open new doors, Unlock Up To $8,000
campus Visit Grant Opportunity
Prospective first-year and transfer students who attend an official visit through the Admission Office will qualify for a $2,000 annual Visit Grant.
Current high school juniors, enrolling in the fall of 2027, will earn their grant by visiting between Jan. 5 and Nov. 21, 2026. That’s $8,000 over four years!
