Payment Plans
Susquehanna offers flexible, interest-free monthly payment plans to help families manage education costs. Spread all or part of your semester’s charges — tuition, fees, housing and dining — over three to six automatic installments, with no credit check or loan application required.
Unless covered by your payment plan, fall 2026 total direct charges are due in full by August 3, while spring 2027 charges are due by January 8, 2027.
How It Works
Plan Setup
: Enroll online to select a payment plan that fits your budget. Re-enrollment is required each semester.
Payment Setup
: Provide a checking/savings account or a credit/debit card number for automatic payments.
Fees
: Our payment vendor charges the following transaction fees:
$50 nonrefundable enrollment fee per semester
Service fee of 2.85% for credit and debit card payments
$30 nonrefundable returned payment fee
Vendor fees are subject to change. Susquehanna does not determine or receive any portion of vendor fees.
How to Enroll
Log into your Student Account Center Portal via
MyNest
under the My Aid & Student Account tile. For parents or guardians to help set up a payment plan, students must grant them access.
For guidance, watch the “
How to Set Up a Payment Plan
” video. To calculate your total amount due for fall and spring before the week of July 6, 2026, for fall and the week of December 7, 2026, for spring, visit the
Financial Plan webpage
.
