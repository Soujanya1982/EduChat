Make Moves, Major in Accounting
Join one of U.S. News & World Report's top-ranked undergraduate accounting programs! Accounting students are prepared for a wide array of professional opportunities, and are a perfect fit for positions in a variety of businesses and not-for-profit organizations. Accounting is a degree with 360 degrees of possibilities: every field from the arts to charity to sports to international business needs the insight of an accountant.

Accounting students learn from leading faculty and gain in-demand skills, including: how to prepare and examine financial records, financial analysis techniques, profound knowledge of business transactions, how to present financial statements to assist in decision making, how to ensure that financial statements comply with laws/regulations, and tax return preparation.
