Begin Your CNU Experience
There’s nothing like seeing Christopher Newport University in person. From the moment you set foot on our stunning campus in the heart of Newport News, Virginia, you’ll feel the energy and excitement that sets us apart. CNU is a vibrant, welcoming community surrounded by natural beauty, rich history, and endless opportunities for fun. Just minutes from the James River and a short drive from Virginia Beach, Richmond, and Washington, D.C., we’re perfectly positioned for exploration and connection. Whether you’re here for a campus tour, a performance, or just to get a feel for life as a Captain, we can’t wait to welcome you.

Welcome to Coastal Virginia
Newport News is more than just our home, it’s part of the CNU experience. Our coastal city offers a dynamic mix of local charm and big-city access, with parks, museums, shopping, and entertainment options.
Explore the Virginia Living Museum or hike the trails of Newport News Park, which is nine times the size of Central Park. Catch a concert or Broadway performance at our own Ferguson Center for the Performing Arts or enjoy waterfront dining along the James River.
With major employers like Huntington Ingalls Industries, NASA Langley, Ferguson Enterprises, Jefferson Lab, and Canon Virginia, Newport News is a hub for innovation and career opportunities. Our students gain hands-on experience alongside industry leaders, from global powerhouses to trailblazing startups, giving them unparalleled access to real-world insights, innovation, and professional networks.
