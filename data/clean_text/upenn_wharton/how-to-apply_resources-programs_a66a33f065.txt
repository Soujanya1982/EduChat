TITLE: Resources & Programs | Penn Admissions

At Penn, we believe it is our collective responsibility to make higher education more accessible for all. So, we’ve created resources and developed programs to support your journey to college—no matter where you ultimately apply. These tools are designed to help guide you step-by-step through the college application process and strengthen your applications to any school on your list.
If you’re interested in Penn, you may find these options particularly helpful as you prepare to apply; however, none of these are required for a successful application to Penn.
These free resources are here to help you and we encourage you to explore what works best for you!
