TITLE: Exploring Business & Leadership | Drexel LeBow

LeBow Launch is a two-day introductory leadership program designed to provide incoming LeBow College of Business first-year students with a head start on college. This includes an early move-in component allowing students to explore key business concepts and sharpen leadership skills — all in preparation to tackle the course work ahead.
Skills essential to success in business include strong leadership and analytical skills combined with the ability to communicate effectively. Students will participate in case studies and team-building activities, all while building community and exploring their new home away from home.
This event happens annually in early September.
