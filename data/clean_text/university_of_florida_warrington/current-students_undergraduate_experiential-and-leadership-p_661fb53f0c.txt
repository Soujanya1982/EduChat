TITLE: Experiential and Leadership Programs - UF Warrington College of Business

Experiential and Leadership Programs
Building the student journey
A cornerstone of the Heavener School of Business mission is to create profound pathways of self-discovery and skill development to make students career-ready upon graduation.
The school’s experiential learning programs support your personal and professional development and enhance your undergraduate experiences and beyond. Programs are specifically tailored to address opportunities throughout your undergraduate student journey.
Experiential learning programs
Business Ethics Ambassadors
True business leadership requires a code of ethics. Business ethics ambassadors are highly motivated students committed to bringing ethics to the forefront of entrepreneurial engagement.
Business Undergraduate Membership Program
Incoming students are provided guidance by an experienced student who is currently in their junior or senior year.
Warrington Welcome
This one-credit transition course for first-year students is designed to promote a sense of community at the college and assist you with various academic and personal goals.
Career and Peer Mentors
An internship is a first step. Career and peer mentors provide professional career development to acquire internships. These mentors are Heavener’s most successful third and fourth year students.
Florida Leadership Academy
This year-long leadership program develops future leaders and polished professionals by providing opportunities for personal growth and networking. Students will explore emotionally intelligent leadership principles, practice professionalism, serve the community, and put their skills to the test in a business case competition.
Heavener Leadership Challenge
A semester-long program that empowers you to develop into an exemplary leader, it is framed by the principles from the best-selling book “The Student Leadership Challenge.” Students engage in weekly action-based scenarios to experience leadership practices in real time.
Heavener Second-Year Experience
Through a single-day retreat experience, second-year business students have the exclusive opportunity to find their work style and career potential. Our retreat mobilizes a team of students to execute their career plan while constructing an authentic professional identity in the Warrington College of Business.
Leadership Development Program
This year-long business program for undergraduate students is structured after The Generative Partnership® Model of Leadership. Students actively apply skills and knowledge, ensuring a practical and hands-on experience that reinforces their leadership development.
Warrington Diplomats
The Warrington Diplomats are the official student ambassadors of the Warrington College of Business. This organization is comprised of highly committed and motivated business students selected through a competitive application process.
Get your foot in the door
Through Heavener’s experiential learning programs, you can develop your career-readiness skills. Reach out if you need assistance navigating the opportunities.
