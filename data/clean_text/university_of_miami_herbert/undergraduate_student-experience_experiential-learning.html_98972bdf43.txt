Experiential learning is a great way to partner with the university in their educational mission while also solving real business problems at your organization. Through capstone projects and consulting projects in different classes, teams of undergraduate or graduate students under the supervision of our world-class faculty or practitioner coaches can bring a fresh perspective to the challenges your company is facing.
How can you leverage AI to improve your services and operations? How can you reach a new demographic or geographical location with your message or product? Are your systems and processes truly optimized compared to your competition? How can you improve your customer experience? These types of questions and more can be addressed through class projects.
Do you have a challenge where there are many possible paths to take? Consider sponsoring a case competition where several teams of students compete to define the best solution to meet your goals.
Interested in learning more? We can help to develop a customized program that fits both the business needs of your company and the learning needs of our students.
