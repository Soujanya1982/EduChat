Job and Internship Postings
The Business Career Center can provide multiple avenues to post internship and employment opportunities for your company.  In cooperation with the MU Career Center, we are able to provide employers the ability to access
Handshake
, a campus-wide job and internship posting website.  Handshake also allows employers, schedule on-campus interviews, access student and alumni resumes, and register for career fairs and events.
Business Career Center staff
are happy to assist you in establishing an account and to answer any of your questions.
