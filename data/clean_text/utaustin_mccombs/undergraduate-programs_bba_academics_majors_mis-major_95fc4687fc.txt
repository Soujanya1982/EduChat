TITLE: MIS Major - McCombs School of Business

Make Moves, Major in MIS
The Management Information Systems (MIS) major focuses on technology’s role in all aspects of business. If you are comfortable in fast-paced, tech-heavy environment, you may be particularly well-suited to the growing fields MIS will prepare you for. McCombs' MIS major is one of the top-ranked programs in the country by U.S. News & World Report.
See the degree plan
See What You'll Learn
MIS students learn from leading faculty and gain in-demand skills, including how to use information technology to solve business problems, fundamental and advanced aspects of business and technical systems analysis, new applications for cutting-edge technologies, and problem-solving and critical thinking skills.
See the Jobs You Can Get
MIS students excel across industries and often pursue careers such as Business Analyst, Consultant, IT Analyst, IT Developer, Software Developer, System Analyst/Engineer, and Technology Risk Consultant.
