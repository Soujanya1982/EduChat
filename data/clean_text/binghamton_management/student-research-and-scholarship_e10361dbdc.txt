External Scholarships and Undergraduate Research Center
External Scholarships and Undergraduate Research Center
The External Scholarships and Undergraduate Research Center develops, recognizes,
and awards undergraduate research; promotes and assists students with locating and
applying for external scholarships; and provides outreach and advising to undergraduate
students, faculty, and the broader campus community. These objectives lay the foundations
for providing a transformative learning community that prepares students for advanced
education, careers, and purposeful living.
Source Project
First year students have a unique opportunity to enroll in a sequence of two research
seminars in the humanities or social sciences to develop their ability to discover,
analyze and communicate new knowledge.
Undergraduate Research
Work with faculty on projects designed by them or by you and earn credit or receive
a stipend. Find opportunities to present and publish your research. Receive funding
for conference presentations and for your research expenses. Learn about opportunity
programs on campus that may be a good match for you. Meet with us to learn more about
ways to become involved with research and creative activities on campus.
External Scholarships
We have walk-in and appointment hours available every day to meet individually with
graduate or undergraduate students who are interested in applying for competitive,
merit-based internal or external scholarships, fellowships, awards.
