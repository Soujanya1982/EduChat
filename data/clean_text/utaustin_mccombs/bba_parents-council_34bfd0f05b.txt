McCombs Parent Council
The McCombs Parent Council serves as a platform for parents of enrolled business students to be involved with McCombs and to support BBA Program Office programs and initiatives.
In Partnership with the BBA Program Office
The BBA Program Office is the central office for all McCombs undergraduate programming including, but not limited to, admissions, recruitment, student-led organizations, study abroad, leadership opportunities, and commencement. The BBA Program Office’s talented and dedicated staff works year-round to ensure that McCombs undergraduate students have access to the best programming in and out of the classroom. They also work with the Development and External Relations team to secure consistent funding that keeps our programs affordable and accessible to every student. This office is highly important for Parents Council involvement and support because it houses the programs that will have a direct impact on your students during their tenure at the McCombs School of Business.
