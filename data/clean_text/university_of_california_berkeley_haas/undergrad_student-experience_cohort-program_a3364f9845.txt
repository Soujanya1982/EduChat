TITLE: Cohort Program - Undergraduate Program - UC Berkeley Haas

Undergraduate Cohorts and Leaders
The Cohorts within Haas are designed to enhance community spirit and foster a supportive, united environment for students. Upon entering the Haas Undergraduate Program, students are placed into cohorts, providing them with a smaller group of classmates to navigate their major experience with exciting activities focused on socializing, community involvement, and various engagement opportunities are at the core of the cohort experience.
For 2024, the six Cohorts are:
Poppy
Iris
Manzanita
Sequoia
Pine
Oak
Cohorts will be assigned upon entering our Haas Base Camp
