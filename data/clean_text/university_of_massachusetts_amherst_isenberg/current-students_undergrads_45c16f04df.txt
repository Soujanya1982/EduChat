Undergraduate Scholarship Program
Isenberg Scholarships
We encourage all current/matriculated undergraduate Isenberg School students who intend to return to UMass Amherst for the following academic year to apply to the Isenberg Undergraduate Scholarship Program. The majority of these scholarships are generously funded by alumni, members of the Isenberg School community, and business friends. These scholarships are intended to recognize the hard work and academic success of our students, as well as assist them with the expense of their education.
