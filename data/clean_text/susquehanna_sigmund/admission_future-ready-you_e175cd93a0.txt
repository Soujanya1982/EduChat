Career Accelerator

Expert-Guided Experience
Expert-Guided Experience
Learning sticks when you use it. Every student completes at least one high impact experience — an internship, job, research project or study away opportunity — guided by faculty mentors who know you and your field. You won’t just learn concepts. You’ll prove you can apply them.
Learn by Doing
Global Engagement
Global Engagement
The world is your classroom. Every Susquehanna student completes a cross cultural experience, and nearly all choose to study abroad. You’ll expand your perspective, build cultural fluency and learn to navigate complexity with confidence.

Capstone Credibility
Your education culminates in a capstone experience that brings everything together. You’ll apply what you’ve learned to a meaningful project — research based, creative or professional — and graduate with a compelling story that demonstrates your readiness for what’s next.

When, Where and Why It Matters
When
: Four years or less — intentionally designed, flexible and personalized to your goals.
Where
: Susquehanna University — a closeknit campus where faculty mentorship and hands-on learning are the norm.
Why
: Distinctive value and proven outcomes. Graduates are prepared to earn more, adapt faster and do meaningful work from their first job on.
Measure the results
Student Outcomes That Matter
Your Susquehanna experience positions you to launch confidently — not just into a job, but into a career with momentum. Graduates consistently demonstrate strong employment outcomes, graduate school placement and long term return on investment.
