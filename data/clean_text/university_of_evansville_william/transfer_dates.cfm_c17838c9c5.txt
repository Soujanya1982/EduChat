FAFSA (Free Application for Federal Student Aid)
The 2025-26 FAFSA is available to submit for Spring 2026. The 2026-27 FAFSA becomes available in October 2025 for Fall 2026 and Spring 2027.
It’s always advisable to file your FAFSA by your state’s preferred deadline for full consideration of state grant funds.  For Indiana residents, the deadline is April 15.  UE’s FAFSA school code is 001795.
