Managing Your Student Account
Your student account is where you and your parents access your student account and your eBill, set up online payments, access your financial aid and more.
How to set up your student account
Visit myiona.iona.edu to set up or access your student account. After your account has been created, you will be able to add a parent or guardian as an authorized user of the account.

How to accept your financial aid awards
Once you've reviewed your financial aid package, log in to your student account to accept.
