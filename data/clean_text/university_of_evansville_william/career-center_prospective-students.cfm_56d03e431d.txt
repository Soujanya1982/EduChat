Prospective Students (Future Aces)
In today’s world, students and families are looking at a college for more than just a degree. They want a clear path to a successful future and the confidence to get there.
At the University of Evansville, the Center for Career Development understands that peace of mind comes from knowing your education will lead to real opportunities. That’s why our support begins long before you set foot on campus. As you start exploring UE, we’ll work with you to connect interests and strengths with academic and career options. This creates a foundation that you can then build upon as an Ace through active academics, extraordinary experiences, and a caring culture.
