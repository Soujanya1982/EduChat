TITLE: Scholarships for Direct Admit First-Year Students | Bachelor's Degree in Business | Indiana Kelley

If you are an in-state student, once you have been admitted to IU and offered direct admission to Kelley, you may be eligible for the
Kelley Scholars Program
. This scholarship program provides standard tuition, fees, and a generous living stipend for four years of study in addition to the opportunity to study overseas.
As a returning Kelley student, you may be eligible for private donor scholarships administered by the Kelley School. To ensure consideration for the next academic year, submit the
IU general scholarship application
between
September 1 and February 1
each spring. Be sure to reapply each year.
Qualified students may also gain consideration for scholarships by
completing an application
with the
IU Office of Scholarships
.
Socially conscious, in-state students may be eligible for the
Thomasson Fellows Program
. This scholarship supports students to participate in volunteer and service activities through the Kelley Institute for Social Impact throughout their four years at the Kelley School.
If you want to be considered for scholarships beyond your first year at Kelley, you must submit the
IU general scholarship application
between
September 1 and February 1
each spring. Be sure to reapply each year.
As an incoming student offered direct admission to Kelley, you must complete the
IU General Scholarship application
by
February 1
to be considered for the scholarships listed below.
Payment of the
IU enrollment deposit
is required by May 1 to accept any undergraduate business scholarship you have been offered by the Kelley School of Business. You must also complete all processes outlined in your notification letter.
