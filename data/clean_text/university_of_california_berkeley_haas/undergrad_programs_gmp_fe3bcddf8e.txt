TITLE: Global Management Program - UC Berkeley Haas

Learn the Fundamentals of Business Across the Globe
Are you ready to explore the world of global business? The Global Management Program (GMP) combines the expertise of Haas School of Business academics with engaging project-based travel opportunities to help you cultivate a global perspective.
We are thrilled to announce that starting Fall 2025, the program’s semester abroad location will be in vibrant Cape Town, at the University of Cape Town. Class of 2029 and beyond will start their academic journey alongside their peers in the Spieker Undergraduate Business Program. This means no more required first-year summers!
During your sophomore fall term abroad, GMP students will have the chance to study essential business classes and choose from hundreds of other courses available to exchange participants. The updated location offers enhanced opportunities for business visits and an immersive experience at the University of Cape Town where you will work with local students on consulting projects.
Students with an exploratory, open mindset and keen interest in international business thrive in the GMP. Join us to unlock new horizons and build lifelong connections!
