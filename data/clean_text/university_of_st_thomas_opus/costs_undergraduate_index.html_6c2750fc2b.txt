Cost of Attendance for Undergraduate Students
The following Financial Aid Cost of Attendance (COA) Budget Components are intended to provide estimated allowances of educationally related expenses.
Financial Aid
, including
scholarships
,
grants
and
loans
, may be available to help with these expenses.
These figures are estimates and your actual costs may differ. All figures below reflect information based on a 9-month period of enrollment (Fall and Spring Semester). Jterm and Summer term costs are additional.
