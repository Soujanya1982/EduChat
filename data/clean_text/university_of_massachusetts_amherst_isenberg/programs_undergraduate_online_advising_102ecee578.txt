Online BBA Student Advising Appointments
Undergraduate online degree completion students have an appointment link provided in every monthly email.
Undergraduate online degree completion students are welcome to schedule a phone or Zoom appointment with any questions and for academic advising.  With Zoom we can screen share to look over your Progress Report, Academic Requirement Report in SPIRE, search for classes, etc.
We are also generally available by e-mail Monday through Friday 8 a.m. - 4:30 p.m. EST.
