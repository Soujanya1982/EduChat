TITLE: Plan Your Visit : University of Dayton, Ohio

We'd Love to Meet You!
Wondering if the University of Dayton School of Law is the right fit for you? We strongly encourage prospective law students to meet our helpful admissions staff and engaging students by visiting Joseph E. Keller Hall. You will experience the positive, caring and supportive atmosphere awaiting you at the University of Dayton School of Law.
Students who visit (in person or virtual) by April 17 and enroll for 2026 will receive a
$1,000 Visit Scholarship
for their first-year at Dayton Law.
