GSAS Application Deadlines
2025 - 2026 Admissions
Now accepting spring and fall 2026 applications
The 2026 GSAS application is currently open and accepting applications for master's degree programs for Spring and Fall 2026.
We strongly encourage international applicants residing outside the U.S. to submit their complete applications by the earliest program deadline to allow sufficient time for the visa process.
