Business Analytics Major
In today’s data-driven world, the ability to transform raw data into actionable insights is a skill highly sought after by businesses across all industries. The Business Analytics major at Marquette University equips students with the knowledge and tools to analyze complex datasets, make informed decisions, and drive strategic business outcomes. This program bridges the gap between business acumen and technical expertise, preparing students to become key players in the dynamic field of business analytics.
Why Choose Business Analytics?

Our Business Analytics major is designed for students who are passionate about data and its potential to impact business strategies and operations. By integrating cutting-edge analytics techniques with real-world business applications, we provide a comprehensive education that covers everything from data mining and predictive analytics to machine learning and data visualization. Students will learn to leverage advanced analytics tools to solve business problems, optimize performance, and uncover new opportunities.
Program Highlights:
Hands-On Learning:
Engage in practical, hands-on projects that simulate real business scenarios. Work with actual datasets to develop your analytical skills and gain experience in applying them to solve business challenges.
Interdisciplinary Approach:
Benefit from a curriculum that combines business courses with advanced analytics. This interdisciplinary approach ensures that you gain a well-rounded understanding of both the business context and the technical skills required to analyze data effectively.
Industry Connections:
Take advantage of our strong industry partnerships and network with professionals in the field. Participate in internships, workshops, and seminars to gain insights from industry experts and enhance your career prospects.
Career Readiness:
Our program prepares you for a wide range of careers in business analytics, including roles such as data analyst, business intelligence analyst, data scientist, and more. You’ll be equipped with the skills needed to thrive in various industries, from finance and marketing to healthcare and technology.
Courses
The Business Analytics major
must
be taken as a second major.  Any other business major is acceptable. Students are encouraged to complement the Business Analytics major with quantitative courses from their other major.  Due to the prerequisites attached to the courses that fulfill the major, students should begin this major during their sophomore year.
Requirements
: 15 credits, plus an additional major in the college.

The Business Analytics major at Marquette University is your gateway to a successful career in the rapidly evolving field of data analytics. By combining rigorous academic training with practical experience, we ensure that our graduates are well-prepared to meet the demands of the modern business world. Learning Outcomes
Define a business problem that can be addressed with data analytics
Identify appropriate data sources and acquire required data
Prepare and organize the data for analysis of the business problem
Apply and interpret appropriate descriptive, prescriptive, & predictive analytical methods to address a business problem
Effectively communicate the results and insights gained from the analysis to address the business problem
Understand ethical issues and implications regarding the data used and results obtained
