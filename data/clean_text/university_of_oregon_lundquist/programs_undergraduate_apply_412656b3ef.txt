Two Paths
After being admitted to the university, you will follow one of two paths to become an accounting or business major.
Standard Admission
After being accepted to the university, students must complete lower division requirements before applying to the business or accounting major and beginning upper division courses.
A 3.0 lower division core and cumulative GPA is required to apply to the business or accounting major.
View Standard Admission Process
First-Year Direct Admission
After applying to the university and indicating an interest in a business or accounting major, high school seniors are selected as a direct admit based on test scores and GPA, among other factors.
Students complete lower division requirements before starting their upper division course—no need to apply for full major status.
View Direct Admissions Process
