FAQ's about International Applications
How do I apply to a graduate program at the Lally School of Management?
You can apply online through Rensselaer’s
Graduate Admissions Portal
. Select your desired Lally program (MBA, M.S., or Ph.D.) and follow the application checklist, which includes transcripts, a resume, statement of purpose, letters of recommendation, and English language proficiency scores (if required).
How do I learn more about financial aid for international students at Lally?
Lally offers merit-based scholarships to qualified international applicants—no separate application is required. You can also explore fellowships, external scholarships, and employer sponsorship opportunities. Visit our Graduate Scholarships and Grants page or contact lallygrad@rpi.edu for personalized guidance.
Do Lally graduates get jobs in the U.S.?
Yes. Lally’s graduate programs are STEM-designated, making them eligible for 3 years of OPT. Our students benefit from career workshops, employer networking events, and access to top companies that sponsor H-1B visas.
