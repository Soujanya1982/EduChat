Undergraduate Advisement Center
We are here to help students enrich their educational experience.
Our goal is to help you prepare to enter the BYU Marriott School of Business; understand academic requirements, university tools, and resources; and support you in your path to successful graduation. We encourage you to meet with us early and often.
We provide several services. Visit us to:
Learn about BYU Marriott majors and minors
Prepare for the application process to limited-enrollment programs
Connect with BYU Marriott student associations and extracurricular activities
Plan GE, religion, degree requirement, and elective courses
Navigate university registration tools
Clarify business transfer requirements
