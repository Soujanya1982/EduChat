Sustainability Advisory Council
The Wake Forest University School of Business Sustainability Advisory Council includes students and alumni that share the University’s passion and commitment to sustainability initiatives. In coordination with Ashley Wilcox, the Director of Sustainability Initiatives, they bring valuable insights and experience to ongoing efforts in the pursuit of sustainability policy, education, and awareness both on the campus and in the wider community.
