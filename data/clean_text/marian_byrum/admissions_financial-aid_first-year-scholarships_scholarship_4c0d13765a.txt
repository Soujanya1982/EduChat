Athletic Award
The Marian University Department of Athletes prides itself on the academic success of our student-athletes and the performance of our varsity sports teams.
The amount of athletic scholarships at Marian University varies, ranging from partial to full tuition.
Our athletic awards are competitive. Students selected for athletic scholarships are evaluated based on their athletic abilities and their potential to make a positive team contribution. Personal interviews with team coaches are required.
Athletic awards are available for every varsity sport, including our newest sports: competitive cheerleading, dance, women's lacrosse, and wrestling.
