Tuition and Financial Aid
At Marquette, we understand that a college education is a significant investment for our students and their families.
We remain committed to making a Marquette education accessible for all: 99% of our undergraduate students received some form of financial aid last year, and we distributed more than $170 million in scholarships.
See our current tuition rates and learn more about financial aid opportunities below.

The State of Wisconsin provides a tuition subsidy for certain dental students who are Wisconsin residents. As with any governmental appropriation, the exact amount is subject to change as part of the legislative process. The expected state subsidy for 2025-26 is $4,330 per term/ $8,660 per year, per Wisconsin resident, but is subject to change at any time. Students will be billed for any reduction in the expected state subsidy and credited for any increase.
