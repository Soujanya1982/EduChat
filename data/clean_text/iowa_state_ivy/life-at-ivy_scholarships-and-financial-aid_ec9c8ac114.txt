Scholarships and Financial Aid
Scholarships are available
We have great news! You may be eligible for scholarships as an incoming business student. At Ivy, we offer more than $2.5 million in scholarships, with an average scholarship award of $2,800. That can be combined with other scholarships and support. Once Iowa State University offers you admission, you can apply for scholarships. Make sure to
apply for admission now
.
We have a long list of scholarships available, but that’s not something we want you to stress about. Completing
one application
ensures that you will be considered for all Ivy College of Business scholarships for which you are eligible. We’re doing all we can to make this easier for you.

Graduate students
There is no application needed at this level. Graduate students in the Ivy College of Business are automatically considered for qualifying scholarships with their admission into an Ivy College of Business graduate program. Please note that not all graduate programs have scholarships available. If you have questions regarding graduate program scholarships, please contact
busgrad@iastate.edu
.
Graduate funding opportunities
Undergraduate study abroad students
Students who have been accepted to a study abroad program may be eligible for scholarships through the Ivy College of Business. More information on applying for scholarships can be found on the
Ivy College of Business study abroad scholarship web page
.
Emergency assistance
Emergency grant funding is sometimes available to current Ivy College of Business students who have exhausted all options available through the Office of Student Financial Aid. Funding is available on a first-come, first-serve basis, and student needs will be evaluated on an individual basis. For more information, please email
ivyscholarships @iastate.edu
.
