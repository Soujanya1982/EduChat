Talent Award for Vocal Performance
The Fine Arts Talent Award for Vocal Performance is available for accepted students with outstanding vocal abilities who agree to be active participants in the Marian University Show Choir. This award is based on the level of the recipient's commitment and openings within the choir for the current season.
Eligibility
If you wish to apply for the Fine Arts Talent Award for Vocal Performance, you must:
Have completed the
Marian University admissions application process
and been admitted as a student in good standing.
Perform vocally during a live audition on campus.
Demonstrate outstanding vocal ability, talent, and commitment through the audition process.
Commit to an educational experience framed within the context of Marian University’s Franciscan values of
dignity of the individual
,
peace and justice
,
reconciliation
, and
responsible stewardship
.
Application process
Complete the
admission process to Marian University
.
Schedule and complete a live audition. Contact Beverly Day at
bday@marian.edu
or (317) 955-6176 for more information.
Selection
If you are selected for this award, you will be notified in writing.
Renewal
The Fine Arts Talent Award for Vocal Performance is renewable annually if you maintain full-time student enrollment status and successfully fulfill all requirements outlined in Marian University's Vocal Performance Participation Agreement.
