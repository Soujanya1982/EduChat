Global Engagement Office
International Student Success
We welcome and support you by:
Understanding your immigration responsibilities
, especially with regard to class enrollment and employment.
Helping you learn how to navigate daily life in the United States
, such as obtaining a driver's license, phone, bank account, and other necessities.
Creating educational and social activities
that empower you to become an active, engaged community members, both on campus and around the city.
Serving as an advocate for you and your
unique cultural, immigration, employment, and academic concerns.
Ensuring you have a positive academic and student experience
at Marian University.
