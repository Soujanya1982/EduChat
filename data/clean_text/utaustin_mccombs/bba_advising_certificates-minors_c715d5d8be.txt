Certificates and Minors
Whether you're majoring in psychology, engineering, advertising, finance, or anything in-between, a background in business provides a valuable skillset for the professional world, no matter where your career might take you.
Minors & Certificates for Undergraduates
Minors and certificates available are based on your catalog year.
Minors and certificates
are not required
to complete a BBA degree.
After you apply for a minor/certificate and are approved, you
must
have your advisor officially attach the minor/certificate to your degree profile.
You can only have
one minor
attached to your degree.
You can add as many certificates as you’d like to.
You
cannot
earn a minor in the same field of study as your major.
All minor courses
must
be taken on a letter-grade basis.
Minors will show on your transcript and
must
be completed by the time you graduate. Certificates can be completed
up to one year
post-graduation.
