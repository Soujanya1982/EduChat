Discover the Lerner MBA
Thinking about earning your MBA? Don’t miss this great opportunity to join a virtual information session with a representative from the UD Lerner MBA admissions team. In these sessions, you’ll learn about the MBA program, including admissions requirements, curriculum structure, career and networking opportunities, support resources for students and more. Click below to register and take the next step toward your UD Lerner MBA, whether on campus, online, or a hybrid of both.
