Academic Advising Appointment Details:
Students schedule and attend a mid-semester 20-minute appointment with their assigned college academic advisor each official advising period in the fall and spring semesters. Appointments are not required during a student's final semester.

Advising prep form:
Complete before your academic advising appointment each semester. After completing, results will automatically be shared with students and advisors.
Students who do not complete the Advising prep form and meet with their academic advisor during the designated timeframe will have an academic hold added to their account that prevents registration of future courses until the final day of assigned registration times.
