Imagine Spieker | High School Student Information Session
VIRTUAL EVENT
Imagine Spieker is an information session available to high school students who are considering applying to the Business Major at UC Berkeley. In this admissions information session, we will cover the business major, admissions & application process, and the Haas community.

Business@Berkeley | UC Berkeley Continuing Student Outreach Event
VIRTUAL EVENT
Business@Berkeley is an information session available to Continuing UC Berkeley students who are considering applying to the business major. In this admissions information session, we will cover the business major, admissions & application process, business curriculum, the Haas community, and opportunities for non-degree seeking students.
