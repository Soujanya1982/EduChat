How To Apply
Follow the steps below for details regarding Purdue's
Office of the Vice Provost for Graduate Students and Post Doctoral Scholars application
process:
First Time User: Create an Account
You will be emailed a temporary PIN to log into the Office of the Vice Provost for Graduate Students and Post Doctoral Scholars Application. Check your junk mail folder if you do not see the email.
Log in using your birth date and this temporary PIN. Once logged in, you will be prompted to create a password. Use this password along with your email address in subsequent logins.
Activate Your Account using your temporary PIN.
Begin your application. Your application will autosave as you move between fields.
Complete your application!
