Family Weekend @ UT Austin
UT Austin's Family Weekend has gone by many names through the years, like "Dad's Day" and "Parents Weekend". Regardless of what it's been called, this annual campus tradition has brought many generations of Longhorn families to the Forty Acres to visit their students and learn what it means to live the Longhorn life.
