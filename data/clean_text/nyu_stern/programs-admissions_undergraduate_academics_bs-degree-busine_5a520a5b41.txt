TITLE: BS in Business/BFA in Film and Television - NYU Stern

Dual Degree Program
BS in Business/BFA in Film and Television
Earn two degrees in five years through this joint NYU Stern and NYU Tisch dual degree program.
The NYU Stern-Tisch BS in Business and BFA in Film/TV (BS/BFA) is a five-year, STEM-eligible degree program that provides students with a passion for ﬁlm/television and business the opportunity to combine full degrees in both program areas. While completing your degrees, you will have full access to resources from both NYU Stern Undergraduate College and the NYU Tisch Kanbar Institute of Film and Television. You will also receive personal advising in both programs and exclusive networking opportunities in the media and entertainment industries. Stern-Tisch BS/BFA students are prepared to pursue entrepreneurial creative endeavors or embark on various careers in the entertainment industry.
You will also have the ﬂexibility to study away, concentrate in business areas, and take advantage of cross-school minors at NYU.
Application Requirements
To apply, you must be a fully- matriculated NYU Stern or NYU Tisch undergraduate student entering sophomore or junior year
Minimum GPA of 3.0
Submission of a creative portfolio that clearly demonstrates creative ability in ﬁlm and television
Prior coursework and resume that clearly demonstrates quantitative abilities and a strong interest in the ﬁeld of business
