Apply Now!
At Duquesne University, we believe it is important to help international students
who want to come to the United States for their education in any way that we can.
We have a dedicated
Center for Global Engagement
staff who assist students through the application and new student process. Each year,
we welcome students from more than 85 countries.
As a Duquesne student, you will learn with students from all cultures and backgrounds.
Make the Center for Global Engagement your first point of contact. We look forward
to serving you as you explore your opportunities at Duquesne.
