4995 Form
Permission for an Undergraduate Student to Enroll in a Graduate Course
Students admitted into Accelerated Degree Programs (ADP) receive priority registration for undergraduate seats available in graduate level courses. Students not admitted into ADP will be considered for graduate courses once the registration period closes, and permission numbers will be sent the following week. Course requests are due by the Thursday before the add/drop deadline each session. Students can take a maximum of 6 graduate credits per term.
To speak with a recruiter regarding an ADP program and the application process, please reach out to
gsm@marquette.edu
.

Marquette Core Curriculum Discovery Tier Theme Declaration
Students should declare a Discovery Tier Theme by the end of sophomore year. Students do not have to declare a theme in order to enroll in one of the courses. Students can change Discovery Tier Themes, but courses previously taken may not apply to the new selected theme.
