Business Technology, BSBA
Objective
The Bachelor of Science in Business Administration with a focus on Business Technology stands as the heavy technical track that provides students with the advanced coding skills and applied computer science to carry out functions such as app development, systems analysis, or big data management.
What You'll Learn
In addition to satisfying the requirements of the University’s Cognates Program of General Education and electives to meet the overall 120-credit degree requirement, students pursuing the BSBA in Business Technology must complete the BSBA Business Core of Required Courses and the specific coursework for the Business Technology area of specialization.
