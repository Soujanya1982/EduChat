Interviews
Join Us for an Interview
Schedule an interview to share more about yourself and your interest in Fairfield, while also getting answers to any questions you may have about the University.
Interviews are available to prospective students beginning the summer before their senior year of high school. They are offered during Fairfield’s summer term, fall semester, and spring semester through mid-February. At this point in time, interviews are available to students who graduate high school in 2027.
For Summer 2026, interviews (both on-campus and virtual) are offered Monday through Thursday from 9:30 a.m. to 12:00 p.m. and again from 1:00 p.m. to 3:30 p.m. running Monday, June 1 through Thursday, August 6. Interviews last approximately 30 minutes and are conducted by the Office of Undergraduate Admission.
