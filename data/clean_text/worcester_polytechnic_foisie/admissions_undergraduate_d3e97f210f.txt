Welcome to WPI Admissions!
Is STEM your thing, but not your only thing?
WPI is a top-ranked university with more than
50 undergraduate degree programs
in science, engineering, technology, math, business, and the humanities and arts. A WPI education will enable you to pursue your passions at a world-class research institution with an innovative project-based curriculum where you combine theory and practice.
