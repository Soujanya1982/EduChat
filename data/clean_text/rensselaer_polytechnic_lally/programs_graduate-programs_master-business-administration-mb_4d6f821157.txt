FAQ's about Lallys MBA in Management
What makes the Lally MBA program unique?
The Lally MBA is a STEM-designated program that blends business leadership with technical and analytical skills. Students benefit from RPI’s strong reputation in technology, small cohort sizes, personalized mentorship, and real-world project experience.
Is the Lally MBA a STEM-designated program?
Yes, the Lally MBA is officially STEM-designated, allowing international students on an F-1 visa to apply for a 24-month OPT extension after graduation.
How long does it take to complete the MBA?
The full-time MBA can be completed in 1 to 2 years, depending on your academic background, course load, and chosen specialization.
What are the admission requirements for the MBA program?
Applicants typically need a bachelor’s degree, resume, transcripts, letters of recommendation, and a personal statement. GMAT/GRE scores are optional. International students must also provide proof of English language proficiency.
Are scholarships or financial aid available?
Yes. Merit-based scholarships are available for qualified students. U.S. citizens and permanent residents may also apply for federal financial aid.
Can I pursue the Lally MBA part-time or online?
Currently, the MBA program is offered on campus fostering immersive learning and hands-on collaboration. For other options, visit

What career support is available for MBA students?
MBA students receive personalized career coaching, resume and interview prep, networking opportunities, and access to top employers through RPI’s Center for Career and Professional Development.
