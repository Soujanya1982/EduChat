TITLE: Berkley Achievement Scholars Program - NYU Stern

Berkley Achievement Scholars Program
The Berkley Achievement Scholars Program enables a select group of talented students with financial need from across the United States and representing the full cross-section of the country to attend the Stern Undergraduate College
tuition-free
.
History
This program was made possible through a landmark scholarship gift from the Berkley Family Foundation, established by alumnus William R. Berkley and his wife, Marjorie. At the time of giving, this was the single largest scholarship gift in Stern and NYU history. The first class of scholars entered in 2022.
Vision
To empower high-achieving students with exceptional ability and fortitude to make a positive impact in their personal and professional lives.
Mission
Through academic excellence, leadership development, and career readiness, Stern equips Scholars with the skills, relationships, and experiences to help them succeed in their undergraduate career.
Program Benefits
Berkley Achievement Scholars enjoy benefits including but not limited to:
Full tuition support
One-on-one coaching
Academic support
First Year Seminar
InSternships
Career development
Cultural experiences
Corporate site visits
Exclusive networking
Eligibility
Berkley Achievement Scholars are selected upon admission to NYU Stern Undergraduate College.
