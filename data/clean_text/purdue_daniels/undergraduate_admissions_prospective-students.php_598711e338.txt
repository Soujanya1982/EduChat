Finding Your Fit
Purdue’s
Mitch Daniels School of Business
is an innovative, data-driven program within one of the nation’s top STEM universities. Our students learn to think analytically, lead with confidence and solve complex problems at the intersection of business and technology.
As a Daniels School student, you’ll benefit from the energy, resources and real-world opportunities that come with being part of a Big Ten, Research I university.

Your Degree, Your Way
At the Daniels School of Business, you will build a foundation for leadership, innovation and long-term success. All undergraduate business majors focus on rigorous data analysis, logical reasoning, sophisticated communication skills and critical thinking.
Accounting
Learn to turn financial data into strategic insight. Build a foundation for roles in auditing, tax, consulting or corporate finance.
Business Analytics and Information Management
Use data to drive smart business decisions. Gain the technical and analytical skills top employers demand.
Economics
Understand how markets work and business decisions are made. Prepare for careers in policy, consulting, finance or law.
Finance
Master the principles of investing, financial strategy and risk management. Launch a career in banking, investing or corporate finance.
General Management
Develop leadership and decision-making skills across business functions. Ideal for students seeking flexibility and broad expertise.
Integrated Business and Engineering
Combine business strategy with engineering thinking. Tackle complex problems and lead in tech-driven industries.
Marketing
Learn how to build brands, connect with customers and drive results. Perfect for creative thinkers with a strategic mindset.
Quantitative Business Economics
Blend economics, statistics and business tools to analyze data and predict trends. Great for careers in research, analytics and policy.
Supply Chain and Operations Management
Design and manage the systems that keep business moving. Focus on efficiency, logistics and global operations.
Take the next step
Choose your major and apply to the Daniels School of Business to begin shaping your future at Purdue.
Want to learn more before you commit? Join us for an admitted-student event. From group visits to individual appointments, we’ll help you explore your options and get a feel for life as a Boilermaker.
The Office of Admissions considers each applicant through a holistic review process. If your academic profile falls below our average range, we encourage you to list a second-choice major to keep another path to Purdue open.
