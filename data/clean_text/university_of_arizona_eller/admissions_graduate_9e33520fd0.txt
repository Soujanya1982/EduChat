Dual Degrees
Many Eller students choose to enhance the value of their graduate degree even more by earning a dual degree. In fact, there are 11 available within Eller College disciplines and an additional 19 potential options with other UA graduate programs. Students do need to apply to their second degree program separately.
Explore Our Dual Degree Options
For additional information regarding admissions, please contact the appropriate program. Note that admissions deadlines are subject to change.
