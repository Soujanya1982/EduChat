Excel Exam Registration
Before declaring a Robins School of Business major or Business Administration minor, undergraduate students must complete at least 12 units including MATH 211 & ECON 101 & ACCT 201 as well as earn at least a 2.7+ cumulative grade point average and pass with an 80% or better the Excel Competency Exam. The exam is offered multiple times during the academic year and students have three attempts to pass the exam. Watch the video for more information about the Excel Exam.
Students must pass the exam prior to declaring. University policy requires that a student wishing to declare a Robins School major or Business ADministration minor must declare no later than the start of their junior year. The in-person exam is conducted through SAM (a Cengage platform). To take the exam you will need to create a SAM account.
