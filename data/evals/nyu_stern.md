## Q09 [9/10 PASS]

**Question:** What are the requirements for international students applying as freshmen?

**Answer:**

To apply as a freshman at NYU, international students must complete their country's pre-university education (equivalent to U.S. 12th grade) before enrolling [S1]. They may also qualify if they are completing an internationally-recognized equivalent, such as the IB Diploma, or applying from countries with school leaving qualifications based on the British educational model [S4]. Additionally, international students must submit official documents, including transcripts and test scores, and may need to provide proof of English proficiency [S5]. They can apply using the Common Application, and must meet the same admissions standards as domestic applicants [S1].
