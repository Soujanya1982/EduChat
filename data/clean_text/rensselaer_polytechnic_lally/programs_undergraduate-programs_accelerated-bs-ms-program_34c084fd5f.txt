TITLE: Accelerated Masters, Bachelor of Science (B.S.) + Master of Science (M.S.)

Earn Two Degrees in Five Years — and Launch with a Competitive Edge
The Accelerated Master’s Program at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute (RPI) allows high-achieving students to earn a Bachelor of Science (B.S.) and a Master of Science (M.S.) in just five years through the Co-Terminal program.
Students can start graduate-level business coursework as early as their junior or senior year, continuing eligible Rensselaer undergraduate funding for up to two semesters of graduate study. This accelerated path saves both time and tuition while equipping students with business depth for fast-growing industries.
Program Overview & Timeline
Whether you're majoring in engineering, science, technology, or business, this program helps you understand and operate effectively in a business-driven world. Lally’s M.S. degrees build your analytical, financial, and strategic capabilities so you can lead with confidence in any industry.
Available M.S. Programs:
Business Analytics
Quantitative Finance and Risk Analytics
Supply Chain Management
Typical Timeline:
Years 1–3: Complete your undergraduate degree requirements.
Year 4: Take up to two M.S. courses during your senior year. These may be counted as free electives under RPI’s credit-sharing policy.
Year 5: Finish the remaining M.S. coursework in two graduate semesters, graduating with both degrees.

FAQs
Can I complete both degrees in five years?
Yes. With strategic planning and advisor support, most students complete both the B.S. and M.S. within five academic years.
Can I begin my M.S. coursework as an undergraduate?
Yes. You may start taking M.S. courses during your
junior or senior year
, depending on your schedule.
What are the benefits of an M.S. over an MBA?
The M.S. is
