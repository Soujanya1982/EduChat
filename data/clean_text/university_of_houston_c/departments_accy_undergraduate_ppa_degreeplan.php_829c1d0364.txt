If admitted into the MSACCY program, PPA students have the option of taking up to 6hrs of graduate-level courses during their undergraduate career. ELEC
Double Count
Details
If admitted into the MSACCY program, PPA students have the option of taking up to 6hrs of graduate-level courses during their undergraduate career. If the grade earned in the course meets the graduate-level minimum grade requirements, the hours will count toward BBA electives and MSACCY degree requirements.*
*Students must enter the MSACCY program in the next long semester after graduating from the BBA program. Failure to do so means the double-count classes will not automatically count toward MSACCY degree requirements.
