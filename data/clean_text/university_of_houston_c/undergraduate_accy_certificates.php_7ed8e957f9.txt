Internal Audit Certificate
The mission of the Internal Audit (IA) Certificate is to provide students with the most up-to-date theory and practice in the field of Internal Auditing. The Accountancy & Taxation Department at the C. T. Bauer College of Business has 1 of only 12 internal audit programs in the world and 1 of only 7 in the country to be recognized as a Center of Excellence by the Institute of Internal Auditors. The IA offers courses in Internal Audit that can culminate in a certificate at the Undergraduate level. These courses are taught by current practitioners in the Internal Audit field, who bring a wealth of real-world experience to the classroom.
