How to Apply for Aid
Four Simple Steps
It might surprise you, but there are only four main steps to receiving financial support at Seattle University.
Step 1
Apply for Admission
Apply for admission to Seattle University.
Apply Now
Step 2
File your FAFSA or WASFA
Submit your Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA).
Undocumented students submit the Washington Application for State Financial Aid (WASFA) instead of the FAFSA application.
