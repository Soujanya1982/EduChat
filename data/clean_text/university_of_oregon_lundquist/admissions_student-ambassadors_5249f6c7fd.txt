What's it like to be a Duck?
One of the best ways to learn about the University of Oregon community is to talk with current students. Our student ambassadors are current students focused on earning their degree and giving back to the community. They are available to answer questions you may have about their degree programs, chosen specializations, student clubs, moving to the area, life on campus, experiential learning, the Duck network, and much more.

Undergraduate Ambassadors

Graduate Ambassadors
Our graduate student ambassadors are current students enrolled in one of our four specialized master’s programs: the Master of Accounting, MBA, Master of Science in Finance, and Master of Science in Management. These dedicated volunteers are passionate about sharing their experiences and offering insights into life at the Lundquist College of Business. Whether you're exploring your options or preparing to apply, our ambassadors are here to help. Select the program you're interested in below to connect with a current graduate student who can answer your questions and provide a firsthand perspective.
