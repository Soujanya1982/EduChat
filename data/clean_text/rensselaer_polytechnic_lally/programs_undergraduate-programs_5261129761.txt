Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)- Undergraduate Programs at Lally
What undergraduate business programs are offered at Lally?
We offer several undergraduate business programs, including STEM-designated options like Business Analytics, Marketing and Advanced Computing, and Finance, Information Technology and Web Science, Markets, and Emerging Technologies, as well as a comprehensive Business and Management degree.
Is a business analytics bachelor’s degree a good career choice?
Yes! A business analytics bachelor’s degree is one of the most in-demand programs today. It prepares students for careers in data-driven fields such as consulting, finance, operations, and tech.
What makes Lally one of the best undergraduate business schools?
Lally combines rigorous academics, real-world experience, and strong industry connections. With high salary outcomes, experiential learning, and interdisciplinary options, it’s consistently ranked among the top business schools for marketing, analytics, and innovation.
Can I pursue an accelerated MBA as an undergraduate?
Yes. Our accelerated MBA for undergraduates allows students to earn both a bachelor’s and master’s degree in a shortened time frame, saving both time and tuition while maximizing career impact.
Do business majors from Lally earn high salaries?
Definitely. Graduates of our business programs report some of the highest starting salaries nationwide, with average starting compensation above $71K and top salaries reaching $110K.
Can I earn a STEM business degree as an undergraduate student?
At Lally, we offer four forward-thinking,
STEM-designated B.S. degrees
that integrate business education with technology, analytics, and innovation:
Finance, Markets, and Emerging Technologies
Develop a strong foundation in financial systems, market dynamics, and emerging tech to prepare for roles in fintech, investment, and global markets.
Marketing and Advanced Computing
Combine creative strategy with advanced data analysis and computing tools to drive marketing decisions in today’s digital economy.
Business Analytics
Master the art of transforming data into actionable insights with this analytics-focused degree designed for data-driven business leadership.
Information Technology and Web Science
Interdisciplinary from day one, the ITWS program equips you to shape the future of the Web by combining a strong foundation in IT and Web systems with a customizable focus across 20+ fields from AI and engineering to management and medicine.
These programs position our students at the intersection of business, technology, and quantitative analysis—ideal for those seeking high-growth careers in today’s most competitive industries.
What is a dual major business program?
Our dual major business programs allow students to combine a B.S. in business with other RPI majors, accelerating their academic journey and broadening career opportunities.
