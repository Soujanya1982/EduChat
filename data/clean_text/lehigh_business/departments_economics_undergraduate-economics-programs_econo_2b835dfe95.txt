Economics
Minoring in Economics
All students from the College of Arts & Sciences, the P.C. Rossin College of Engineering, the College of Health, and the intercollegiate programs IDEAS, CSB, and IBE are eligible to minor in economics.
Requirements for the minor are show below. In all cases, students are responsible for completing any prerequisites the required courses might involve. Please check with the Minor Advisor for more information.
Minor Advisor:
Professor Mengxiao Liu
(Rauch Business Center, Room 459;
mel622@lehigh.edu
)
Requirements
A student must complete 16 credits to minor in economics. These credits consist of three required courses:
ECO 001, Principles of Economics: 4 credits
ECO 146, Intermediate Microeconomics Analysis: 3 credits
ECO 119, Intermediate Macroeconomics Analysis: 3 credits
plus two electives, one of which can be a 200-level course, but one of which must be a 300-level course:
ECO 2xx or ECO 3xx: 3 credits
ECO 3xx: 3 credits
Important
– Please note that ECO 201, ECO 259, ECO 273, ECO 274, ECO 301, ECO 362, ECO 371, and ECO 389
may not
be used to fulfill the elective requirement.
