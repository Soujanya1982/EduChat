BESP Fellows
What You’ll Learn
The BESP Fellows Program admits around 30 students a year from the Mendoza College of Business, chosen in a competitive application process in the spring semester.
BESP Fellows supplement the standard course of business studies with a deeper appreciation of the philosophical, theological, and moral foundations of business careers and commercial society. This 1.5 credit-hour colloquium is a seminar-style, small group program each semester that includes catered dinners, special guest lectures, and fascinating discussions.
Admission
Admission to the BESP Fellows Program is competitive and requires submission of an application. (Letters and nominations from faculty members are not required, but are welcome.)
