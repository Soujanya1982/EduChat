Isenberg's academic approach blends innovative coursework with real-world experience, giving students competitive business skills along with essential knowledge. The school offers seven majors and students have the option to combine a business degree with another major at UMass Amherst. Accounting and Political Science? Yes. Marketing and Computer Science? Absolutely.
