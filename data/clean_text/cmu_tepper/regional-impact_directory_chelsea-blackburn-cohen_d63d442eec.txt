Collaboration Interests
As chair of the Community-Based Work Coalition, I serve as a bridge between CMU people and programs to meet interests, needs, and opportunities in our broader external community. I am interested in supporting cross-collaboration throughout Carnegie Mellon and identifying opportunities to amplify the community engagement and impact of CMU's faculty, staff, and students.
I am passionate about data-driven decision-making to maximize institutional impact and engagement. (Talk to me about CMU's Impact and Engagement framework!) I am always happy to meet with new colleagues to understand their work's scope and to synthesize connections to other relevant people and programs across the university.
