BBA
Go Farther. Go Anywhere.
Whether you work for-profit or non-profit, whether you start your own business or land a spot at one of the world’s leading corporations, a business degree is a hot commodity, helping you build crucial skills like leadership, strategy and negotiation that you can leverage for success in any situation.
And no other school can deliver a BBA experience like TCU Neeley.
Curriculum based on real-world strategies and hands-on learning. Small classes taught by faculty who are globally recognized for their expertise and passion. Advisors who are friendly and eager to help you design your unique degree plan. And an alumni network full of thousands of leaders from across the U.S. and around the world. All this from a gorgeous campus in the heart of Dallas-Fort Worth, the fourth-largest metropolitan area in the nation, full of culture, entertainment – and connections.

Professional Program in Accounting
Accelerate your path to professional success with the Professional Program in Accounting (PPA) at TCU Neeley. This distinguished five-year pathway integrates your BBA with a Master of Accounting, gives you early access to the graduate program, and primes you for the CPA exam – all while building leadership, professional development and career readiness. Open to Accounting majors and minors, the PPA starts in your junior year with immersive experiences, networking opportunities and strategic career education that help you stand out to top employers.
