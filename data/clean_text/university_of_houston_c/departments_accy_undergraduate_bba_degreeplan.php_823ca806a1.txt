Fast Track Exam (Optional)
The Department of Accountancy & Taxation offers an optional Fast Track Exam for students who wish to attempt to waive ACCT 3366 Financial Reporting Frameworks and enroll directly in ACCT 3367 Intermediate Accounting 1.
The exam is administered in person through CASA and is offered at the end of the fall, spring, and summer semesters.
To register, students must:
Complete
the form here
by the deadline indicated on the form.
Reserve a testing time through CASA.
