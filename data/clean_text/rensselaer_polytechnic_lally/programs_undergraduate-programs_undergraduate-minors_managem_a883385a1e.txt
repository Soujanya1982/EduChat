TITLE: Management, Minor | RPI Lally School of Management

Program Overview
Add a new perspective to your studies in engineering, science, or the humanities with a minor that introduces you to managerial functions and processes, corporate financial decision-making, financial accounting systems, and more. This program is designed specifically for Rensselaer students majoring in other fields.
Required Courses:
MGMT 1100 —
Introduction to Management
MGMT 2300 —
Financial Accounting
MGMT 2320 —
Managerial Finance
4000-Level Restricted Elective (see minor adviser)
Designated minor advisers can approve course substitutions to meet individual student needs.
