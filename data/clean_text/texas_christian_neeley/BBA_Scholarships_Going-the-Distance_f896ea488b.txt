Going the Distance
Finances are understandably a major part of your educational experience, and that’s why we’re here to help. Whether it’s textbooks, meals or dorm essentials, we’ve got you covered.
At TCU Neeley, we never want to see a student have to drop a class, or leave the Neeley School or TCU because of student-related financial challenges.
Application
If you are experiencing financial hardship, consider applying for assistance from the Going the Distance Fund.
