The university also permits applicants to use Score Choice to discern which score reports to submit [S1]. Additionally, applicants who face hardship in meeting this requirement can submit a waiver instead [S2].

## Q05 [10/10 ✓]

**Question:** What scholarships or financial aid are available to incoming freshmen?

**Answer:**

Incoming freshmen may be eligible for financial aid packages that cover various expenses, including tuition, fees, housing, and dining [S1]. Students with family incomes less than $75,000 receive financial aid packages guaranteed to cover all billed expenses and receive additional resources [S1]. They are also eligible for benefits like laptop funding, summer courses, and summer research and internship experiences [S1]. Additionally, students with family incomes up to $200,000 who are eligible for aid receive a financial aid package that covers at minimum full tuition [S1][S3]. The university also offers support for low-income students, including waived summer savings expectation, dedicated financial aid counselors, and emergency funding [S2].
