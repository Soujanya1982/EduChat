Minors
The College of Business Administration offers minors to students that are
not
enrolled in the College of Business Administration. These minors provide students from other disciplines the opportunity to gain business knowledge. In addition, students can enrich their current area of study with a complementing minor (e.g., a psychology major with a human resources minor).
