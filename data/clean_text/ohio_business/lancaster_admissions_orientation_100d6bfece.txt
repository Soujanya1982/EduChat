TITLE: Lancaster Campus New Student Orientation | Ohio University

Get ready to start your Forever OHIO journey at the Lancaster Campus by attending Bobcat Blitz New Student Orientation! At orientation, you'll meet the people and gather the resources you'll need to make the transition into college life with confidence.
During this one day event, you'll:
Learn about success strategies and faculty expectations
Explore online resources and on-campus resources, like career and accessibility services
Gain more understanding of financial aid
Learn about technology on campus
Meet your fellow Bobcats before fall semester begins
Tour campus
Please note: Bobcat Blitz is not conducted in an open house format. We ask that you plan to attend the entire orientation session, which typically lasts a few hours.
Who Should Attend Bobcat Blitz?
Bobcat Blitz is a student welcome event for incoming freshman, transfer and College Credit Plus.
