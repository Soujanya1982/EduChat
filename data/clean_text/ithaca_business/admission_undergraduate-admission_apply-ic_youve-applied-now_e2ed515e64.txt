TITLE: You've Applied. Now What? | Ithaca College

Take the Next Steps
Now that you've submitted your application to IC, it's time to follow through.
Step 1: Activate your personalized IC Connect account and review your application checklist.
After you have submitted your application, you will receive an email with a link to activate your personalized
IC Connect account
. Follow the instructions in the email, and log in to see which pieces of your application have been received. (Please submit any outstanding documents at your earliest convenience.) If you don't see the email in your Inbox, be sure to check your Spam and Promotional folders.
Step 2: Apply for financial aid.
The FAFSA is the only form required to apply for federal and institutional financial aid.
Please be sure to release your data to us.
The Ithaca College school code for the FAFSA is 002739.
The 2026-2027 FAFSA is now available.
The priority deadlines for the FAFSA are:
Early Decision applicants – November 1, 2025
Early Action applicants – December 1, 2025
Regular Decision applicants – February 1, 2026
