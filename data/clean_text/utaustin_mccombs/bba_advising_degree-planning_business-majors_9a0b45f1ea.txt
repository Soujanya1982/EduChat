Degree Planning for Business Majors
Students enter the McCombs School of Business as Unspecified Business majors and may declare their major once they meet all requirements. Business students can only declare and complete one BBA major.
Browse majors & degree plans
Requirements for Declaring a BBA Major
Review college credit requirements by catalog year.
Catalogs 2022-24 and before
Complete 30 hours of college credit
M 408Q or M 408K & M 408L (or equivalent): Calculus
ECO 304K & ECO 304L (or equivalent): Micro and Macro Economics
B A 101 Business Administration
MAN 101: Leadership Challenges & Innovation (2020-22 and later catalogs only)
Catalogs 2024-26 and after
Complete 30 hours of college credit
M 408Q or M 408K & M 408L (or equivalent): Calculus
ECO 304K & ECO 304L (or equivalent): Micro and Macro Economics
B A 101: Business Administration
MAN 101: Leadership Challenges & Innovation
B A 324: Business Communication (Oral and Written) [credit or registration for B A 324]
STA 235: Data Science for Business Applications [credit or registration for STA 325]
Declaring/Changing Your Major
Current business students can
declare or change their major online
and can change their major as many times as needed.
The Canfield Business Honors Program and Integrated BBA/MPA have separate applications for current students.
