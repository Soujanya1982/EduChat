Graduation with University Honors
Students who graduate with University Honors have displayed high levels of achievement throughout their time at UT Austin.
Requirements to Graduate with Honors
No more than 20% of the May graduating class of each college/school may receive honors, high honors, or highest honors.
Students in the Business School
must
have
60 in-residence hours
taken for a
letter-grade
to qualify for Graduation with Honors. Courses with CR (credit)
will not
count as part of the 60 required hours for eligibility.
Percentage requirements
are not
applied to
August and December
graduating classes. The grade point averages for May graduates are applied to the following August and December classes to determine honors, high honors, and highest honors.
