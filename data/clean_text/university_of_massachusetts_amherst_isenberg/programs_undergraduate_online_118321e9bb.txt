Degrees and Majors
Isenberg’s supportive faculty engages students with real-world experience and expertise, encouraging students to become resilient problem solvers for today's fast-paced business climate. Designed for hands-on learning through enhanced technology, Isenberg’s online degree completion program is structured to advance your knowledge in a specialized major, while building core communication and leadership skills through lively discussions and collaborative group projects.
