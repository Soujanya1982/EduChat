Which MBA Is Right for Me?
The Daniels College of Business offers four MBA programs designed for the individual needs and experiences of business professionals.
Denver MBA
Our full-time, on-campus curriculum combines an intensive classroom experience with immersive, real-world business challenges.
Learn More >
Professional MBA
This part-time evening program lets early- to mid-career professionals take the classroom to work and work to the classroom, all while learning in a tight-knit cohort.
Learn More >
Executive MBA
Tailored to meet the needs of professionals with 10–20 or more years of experience, the Executive MBA offers a cohort of peers who learn and lead strategically.
Learn More >
Online MBA
Working professionals can develop the skills to become indispensable to their organization from anywhere in a program that incorporates live virtual classes, self-paced coursework and in-person immersive experiences.
Learn More >
* A specialized MBA is also available for military veterans.
