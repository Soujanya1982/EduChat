Meet the Advising Team
Our advising team is dedicated to helping our students succeed here at NC State. All of our advisors have a master's or higher degree and are professionally trained to assist in designing each student’s academic career at NC State.

Email
Poole College of Management students are encouraged to email questions to the Poole Advising Team at
poole_advising@ncsu.edu
. Our response time is 2-3 business days. Response times may be longer during the first week of classes and peak registration season. To ensure the fastest possible response, please note that sending additional follow-up emails will place your message at the bottom of our inbox.
Appointments
Schedule an Appointment
*Individual appointment availability will be limited during the peak registration period (Monday, March 23rd – Monday, April 13th).
Poole College of Management majors can make an appointment with an advisor. Appointments are used for more in-depth questions, long-term degree planning, or deeper conversations and are scheduled for 30 minutes. Students can use the Student Success Navigate system to make these appointments at
go.ncsu.edu/navigate
.
