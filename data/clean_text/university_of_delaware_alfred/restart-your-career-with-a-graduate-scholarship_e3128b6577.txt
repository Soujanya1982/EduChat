Restart Your Career with a 20% Graduate Scholarship
Your career comeback starts here.
If you’ve experienced job loss or financial hardship in the last two years, the University of Delaware’s Alfred Lerner College of Business and Economics can help you move forward with a 20% graduate scholarship.
What you’ll receive:
✔ 20% scholarship toward any Lerner graduate program
✔ $75 application fee waiver
✔ No GMAT/GRE required for most programs
✔ Flexible formats: Full-time, part-time or fully online
✔ Affordable tuition
To determine your eligibility for one of these scholarships, please contact us at
lernergrad@udel.edu
.
Only one 20% graduate scholarship may be applied per qualifying student for the 2025-2026 academic year. These scholarships cannot be combined with other University of Delaware funding offers, including merit-based awards or assistantships.
