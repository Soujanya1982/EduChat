Banos DSouza Scholarship
Amount
$5,000 and up
Eligibility
Accepted and enrolled at Texas Christian University.
First- or second-year student at time of application.
Demonstrated financial need.
Majoring in any area
other
than business. Business majors may not apply.
Interested in pursuing a minor in Entrepreneurship and Innovation.
Requirements
Online application. Apply below.
A 100-word statement about you, submitted with online application.
If selected as a finalist, submit a video up to 5 minutes explaining how people around you, today and in the future, will be positively impacted if you receive this scholarship.
If selected as finalist, be available for an interview.
Timeframe
Application Closes:
April 3, 2025
