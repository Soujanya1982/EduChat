TITLE: Haas Base Camp - Undergraduate Program - UC Berkeley Haas

Haas Base Camp is a retreat centered on inclusion and cohort groups provides a life-changing experience for students. It goes beyond mere outdoor activities, fostering a deeper understanding, empathy, and appreciation for diversity. Participants depart with more than just memories of exciting adventures; they also gain a broader worldview and forge lasting friendships.
Haas Base Camp (HBC) Fall 2025

September 13

HBC 2025 is an invaluable opportunity for you to make meaningful connections with your fellow cohort, cohort leaders and classmates.
