Suit Up – Lead On
The Suit Up – Lead On program is
supported by the generosity of Monica and Les Garrett '96
and based on the Neeley Promise:
The Neeley School of Business unleashes human potential with leadership at the core and innovation in our spirit®.
This initiative provides one-on-one personal branding consultations and
professional attire
for students with financial need. Awards may vary according to need. All funds will apply only toward professional attire and will not apply to tuition. Each awardee will be provided with a suit, including a shirt/blouse.
