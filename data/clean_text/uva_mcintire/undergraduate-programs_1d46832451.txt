McIntire School of Commerce
Undergraduate Programs
The McIntire School of Commerce blends the expansive knowledge of a liberal arts education with the cutting-edge skills sought by today’s fiercely competitive global marketplace. Widely renowned for our longstanding undergraduate program, and pan-University minors in Entrepreneurship, Leadership, and Real Estate, our programs are designed with an inventive, engaging curriculum that incorporates real-world business experiences of unparalleled quality.

Learn the skills to lead and shape the global business landscape. Tackle real-world problems for executives through the Integrated Core curriculum, and concentrate in one or more study areas: Accounting, Finance, Information Technology, Management, and Marketing.
