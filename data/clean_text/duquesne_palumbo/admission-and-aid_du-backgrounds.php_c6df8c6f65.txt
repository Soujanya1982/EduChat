Duquesne University Quest
As you embark on your college search journey, you can earn badges as you complete
key milestones as part of D.U. Quest.  The D.U. Quest badges mark key moments in the
process like applying, visiting campus, attending a college fair, or getting accepted
to name a few of the badges expected to be available. The goal is to make the admissions
process more personal and interactive, while also giving you a fun, visual way to
track your progress.
