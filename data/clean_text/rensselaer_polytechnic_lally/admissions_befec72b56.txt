FAQ's about Admissions at the Lally School of Management
What GPA is needed to get into the Lally School of Management?
Undergraduate students are typically admitted with GPAs above 3.5. Graduate applicants should have a minimum 3.0 GPA; 3.3 or higher is recommended for competitiveness.
Is the Lally MBA STEM-designated?
Yes. The full-time MBA and M.S. programs are STEM-designated, making them eligible for extended OPT for international students.
Can I apply to RPI/ Lally as an undecided student?
Yes. Rensselaer allows undergraduate students exploration through the business core, and you can declare a major in Lally in your second year.
What career services are available?
Lally students have access to the RPI Center for Career and Professional Development, corporate partnerships, one-on-one coaching, and networking events.
