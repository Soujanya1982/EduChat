Analytics & AI Track
The Analytics & AI Track provides students with broad, interdisciplinary knowledge and skills that will help them as managers to leverage analytics and AI to improve organizational performance and decision-making. Students acquire practical expertise in data management and analysis, utilizing a spectrum of tools—from visualization and statistics to machine learning and AI. This prepares them not only to generate meaningful data-driven insights for diverse audiences, but also to oversee products that leverage data to deliver outcomes across organizations.
Eligibility
The Analytics & AI Track is open to all Commerce students whose academic requirements allow them the flexibility to take the required 12 credit hours.
Coursework
Students are required to complete the following courses:
Required Courses
COMM 3220 Database Management Systems and Business Intelligence
COMM 3330 Marketing and Research Analytic Techniques
Either:
COMM 4260 Foundations of Machine Learning and AI with Low-Code Technologies, or
COMM 4259 Foundations of Machine Learning and AI with Python
Elective Courses
Analytics & AI Track students select 3 credits from the courses listed below:
COMM 4210 Managerial View of AI
COMM 4211 The Ethics of Artificial Intelligence
COMM 4221 Data Exploration and Visualization with R
COMM 4230 Information Technology in Finance
COMM 4263 Intro to Cybersecurity
COMM 4350 Customer Analytics & Brand Strategy
COMM 4351 Marketing Analytics for Big Data
COMM 4371 Strategic Brand Consulting & Communications
COMM 4380 Consumer Behavior and Marketing Strategy
COMM 5140 Accounting for Decision Making and Control
COMM 5559 Accounting Analytics in Financial Markets
