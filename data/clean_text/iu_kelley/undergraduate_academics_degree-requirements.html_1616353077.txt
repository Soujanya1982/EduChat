TITLE: Learning Goals | Bachelor's Degree in Business | Indiana Kelley

In fulfilling the requirements of your Bachelor of Science (BS) in Business degree, you’ll take a combination of classes that balance business and economics with liberal arts to acquire a holistic and relevant business education.
Course requirements
Since we periodically update our course requirements to meet the demands of today’s quickly changing market, the year you start at IU Bloomington will determine your exact course requirements.
The Academic Bulletin corresponding to your year of matriculation will inform you of your specific requirements for completing the following:
IUB General Education requirements
I-Core prerequisites and I-Core
Other courses and credits required for graduation
Major requirements
Academic Bulletin
Academic advising
Work with Kelley academic advisors to explore academic options, develop goals and plans, and track your degree progress. Advisors can also connect you with academic help and other support resources.
