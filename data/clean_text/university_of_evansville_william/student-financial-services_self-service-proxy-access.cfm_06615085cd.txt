Self-Service offers the following features:
Financial Aid
View missing and received documents, financial aid awards, and Satisfactory Academic Progress status. Report outside scholarships and accept or decline Federal Direct Loans and/or Federal Work-Study offered.
Student Finance
View semester billing statements, detailed account activity, and accessing online payment portal (Transact).
Student Planning
Develop academic plan and register for classes.
Employee
View tax information - W-2s when published and 1095-C.
Proxy Access
The Family Education Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA) governs the amount of student information which can be disclosed. UE offices may not discuss account details with anyone not granted Proxy Access by the student. Students may grant Proxy Access to parents and others to view complete or selected access of their financial information. Proxy Access is not available for Student Planning and Employee information at this time.
