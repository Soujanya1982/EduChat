Global Launch in Dublin: First Semester Abroad
UMass Amherst offers first-year accepted Isenberg students the opportunity to begin their college journey abroad through the Global Launch in Dublin&nbsp;Program. Building global business perspectives
UMass Amherst offers first-year accepted Isenberg students the opportunity to begin their college journey abroad through the
Global Launch in Dublin
Program. Building global business perspectives while forming close connections with fellow Isenberg classmates, students will study together in Dublin, Ireland, taking a hybrid of University College Dublin and UMass courses during the fall semester. Students will transition to UMass Amherst in the spring semester as part of a strong, supportive community of future business leaders.

International Study
Students who study abroad consistently describe the experience as life-shaping, and it is something that employers and graduate schools love to see on resumes. You can study for a semester or a year as part of a formal exchange program, or you can take courses that include trips to other countries as part of the class experience. Each Isenberg short-term program combines class time with a study abroad experience led by our faculty.

Our community includes a wide range of student clubs, including Isenberg Women in Business and the Association of Diversity in Sport, as well as an award-winning chapter of the accounting honors society Beta Alpha Psi. Life On Campus
Amherst has been voted one of the best college towns in the nation. With a lively downtown filled with coffee shops, eateries and nightlife, it’s easy to see why it ranks at the top. Nestled in a stunning setting, students enjoy hiking, biking, kayaking, skiing and local farm-to-table eating. On campus, students get the best of both worlds—a close-knit community in a large, vibrant university.
