What's next?
As an admitted student to CU Boulder, here are some next steps you can take:
Accept your offer of admission
Make it official and confirm now! Accepting your offer of admission will allow you to apply for housing and continue your journey to CU Boulder.
Explore housing and dining options
As a first-year student, you
are required to live on campus for your first year. Learn more about community options, including Residential Academic Programs, Living & Learning Communities and traditional communities to decide which options fit you the best.
Get connected with New Student and Family Programs (New Student Orientation)
New Student and Family Programs (NSFP) will help you prepare for academic success, simplify your transition to campus and support your emotional journey. Learn more about
orientation during Admitted Student Day. They will be offering sessions on your next steps to becoming a CU Buff.
