Why Fordham GSE?
At Fordham, we don’t just educate—we empower. Here’s why our Graduate School of Education is the right place for you:
Diverse and Comprehensive Programs:
Whether you're starting your career or advancing to the next level, our programs are designed to meet your goals.
Expert Faculty:
Learn from leaders in education who bring a wealth of experience, research, and industry connections to the classroom.
Hands-On Experience:
Prepare for real-world challenges with internships, field placements, and research opportunities that give you practical experience.
Supportive Community:
Join a vibrant and inclusive community that offers robust support services, from academic advising to career counseling, ensuring you succeed both personally and professionally.
Prime Location and Network:
Benefit from our location in New York City, with access to an extensive network of schools, organizations, and opportunities that enrich your educational experience.
Become a Non-Matriculated Student
Whether you are deciding if you want to apply for a degree program, need prerequisites before applying, or are pursuing professional development, applying as a non-matriculated student allows you to take courses without enrolling in a degree program.
