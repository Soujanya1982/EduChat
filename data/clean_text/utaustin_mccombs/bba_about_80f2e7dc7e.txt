About Us
Learn about the McCombs BBA program, our Community Values, how you can help support us, and some of the initiatives we're working on. Our Community Values were created through a combination of student, staff, and faculty feedback and are our vision for our school, our students, and our future. We strive to meet these every day and encourage McCombs students to do the same.
McCombs Parents Council
The McCombs Parents Council is an opportunity for parents of enrolled business students to be involved with our programs and initiatives. With multiple ways to engage and different areas to support, you can find something that fits your ideals, whether it's academics, career services, study abroad, or one of our other numerous options.
