Marketing, BBA
Objective
The Bachelor of Business Administration in Marketing equips you with the theory and skills to leverage marketing’s role within any organization and society, to capitalize on specific markets through better identification and analysis of consumer needs, wants, and interests, and to master the methods, procedures and techniques used in developing, implementing, and managing marketing strategies.
The BBA in Marketing provides a broad foundation for careers in such areas as market research, sales management, advertising, and retailing.
What You'll Learn
In addition to satisfying the requirements of the University’s Cognates Program of General Education and electives to meet the overall 120-credit degree requirement, students pursuing the BBA in Marketing must complete the BBA Business Core of Required Courses and the specific coursework for the Marketing area of specialization.
