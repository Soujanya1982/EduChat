TITLE: Undergraduate Advising | Lundquist College of Business

Our undergraduate advising and student experience team is an excellent source of information and can help you create an academic plan that optimizes your Lundquist experience.
The advising office provides many services to undergraduate pre-business, business, and accounting students, and is the first stop for information about major and minor admission processes, degree requirements, scholarships, educational exchange programs, tutoring services, and student organizations.
Students are urged to meet with a college of business advisor at least once a year. During week one and weeks seven through eleven, students need to plan in advance as demand increases at the start of the term and during registration periods.
