Follow Your Next Steps as an Admitted Student
If you’ve already been admitted to Marquette, congratulations and welcome! We look forward to sharing the Marquette experience and community with you as you make your college decision.
Your admitted student page
Everything you need as an admitted student can be found on your personalized admitted student page. This is where you will officially accept your admission, register for visits, and find all important reminders and updates. This page will update throughout the summer with your to-do list items.
