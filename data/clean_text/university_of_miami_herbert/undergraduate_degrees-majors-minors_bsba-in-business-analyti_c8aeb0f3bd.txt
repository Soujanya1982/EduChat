Business Analytics, BSBA
Objective
Students who choose the Bachelor of Science in Business Administration with focus on Business Analytics are trained to combine quantitative, statistical, and computational tools and techniques to help companies understand, predict, and act on large amounts of data, improving decision-making in increasingly complex and interconnected business environments.
What You'll Learn
In addition to satisfying the requirements of the University’s Cognates Program of General Education and electives to meet the overall 120-credit degree requirement, students pursuing the BSBA in Business Analytics must complete the BSBA Business Core of Required Courses and the specific coursework for the Business Analytics area of specialization.
