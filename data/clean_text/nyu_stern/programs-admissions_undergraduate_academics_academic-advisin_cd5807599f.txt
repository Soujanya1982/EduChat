TITLE: Academic Advising - NYU Stern

Advising tailored to you
Academic Advising
With the help of your adviser, design your curriculum, select a minor, study abroad, join extracurricular activities, and more.
The Office of Academic Advising will serve as an important resource during your time at NYU Stern. We aim to educate through both in-classroom learning and experiences beyond the classroom, such as internships, research, and international immersion. We provide comprehensive guidance to help you design a well-rounded academic program to meet that goal, partnering with you from the very start to map out your undergraduate experience that's tailored to your individual skills and interests.
During one-on-one appointments, an adviser will assist you with curriculum planning and can address topics such as course scheduling, choosing a concentration (for students in the core business program), selecting a minor, study abroad planning, and joining extracurricular activities. In addition to individual advising appointments, the office coordinates student programming that will supplement your academic experience.
If you'd like to dig even deeper before you arrive, the
Stern Academic Bulletin
contains a wealth of information, including curriculum and degree requirements.
