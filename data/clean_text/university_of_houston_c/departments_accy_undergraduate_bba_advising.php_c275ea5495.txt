BBA Accounting Advising
The Professional Accounting Advising Office provides advising to BBA Accounting students.
Advising Appointments
Advising appointments are available online (Microsoft Teams or Zoom) or in-person. Declared accounting majors can schedule an appointment via
Navigate
. Please note, all advising appointments must be scheduled in advance.
Office Location and Map
The Professional Accounting Programs Office is located in room 304 of the
Classroom and Business Building (CBB)
, behind Melcher Hall.
Attend an Accounting Event
The Department of Accountancy & Taxation hosts many events each semester, from coffee chats, webinars, class previews and others. Learn about the admissions process, course details, and academic requirements for CPA eligibility. Please visit the
Accounting Upcoming Events
page for information regarding the scheduled events.
