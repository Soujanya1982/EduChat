Our graduate business degrees offer students a better professional and financial future. We are committed to helping veterans earn a master's degree with limited financial burden. Enrolled students are eligible to apply for Master in Business for Veterans Student Support Fund while enrolled in the program. Funds can be applied towards tuition, books, fees, travel, housing, or other expenses incurred while enrolled in the program.
GI Bill benefits help you pay for college, graduate school, and training programs. If you applied for and were awarded Post-9/11 GI Bill education benefits, your GI Bill Statement of Benefits will show you how much of your benefits you’ve used and how much you have left to use.
Leverage resources on the
U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs website
to explore GI Bill programs and benefits that you are eligible to use toward your education.
Goizueta Business School is proud to have been a Yellow Ribbon partner school since 2009. The Yellow Ribbon Program is a provision of the
Post 9/11 Veterans Educational Assistance Act of 2008 (aka Chapter 33)
, and students must first be eligible for the Post 9/11 GI Bill. Please contact the U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) to see if you are eligible and to request a Certificate of Eligibility (COE). According to the VA, you must use your full GI Bill funding before Yellow Ribbon funding is used.
Goizueta Business School provides Yellow Ribbon funding for all eligible candidates up to $17,000 each, which is matched by the Veterans Administration for a total of $34,000 in Yellow Ribbon funding toward tuition.
Yellow Ribbon Program awards for the academic year are granted on a first come, first served basis. To be considered for a Goizueta Business School Yellow Ribbon Program award, you must
Be admitted to one of Goizueta Business School;s graduate degree programs
Verify your intent to enroll by completing the Candidate Intent form found in your admissions portal and paying the deposit to the program to which you are admitted
Submit a Graduate Admissions Yellow Ribbon Application
Complete an
Emory Veteran Enrollment Form
Submit your Certificate of Eligibility stating you are 100% eligible for the Post 9/11 GI Bill

The cost of attendance is an estimate of the total amount it will cost you to go to school for an academic period, and it’s determined using rules established by law. Cost of attendance may include tuition and fees, on-campus room and board (or a housing and food allowance for off-campus students), and allowances for books, supplies, and transportation.
