How We Collect Data
Preqin’s in-house research team of over 450 multi-lingual analysts based around the world collect data through direct contact with industry professionals including fund managers, investors, and service providers. In addition to firms self-reporting information, Preqin’s research analysts also monitor regulatory filings, make FOIA requests, and track industry news sources on a daily basis. Collecting and cross-referencing information from multiple reporting sources ensures that Preqin’s data is accurate, up-to-date, comprehensive and exclusive.
