TITLE: Minors and Certificate Programs | Lundquist College of Business

The Lundquist College of Business is home to business administration, entrepreneurship, and sports business minors, as well a global business certificate program.
Many employers and graduate programs seek liberal arts or preprofessional majors who also have strong backgrounds in business. The Lundquist College has designed its minors to provide professional development for students of all majors. Business minors develop the knowledge and organizational skills necessary for effective leadership in their future professions.
It is possible to earn more than one minor within the Lundquist College of Business. However, for students earning more than one business-related minor, no more than three courses can be used to satisfy multiple business minors.
