Admissions Criteria
Prospective Freshmen Applicants
The primary consideration for admission is the secondary school academic record. In
order for your application to be reviewed by the Admissions Committee, you must:
Have completed Algebra, Biology and Chemistry.
Have a minimum grade point average (GPA) of 3.0.
The Duquesne University School of Nursing has waived the SAT/ACT requirement.
Learn more about test-optional admission
.
Applicants (and students) must meet the
Technical Standards for Admission
and the health requirements found in the
BSN Student Handbook
