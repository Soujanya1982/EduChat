7 Required Subjects
Each student must take an additional seven full subjects from the approved list of Restricted Electives. At least two of the electives must be in the area of
Core Electives in Finance
and there must be at least five electives in total between the areas of
Core Electives in Finance
and
Topics in Finance
.
*
A full subject is either 9 or 12 units. A half subject is 6 units.
