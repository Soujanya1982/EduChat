Application Process for High School Students
When you've decided Marian University is right for you, here are your next steps.
Apply online
for free, and
Upload your high school transcript to our applicant portal or have it sent to the address below:
Marian University
Office of Undergraduate Admission
3200 Cold Spring Road
Indianapolis, IN 46222-1997
Application due dates
To be considered for admission in the fall or spring semesters, submit your application to the Office of Undergraduate Admission by these dates.
Fall 2025 semester:
July 31, 2025
Spring 2026 semester:
December 2025
Admission decisions are made on a rolling basis. If your application is received after the due date, you will be considered for admission beginning with the next available semester.
You will be notified about the status of your admission by email.
