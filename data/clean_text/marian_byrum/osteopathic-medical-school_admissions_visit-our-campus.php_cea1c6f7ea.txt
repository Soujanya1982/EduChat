MU-WCOM Campus Visit Program
On Campus
The MU–WCOM admissions team invites you to join us for a tour of the Michael A. Evans Center for Health Sciences on our Indianapolis campus. During your visit, you’ll learn more about our Doctor of Osteopathic Medicine (DO) and Master of Science in Biomedical Sciences (BMS) programs.
Your campus visit will begin at 9 a.m. with a guided tour of the Evans Center, which may include the anatomy lab, the Osteopathic Manipulative Medicine lab, the Hill-Rom Simulation Center, classrooms, study spaces, and other key facilities.
Following the tour, our Financial Aid and admissions teams will cover each of their processes and discuss any next steps. There will also be time set aside at the end for individual questions.
Please note:
Campus visit agendas are subject to change. If you have already been accepted into the DO or BMS programs, please use the “Tour” tab in your admissions portal to schedule your visit.
Thanks, and we look forward to seeing you on campus!
