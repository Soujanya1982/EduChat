Undergraduate BBA
Academic
Advising
In this Section
An Academic
Support System
Prior to entering the BBA Program, you'll be supported by our team of pre-BBA advisors.  Upon entry to the BBA Program, you will be assigned an academic advisor with whom you will work closely with one-on-one for the duration of your time at Goizueta.  Our advisors go beyond ensuring that you are making satisfactory progress toward your BBA degree and that your course selection is appropriate for your educational and professional goals. Your advisor will provide encouragement, coaching, and assistance in taking advantage of the many resources and opportunities available through the Business School and University.
