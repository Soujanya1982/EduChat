TITLE: Finance Minor | RPI Lally School of Management

Program Overview
Dive into the world of finance and learn the key principles of financial accounting, financial decision-making, and more. This program is designed for Rensselaer students majoring in other fields.
Required Courses:
MGMT 2300 —
Financial Accounting
MGMT 2320 —
Managerial Finance
In addition to two of the following:
MGMT 4310 —
Financial Trading and Investing
MGMT 4320 —
Investments I
MGMT 4340 —
Advanced Corporate Finance
MGMT 4370 —
Risk Management
MGMT 4380 —
Derivatives Markets
Designated minor advisers can approve course substitutions to meet individual student needs.
