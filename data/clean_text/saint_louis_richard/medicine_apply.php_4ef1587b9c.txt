Applying to the M.D. Program
Saint Louis University is one of many medical schools participating in the American
Medical College Application Service (AMCAS). AMCAS offers a centralized online process
that can be used to apply to any of the participating medical schools using one set
of official academic transcripts. The AMCAS application will become available in the
spring. The deadline for submission of materials to AMCAS is December 15.

Apply for Graduate Education
The Saint Louis School of Medicine offers several Ph.D. and master’s level programs,
including M.S and Ph.D. degrees through our graduate program in biological sciences,
a Ph.D. and M.S. in anatomy, as well as an M.A. in family therapy.
All graduate applicants must complete Saint Louis University’s graduate application.
The biomedical sciences programs, which include biochemistry and molecular biology,
molecular microbiology and immunology, pathology and pharmacological and physiological
sciences, require a supplemental application.

Find a Residency or Fellowship
Many of SLU’s residency and fellowship programs use the Electronic Residency Application
Service and the National Resident Matching Program. Another resource in evaluating
GME programs is the American Medical Association Fellowship and Residency Electronic
Interactive Database Access System (AMA-FREIDA).
You may also search our residency and fellowship options by program name, department,
director or coordinator.
