Find Scholarships
As a freshman accepted to McCombs, you are automatically considered for a limited number of McCombs scholarships specifically for incoming freshmen. You will become eligible for more McCombs scholarships as you move from freshman through senior year. Most incoming freshman scholarships are awarded through the university. Scholarships from UT's alumni organization, Texas Exes, are also available.

Apply for Financial Aid
Each year UT Austin awards over $500 million dollars in financial aid. To be considered for grants, loans, and work-study positions offered by the University, you need to fill out the Free Application for Federal Student Aid. All need-based financial aid is awarded based on your FAFSA information, and we highly encourage all students to apply.
