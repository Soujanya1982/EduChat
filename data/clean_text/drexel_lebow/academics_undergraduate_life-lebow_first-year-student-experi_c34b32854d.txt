TITLE: Peer Leader Program | Drexel University's LeBow College of Business

The best way for incoming first-year students to learn the ropes is to learn from other students who have just been there themselves. LeBow first-year students are matched with exceptional sophomore students who are hand-picked for this role because of their leadership potential and strong academics. First-year students can seek advice of their peer leaders prior to the start of the school year and later become better acquainted during LeBow’s Welcome Week in the fall.
Throughout the first year, students meet with their peer leaders both formally and informally, many forging strong friendships and bonds throughout their career at LeBow and beyond.
