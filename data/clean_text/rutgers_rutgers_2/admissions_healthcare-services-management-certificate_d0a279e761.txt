Graduate Certificate in Healthcare Services Management
Our Graduate Certificate in Healthcare Services Management is a great alternative if you're not quite ready to pursue a full master's program.
By completing only four courses as a non-matriculated student, you can earn a Certificate in Healthcare Services Management from Rutgers Business School. These courses and credits will be counted towards the M.S. in Healthcare Analytics and Intelligence degree if you are later admitted to the degree program.
Four courses (12 credits out of 36) to earn a certificate:
Data Analytics and Decision Making
Operations Analysis
Healthcare Service Management
Choose one of the following:
Introduction to Project Management
Lean Six Sigma
Think Like an Executive
