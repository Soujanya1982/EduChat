Talent Award for Instrumental Performance
The Fine Arts Talent Award for Instrumental Performance is available to students with outstanding abilities who agree to be active participants in the Marian University Marching Band or Color Guard. This award is based on the level of commitment of the recipient and position openings with the instrumental program for the current season.
Eligibility
Applicants for the Fine Arts Talent Award for Instrumental Performance must:
Have completed the
Marian University admissions application process
and been admitted in good standing.
Participate in a live audition on campus.
Demonstrate outstanding ability and commitment through the audition process.
Commit to an educational experience framed within the context of Marian University’s Franciscan values of
dignity of the individual
,
peace and justice
,
reconciliation
, and
responsible stewardship
.
Application process
Applicants must complete the admissions process to Marian University.
Applicants must schedule a live audition. Contact Beverly Day at
bday@marian.edu
or (317) 955-6176 for more information.
Selection
Award recipients will be notified of their status in writing.
Renewal
The Fine Arts Talent Award for Instrumental Performance is renewable annually based on full-time enrollment and successful fulfillment of the requirements outlined in the Marian University Student Handbook for the Department of Music.
