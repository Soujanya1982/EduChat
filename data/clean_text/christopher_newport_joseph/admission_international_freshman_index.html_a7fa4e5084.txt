TITLE: Freshman - Christopher Newport University

At Christopher Newport you’ll learn the answers to two very important questions: What am I good at? and What do I love to do? Whatever your passions lead you to study – from engineering to leadership, from music to accounting – the liberal arts and sciences are the foundation of your academic experiences. Here we don’t just teach you to do something; we prepare you to do
anything
.

We have designed an undergraduate experience to instruct and inspire great leaders. Studying the liberal arts and sciences provides knowledge that will prepare you to take advantage of any opportunity that presents itself in today’s global marketplace. Through CNU’s rigorous curriculum you will learn how to think critically and communicate effectively while developing the tools and skills that will serve you for a lifetime.
