TITLE: What if I miss the deadline, my financial aid has not been approved, and I have to pay for my tuition to enroll in classes? - Naveen Jindal School of Management | The University of Texas at Dallas

Unfortunately, you will have to pay the tuition; however, when financial aid is approved, you will be reimbursed very quickly for the tuition you paid (assuming you qualify).
