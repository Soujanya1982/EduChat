Undergraduate Real Estate Minor Overview
The real estate minor is open to all undergraduate students at Lehigh University, irrespective of the student’s home college or department.  Real estate courses are taken in parallel with a student’s chosen academic major and, consistent with the multidisciplinary nature of the industry, integrates topics across a wide range of fields.
Program Overview
An update to the program structure and course content went into effect with the Fall 2019 semester. Students must complete 5 courses; 3 required and 2 elective.
