B.A. Wheaton College (MA)
Territory:
Bristol and Norfolk Counties; Lowell, Concord, and Wellesley, Massachusetts
Where are you from?
Billerica, MA
What’s your favorite event or RWU tradition?
My favorite event is the Senate Massage Bus! It’s an amazing way for students to unwind and recover.
What’s one restaurant you would recommend in Bristol?
The Beehive Café, it’s the perfect spot for a weekend brunch or a peaceful study break.
If you could give one piece of advice to a first-year student, what would it be?
Print out your syllabus at the beginning of the semester and mark all the important dates on a calendar, staying organized early makes a big difference. Also, try to find a study space outside of your dorm, like the library or a café. Your dorm should be a place where you relax, not where you feel extra pressure, so separating work and rest can really help manage stress.
Favorite music artist and TV show?
Noah Kahan is my favorite artist; I’ve seen him live 11 times and in 3 different countries! My favorite TV show is The Haunting of Hill House

Assistant Director, Admission Communication
B.A Westfield State University, M.A Southern New Hampshire University
Territory:
Cape Cod & The Islands and North Central Massachusetts
Where are you from?
Seekonk, MA (but now I’m a Bristol resident!)
What's your favorite event or RWU Tradition?
Spring Weekend! The weather is finally nice again, there is so much to do, and everyone is so excited!
What’s one restaurant you would recommend in Bristol?
For breakfast & coffee: The Beehive or Bristol Bagel! For lunch, dinner, and views: The Beach House or Thames Waterside Grill!
If you could give one piece of advice to a first-year student, what would it be?
As a homebody who had a very hard time adjusting to living away from home, give it a chance! My parents told me to try not to come home until Thanksgiving, and it was SO hard at first. But, the longer I stayed on campus, I made friends, joined clubs, and when Thanksgiving eventually rolled around, I actually ended up having a super hard time leaving! It’s going to be a big adjustment, but if you’re open and putting yourself out there, you’re going to find your home away from home! :)
Favorite music artist and TV show?
I’m a proud day 1 Swiftie, so Taylor is always gonna be my first answer. I also love Kelsea Ballerini, Megan Moroney, Sabrina Carpenter, Kacey Musgraves, the list goes on and on. I’m known for my SUPER specific playlists for every mood/occasion too. For shows, I love a good comfort sitcom… New Girl is the one I always go back to, and I’ll always rewatch Gilmore Girls in the fall. The Summer I Turned Pretty might also become a yearly summer rewatch- time will tell. I also love a good drama (Big Little Lies, Little Fires Everywhere, We Were Liars).
