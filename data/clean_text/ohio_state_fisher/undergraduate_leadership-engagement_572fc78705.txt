Leadership & Engagement
The Undergraduate Leadership & Engagement Office (ULEO) provides students impactful engagement opportunities to enhance leadership competencies, develop professional preparedness, connect with industry and strengthen the Fisher community.
ENGAGE
Learn and explore opportunities and resources for success.
DEDICATE
Enhance personal values, professional preparedness and leadership competencies.
EMERGE
Become involved in self-assessment and leadership opportunities.
LEAD
Continue contributing and giving back to the Fisher community.
