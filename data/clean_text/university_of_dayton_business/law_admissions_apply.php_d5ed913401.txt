TITLE: Apply : University of Dayton, Ohio

Application Requirements
On September 1, we begin accepting J.D. applications for the next academic year. You may apply to the University of Dayton School of Law while you're waiting for your LSAT or GRE score, or even before you've taken a test, though we will not review applications until we receive your scores and all the other required information.
