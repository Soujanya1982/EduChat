TITLE: Marketing, Minor | RPI Lally School of Management

Program Overview
Complement your studies in engineering, science, or the humanities with a minor that provides an inside look at the key principles of marketing. This program is designed for Rensselaer students majoring in other fields.
Required Courses:
MGMT 1100 —
Introduction to Management
MGMT 2430 —
Marketing Principles
MGMT 4470 —
Marketing Research
In addition to one of the following:
MGMT 4460 —
Consumer Behavior and Product Design
MGMT 4490 —
Advertising Strategy and Promotions
Designated minor advisers can approve course substitutions to meet individual student needs.
