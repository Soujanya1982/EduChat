Visit Us
Plan Your Binghamton Visit
in Person or Online
Schedule a campus tour, join a live virtual session, or explore campus from anywhere.
In-person Tours
One of our student tour guides will take you on a guided campus tour and share their
firsthand experiences. If you sign up for a Welcome Session and Tour, your visit starts
with an information session hosted by an admissions counselor, covering academics,
campus life, and what to expect during the application process.
Sign up for a Campus Tour
Virtual Campus Tours
Take a guided online tour of Binghamton University and explore campus highlights,
academic buildings, and student spaces—anytime, from anywhere.
LAUNCH A VIRTUAL TOUR
Live Virtual Sessions
Discover Binghamton University through live virtual sessions designed for prospective
students and families. Feel free to register for more than one session to learn about
the programs, resources, and opportunities that interest you most.
SIGN UP FOR A VIRTUAL SESSION
Explore Campus with a Self-Guided Tour
Explore Binghamton University on your own schedule. When you arrive, stop by the Admissions
Center to scan a QR code and pick up a map to guide your visit.
Planning a Group Visit?
Schedule a campus visit for groups of 15–50 prospective students and their chaperones.
Our group tours provide an overview of academics, campus life, and the admissions
process.
