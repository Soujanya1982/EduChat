The School of Hospitality Business
Pause
Become a leader in the hospitality industry.
The School of Hospitality Business was established in 1927, and in 1944 became the first hospitality program to be housed in a collegiate school of business.
The School of Hospitality Business prepares students for success in a variety of roles across the multi-trillion dollar global industry.
Our renowned and innovative faculty members offer a mix of theory and practical application. Our students earn a highly versatile degree with rigorous and relevant curriculum.
School Information Sheet
Choose Our School
Located at the heart of the beautiful MSU campus is an independent, industry-specific school in the Eli Broad College of Business.
Hospitality business students experience top-rated faculty, an excellent hospitality business foundation, and numerous opportunities to explore career paths.
Recruiters from the nation’s leading hospitality companies return year after year, seeking interns and graduates for hundreds of different types of positions.
Hospitality Business students were awarded  over $600,000 in
merit-based and competitive scholarships
this year.

Admissions
If you are a MSU freshman or a sophomore, you may declare hospitality business as a major. There is a formal online admission process. Enrollments are limited, and admission is competitive.

Curriculum
The hospitality industry offers a wide range of career opportunities in management and entrepreneurship.
The School of Hospitality Business prepares its students to enter management positions in lodging, food and beverage service, consulting, event planning, human resources, sales, information systems, marketing and real estate investment management.
Graduates find opportunities in venues such as hotels, restaurants, resorts, casinos, cruise lines and clubs, as well as in the world of sports and entertainment.
A business-focused curriculum, driven by the hospitality industry.
Two paid
internships
are required as part of the undergraduate program experience.

Education Abroad for Hospitality Business Students
Your customers will come from and travel to every corner of the world.
Education abroad provides you with an excellent and exciting way to build your professional skills while discovering new approaches to customer satisfaction.
Hospitality business students can study abroad at any time during their academic careers. Programs come in a variety of time frames and formats, from a few weeks to a complete academic year.
Scholarships are available
for hospitality business students studying abroad. Start investigating your options and plan early.

The mission of the Hospitality Association is to create and refine hospitality business leaders by providing forums for academic growth, industry experience, leadership roles and career-enhancing opportunities and by encouraging social interaction and the development of student unity. HA is the umbrella organization for the eight clubs, each giving students the chance to learn more about the industry, examine their career choices, develop their leadership skills, and make good friends and important contacts.
