Paying Your Bill - Undergraduate Students
There are several options available for making payments on your account.
One-Time Payments
Lipscomb University uses the TouchNet system, which may be accessed through
myLipscomb
, to manage student accounts, which can be accessed through
myLipscomb
. Once you’re enrolled and ready to make a payment, you can make a one-time payment or sign up for a payment plan using TouchNet.
If you do not make complete payment or set up a payment plan prior to the deadline for each semester, you are at risk of having your schedule dropped. Following are the deadlines for each semester by which you must have either paid in full or set up a payment plan:

Any past due amounts are subject to a $40 late payment fee. Any returned payments are subject to a $30 returned payment fee.
How to Pay Your Bill Using TouchNet
Accessing TouchNet
Once you’re logged into
myLipscomb’s new student homepage
, navigate to the left hand corner of the page and click All Apps > CNS Student Portal.  There you can view My Finances > TouchNet Payment/Plans > Launch TouchNet
Authorizing Users
Authorized users may view student account activity, make payments and set up payment plans.
To give a parent, grandparent, or other trusted person access to TouchNet, visit the CNS Student Portal by clicking All Apps > CNS Student Portal. There you can view My Finances > TouchNet Payment/Plans > Launch TouchNet. On the Homepage of the TouchNet portal, click Add an Authorized Party, fill out the information for your authorized party and click Save.
Making Payments
Once you are logged into the CNS Student Portal, click on the My Finances tab > TouchNet Payment/Plans > Click the Launch TouchNet link under TouchNet Payment/Plans (pop up blocker may have to be disabled) > Click the Make a Payment Button>Select the appropriate Student Account Balance Term Box > Type in your payment amount>Next-Payment Method > Choose your payment method > Submit your payment
Enrolling in a Plan
You may only enroll in a payment plan during the enrollment period.  To enroll in a payment plan, log into the CNS Student Portal, click on the My Finances tab > TouchNet Payment/Plans > Click the Launch TouchNet link under TouchNet Payment/Plans (pop up blocker may have to be disabled) > Set up a Payment Plan under the appropriate term. Once you select your desired payment plan, you can enter your payment method and information to initiate automatic monthly deductions.
Payments from a 529 Plan
A 529 plan is a tax-advantaged savings plan, formally known as a "Qualified Tuition Program," designed to help families save for future education expenses, including college, graduate school, and even K-12 tuition.
When families request payments through a
529 plan’s website,
rather than using Backpack,
the funds are typically mailed as paper checks to the school.
Checks can introduce delays, errors, and added manual work for both families and university staff. Studies show that
nearly 1 in 3 families using paper checks for tuition will face a payment issue at some point.
To avoid any issues,
we ask that all families using a 529 plan use Backpack
to submit their tuition payments electronically.
