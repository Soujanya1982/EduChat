Grades
Credit hours earned at the University of Texas at Austin will count toward your GPA.
Courses transferred from another institution
will not
be included.
Students
must
maintain an overall GPA of
at least 2.0
to continue their academic work.
Graduating students
must
have an
overall
GPA of
at least 2.0
and a
Business
GPA of
at least 2.0
.
Calculate your GPA
To receive credit for a course, undergraduate students
must
earn a grade of
at least D-
. Academic departments may require a higher grade for the course to count toward the student’s degree. Additionally, prerequisite courses may require a higher grade in order to move on to the next stage in coursework.
Be sure to check the course schedule for prerequisites and contact your advisor if you have concerns about your grades.
See the Registrar's listing of grade points
Students can
review their grade report online
through the Office of the Registrar. Students can also
order official or unofficial transcripts online
or by visiting the Registrar’s Office on-campus.
