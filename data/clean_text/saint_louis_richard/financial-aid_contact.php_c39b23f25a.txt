Schedule a Virtual Financial Aid Appointment
SLU's Office of Student Financial Services offers the option to schedule a virtual
financial aid meeting using EAB Navigate Student.
Please Note: Incoming students should schedule a financial aid meeting with their
admission counselor
.
On Mobile:
Download Navigate Student App via Google Play or the Apple App store. Search for
"Navigate Student." Once the app is installed, search for "Saint Louis University"
and use your SLU login credentials to get started.
On Desktop:
You can use the desktop version by clicking on the EAB Navigate icon in your
mySLU
Apps, or go to
https://slu.navigate.eab.com
.
Learn More About Navigate Student
Steps to Create an Appointment
On your Navigate home page, select “Schedule an appointment.”
Select “Student Financial Services” as Appointment Category.
Choose a reason for your appointment (e.g. “Discuss Financial Aid Award”) and click
“Next.”
Select your preferred location (e.g. “Virtual”) and click “Next.”
Choose a date and time from the calendar availability and click “Next.”
On the final page, ensure that appointment information is correct, add comments if
you’d like, and select the reminder methods you prefer: email and/or text. Be sure
to click “Confirm Appointment.”
