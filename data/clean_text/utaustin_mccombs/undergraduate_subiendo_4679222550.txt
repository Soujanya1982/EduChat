Subiendo
SUBIENDO ACADEMY
Subiendo means “climbing” or “going up," and refers to the next generation of rising leaders who will address the challenges of our state and nation.
TOMORROW'S PROBLEM SOLVERS WANTED
Subiendo is a five-day summer leadership program designed to prepare young leaders in addressing the needs of the next generation by problem-solving issues in healthcare, energy/environment, and education. Current Texas high school juniors may apply for this unique opportunity to address real world challenges with today's top business, political, and educational leaders. There is no cost to attend.
Important Dates
The application for the 2026 Subiendo Academy will open November 1, 2025. The Academy will be hosted in person June 14–18, 2026.

The Subiendo Student Experience
Gain leadership skills through training and team building exercises
Opportunities to network with prominent business and political figures

College life information, including on-campus living in the residence halls
Mentoring and guidance from accomplished community and student leaders
Access to a lifelong network of Subiendo alumni, friends and supporters
Mission & Vision
Mission
: Subiendo seeks to empower the next generation of Texas leaders and encourage them to pursue higher education and civic engagement in critical fields.
Vision
: The Academy will encourage the rising generation to believe in their potential and aspire to lead.
