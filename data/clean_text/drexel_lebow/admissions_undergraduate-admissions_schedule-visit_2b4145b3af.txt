Virtual Visit Sign Up
Interested in learning more about Drexel from anywhere? Whether you are located close to the Philadelphia area, across the country, or around the globe, our virtual information sessions and tours are where you can get more information regarding Drexel’s academics, cooperative education program, and student life; our home in Philadelphia; and the application process.
To register for a virtual information session, please select a date from the calendar. After registering, you’ll receive a confirmation email with the Zoom link to join the virtual information session.
LeBow virtual information sessions are hosted once per month.
