TITLE: I am an international student and have taken the GMAT. Can I apply for a scholarship at the start of my degree? - Naveen Jindal School of Management | The University of Texas at Dallas

Yes. You are eligible for scholarships as soon as your degree starts. If you are provided with any scholarship, it will be mentioned in your
Form I-20
as well.
