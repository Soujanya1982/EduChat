Earn Your MBA in Artificial Intelligence & Advanced Computing – A STEM Graduate Degree
The STEM-designated MBA in Artificial Intelligence and Advanced Computing at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute’s Lally School of Management prepares you to lead in the age of AI, big data, and intelligent systems. Whether you’re pursuing a role in AI product management, digital transformation, or data-driven innovation, this program combines core business administration with hands-on exposure to machine learning, cloud computing, and generative AI applications.
Designed for aspiring AI leaders, product strategists, and analytics professionals, the Lally AI MBA offers a competitive edge in today’s high-growth tech economy.

Frequently Asked Questions – MBA with a concentration in Artificial Intelligence
What is the MBA in Artificial Intelligence at RPI?
It is a STEM-designated MBA that integrates business strategy with artificial intelligence, analytics, and computing. Graduates are prepared to lead innovation in AI-driven organizations.
Is the program STEM-designated?
Yes. International students are eligible for a 24-month OPT extension after graduation.
How long does the program take to complete?
The program is 36 credits and is designed to be completed over three semesters.
What careers can I pursue after graduation?
Graduates pursue roles in AI product management, analytics, consulting, digital transformation, and innovation leadership.
