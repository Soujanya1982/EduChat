TITLE: Finance, Markets, and Emerging Technologies (STEM-designated) B.S.

Earn Your BS in Finance, Markets, and Emerging Technologies – A STEM Undergraduate Degree
Explore the future of finance with a bachelor’s degree that blends global markets, financial modeling, and cutting-edge technologies. At Rensselaer’s Lally School of Management, our STEM-designated B.S. in Finance, Markets, and Emerging Technologies prepares students to lead in the digital, data-driven economy. With hands-on experience and a tech-forward curriculum, you'll graduate ready for careers in corporate finance, fintech, banking, and beyond.
Why Choose a Finance Bachelor’s Degree?
Finance is evolving rapidly and, today’s professionals must do more than analyze numbers. They need to understand blockchain, algorithmic trading, sustainability metrics, and the technologies reshaping capital markets.
At Lally, our finance and emerging technologies degree gives you an edge with:
A STEM-designated finance program—ideal for both U.S. and international students
A unique focus on markets, technology, and innovation
Direct experience with financial modeling, risk management, and data analytics
Access to Bloomberg terminals and financial simulation labs
Whether you’re interested in investment banking, fintech startups, or corporate strategy, this degree helps you stay ahead in a competitive field.

Hands-On Learning – Solve Real Financial Challenges
At Lally, finance is not just theory; it’s applied learning from day one. You’ll learn to solve complex financial problems, develop data-backed strategies, and evaluate risk in fast-paced, real-world scenarios.
Real‑World Learning & Student‑Managed Fund
Join the Rensselaer Finance Club and take part in managing real capital through the James Student‑Managed Investment Fund. You’ll gain direct experience making investment decisions, analyzing portfolio performance, and presenting to industry professionals. This hands-on opportunity simulates real asset management and sets you apart in the job market.
Other experiential opportunities include:
Investment and trading simulations
Projects using Bloomberg, Tableau, and financial modeling tools
Finance case competitions and pitch presentations
Research in financial technology, AI in markets, and behavioral finance
Internships at firms like Goldman Sachs, Bank of America, EY, and emerging fintech companies
These experiences help you graduate with confidence, credentials, and a strong network of peers and professionals in the finance industry.

FAQs - Finance, Markets, and Emerging Technologies, B.S.
Is this Finance degree STEM-designated?
Yes. This program is officially STEM-designated, combining finance, analytics, and emerging technologies.
Do students have access to Bloomberg Terminals and financial software?
Yes! Lally students work directly with tools like Bloomberg, Tableau, and Excel to develop real-world finance skills.
Is this program available online?
No, Lally School degrees are not available online. All of our programs are offered in person on the Troy, NY campus.
What is the difference between this and a traditional finance degree?
This degree emphasizes both traditional finance principles and emerging technologies like fintech, blockchain, and algorithmic trading, giving you a competitive edge in today’s market.
Can I pursue graduate study after this degree?
Yes! Many students continue on to earn an MBA or M.S. in Business Analytics at RPI or other top institutions.
What support is available for internships and job placement?
RPI’s Center for Career and Professional Development offers personalized advising, résumé help, career fairs, and employer networking. Plus, our faculty and alumni often help connect students to internship and job opportunities.
