TITLE: Veteran's Services | Ithaca College

Use your VA Benefits at Ithaca College
The Office of Student Financial Services collaborates with Veteran's Services within the Office of the Registrar to activate the use of VA benefits towards education expenses.
All VA Eligible Students
Review the
Benefit Programs
and apply for qualifying program through the U.S. Dept. of Veterans Affairs
Complete a Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) on
www.studentaid.gov
Submit a Certification of Eligibility form to Holley Westbrook in Veteran's Services
Complete a
Benefit Request Form
The Office of Student Financial Services will move forward with the adjustments or creation of a financial aid package including benefit amounts for the academic year.
Read
Important Information
regarding the student responsibilities when using VA benefits.
