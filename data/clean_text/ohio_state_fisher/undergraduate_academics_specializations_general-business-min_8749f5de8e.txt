Business
The Business Minor provides non-business majors with a foundational understanding of key business disciplines. Through five required courses—one each in accounting, finance, management and human resources, marketing, and operations—students gain exposure to core business concepts that are applicable across industries and sectors.
This program is open only to students who are not pursuing a business major or pre-major and is intended to supplement, not overlap with, their primary field of study.
No application is required.
Once all coursework is completed, students follow their college’s minor declaration process. No approval from the Fisher College of Business is needed.
This program was developed with financial support from the Ford Motor Company.
Curriculum Requirements
Prerequisites
Students must complete the following before enrolling in any Business Minor coursework:
College Algebra:
MATH 1130, 1148 or equivalent; or Math Placement Level M or higher
Microeconomics:
ECON 2001.01 or equivalent
Accounting Pre-req:
ACCTMIS 2000 must be completed before taking BUSFIN 3120
Required Courses (15 Credit Hours)
All five courses below must be completed (3 credit hours each). Variations or substitutions are rarely approved and must be petitioned through the Fisher Undergraduate Programs Petition Committee.
Course
Title
Description
ACCTMIS 2000

Introduction to operations management in both manufacturing and service sectors, including supply chain and purchasing.
BUSML 3150
Foundations of Marketing
Explores key marketing principles and how they apply in for-profit and nonprofit contexts.
Frequently Asked Questions
Who can enroll in the Business Minor?
Only non-business majors are eligible. Business majors or pre-business students are not permitted to enroll.
Do I need approval from Fisher to declare the minor?
No. Students declare the minor through their college once coursework is completed. Fisher does not require an application or formal approval.
Can minor courses count toward my major or other minors?
No. Courses used for the Business Minor cannot be used for any major or other minor.
Can I use transfer credit for the Business Minor?
Yes, but no more than
6 credit hours
of transfer coursework may be applied.
What grades are required for the minor courses?
You must earn
a minimum of C-
in each course.
You must maintain a
minimum 2.00 GPA
across all minor courses.
Pass/Non-Pass grading is not allowed.
Can I request substitutions or exceptions to the required courses?
Not typically. Any variations must be formally approved by the Fisher Undergraduate Programs Petition Committee.
