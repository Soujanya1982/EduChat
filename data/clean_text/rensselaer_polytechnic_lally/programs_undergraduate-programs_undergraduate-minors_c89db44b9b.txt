Business Minors at RPI
Complement your major with a Lally business minor. Minors are open to all Rensselaer students and help you build career-ready skills in entrepreneurship, finance, management, and marketing.
Entrepreneurship:
Learn how to launch and scale ventures, from ideation to funding.
Finance:
Strengthen your understanding of investments, valuation, and corporate finance.
Management:
Develop leadership, organizational, and strategy skills.
Marketing:
Explore consumer behavior, branding, and digital marketing analytics.
