Graduate Degree Options
One-Year MBA
The full MBA experience condensed into one intense year...this program is ideal for students continuing on existing career paths and who don’t need an internship to meet their goals or for sponsored and entrepreneurial-focused students.
Program Eligibility
At least two years of post-undergraduate, full-time work experience (unless applying to a joint degree program)
A bachelor's degree and have a strong undergraduate academic record
Competitive scores on the GMAT or GRE and, for non-native English speakers, the TOEFL, PTE, or IETLS
Clearly defined post-MBA career goals that do not require a summer internship
Demonstrated the ability to excel, lead, learn, and grow
Application Deadlines & Process
Two-Year MBA
A top-ranked, highly experiential MBA with proven career outcomes...this program is valuable for career changers or those transitioning into industries that hire from their internship pool.
Program Eligibility
At least two years of post-undergraduate, full-time work experience (unless applying to a joint degree program)
A bachelor's degree and have a strong undergraduate academic record
Competitive scores on the GMAT or GRE and, for non-native English speakers, the TOEFL, PTE, or IETLS
Clearly defined post-MBA career goals
Demonstrated the ability to excel, lead, learn, and grow
Application Deadlines & Process
Evening MBA
A top-ranked MBA with weekday evening classes on a flexible schedule structured specifically for working professionals, you can adjust your quantity, timing, and format (online, hybrid, & on-campus) of courses to match your professional goals and personal life. This program is ideal for working professionals looking to accelerate their career growth or pivot to a new career path.
Program Eligibility
Current full-time employment and for the duration of the program
Several years of post-undergraduate, full-time work experience with demonstrated leadership abilities
A bachelor's degree with a strong undergraduate academic record
Solid quantitative and analytical foundations and strong written and verbal communication skills
Demonstrated the ability to excel, lead, learn, and grow
Application Deadlines & Process
Executive MBA
Designed for emerging executives and accomplished professionals, you'll take classes on weekends to accommodate your busy professional schedule and can complete the program on-campus, hybrid, or fully online. This program is ideal for seasoned and senior-level professionals in or entering senior leadership or wanting to shift industries.
Program Eligibility
Current full-time employment and for the duration of the program
Mid- to senior-level professional experience and readiness to move into a more strategic role
Managerial or project management experience
A bachelor's degree and a strong undergraduate academic record
Demonstrated the ability to excel, lead, learn, and grow
Application Deadlines & Process
Master of Finance
Created by finance industry insiders to develop next gen finance professionals, this full-time finance degree delivers unmatched experience and industry know-how through our unique analyst immersion and Finance Lab. Complete the program in 10 months or extend it to 16 months and gain more experience and depth of knowledge by completing a summer internship and three additional electives. It is ideal for students looking to move into or accelerate a career in the finance industry
Program Eligibility
A bachelor's degree in any discipline with excellent quantitative and qualitative analysis skills
Analytical proficiency demonstrated by coursework, internship, or test scores
A high degree of enthusiasm for finance industry – investment management, trading, banking or FinTech innovation
0-2 years of professional finance experience (including internships) or 0-8 years of professional experience in other fields
Strong working knowledge and understanding of Microsoft Excel
Prior knowledge of finance or economics is not expected or require
Experience with programming languages is also not required, but is a plus
Application Deadlines & Process
MS in Business Analytics
A full-time, 10-month MS in Business Analytics that combines business, data, and technology to make you an effective business data scientist for a data-driven world. Choose from tracks in AI in Business, Business Analytics, Marketing Analytics, and Supply Chain Analytics. Complete the program in 10 months or extend it to 16 months and gain more experience and depth of knowledge by completing a summer internship and three additional electives. Ideal for students looking to launch a business analytics career.
Program Eligibility
• A bachelor's degree in business, engineering, computer science, math/statistics, or economics
• Little to no professional experience in the business analytics field
• A solid aptitude for business management
• A background that includes quantitative and/or technical training
• Taken at least calculus I, calculus II, and statistics
• Exhibited a strong interest in working in a data/analytics related field
• Strong communication and interpersonal skills and the ability to work in teams
Application Deadlines & Process
Master in Management
This 10-month, full-time program is ideal for students with little or no work experience and no prior business education who are looking to leverage existing skills and passions in a professional environment.
Program Eligibility
A bachelor's degree/equivalent in any liberal arts or science major from an accredited college or university that is conferred prior to beginning Master in Management courses
Those with a major, minor, or significant coursework in business will not be considered
Strong academic performance with the expectation of a B average or better
No more than one year of full-time, professional work experience
Extracurricular involvement or other evidence of engagement in university community
Application Deadlines & Process
Master in Business for Veterans
Complete this degree in 11 months of on-campus classes every other weekend (Fridays & Saturdays) while you continue to work. This program is ideal for military veterans looking to transition or leverage their military and leadership experience to succeed in a business career or wanting to launch and sustain their own business.
Program Eligibility
Active or previous military service (military veterans, active duty, National Guard, and Reserve Personnel)
3 years of military service and leadership experience
A bachelor's degree
Demonstrated the ability to excel, lead, learn, and grow
Application Deadlines & Process
