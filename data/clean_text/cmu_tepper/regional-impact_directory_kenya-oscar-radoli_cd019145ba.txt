Collaboration Interests
My mission is to nurture incredibly diverse youthful talent in Pittsburgh's technology space by increasing student access to industry technology workshops, coding boot camps, and in-demand certifications.
I am available to assist with defining and developing project charters and plans. I can also provide media marketing for colleagues' community events on my weekly Tembo Sounds The Culture radio show on WRCT 88.3 FM. The show celebrates the diverse music cultures enjoyed in Pittsburgh and its surroundings. I also interview community members working to improve Pittsburgh and its neighboring communities.
