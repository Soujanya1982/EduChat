Welcome to the University of Evansville!
One of our main values here is inclusive community, and your presence here as an international student will be celebrated!
As your international admission counselors, we want your application and decision to come to UE to be as easy as possible. You can read more about our admission requirements and then apply right here on our website. We will review your application materials for admission and scholarships at the same time!
We are eager to show you what makes UE a special place to live and learn! Hopefully, you will find we are the right fit for your education in the United States. If you have any questions at all throughout the process, don't hesitate to reach out.
