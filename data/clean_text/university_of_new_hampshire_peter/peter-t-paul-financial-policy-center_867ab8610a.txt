Peter T. Paul Financial Policy Center
A prominent center that seeks to promote financial policy research and education.
The Peter T. Paul Financial Policy Center aims to:
Serve as a forum for discussing and investigating critical financial policy issues
Sponsor cutting-edge research that advances the general understanding of key financial issues
Improve the finance curriculum at UNH and diversify course offerings to provide Paul finance students with a state-of-the-art education
Mobilize funding to expand Paul College’s strategic alliance with industry and policy organizations, domestically and internationally
Broaden the exposure of our students to the leading academics and practitioners in finance
Strive to expand  opportunities for students to gain real experience in the corporate and investing areas
Attract leading academics to engage in collaborative exchange of ideas with faculty and students
Enhance the visibility of Paul College, domestically and internationally
