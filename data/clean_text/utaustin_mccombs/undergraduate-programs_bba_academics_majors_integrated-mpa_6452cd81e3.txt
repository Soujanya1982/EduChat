Grab Life By the Horns and Study Accounting
The integrated Master in Professional Accounting program is a five-year undergraduate/graduate program that allows you to earn both a Bachelor in Business Administration and a Master in Professional Accounting.
Explore iMPA
Join the MPA Family
Apply to the iMPA program at the end of your sophomore year and earn your graduate degree from the top-ranked accounting program in the nation. Students planning on pursuing the iMPA will follow the Accounting four-year plan during their first two years before admission into the program.

See What You'll Learn
iMPA students learn from leading faculty and gain in-demand skills, including: How to prepare and examine financial records, financial analysis techniques, profound knowledge of business transactions, how to present financial statements to assist in decision making, how to ensure that financial statements comply with laws/regulations, tax return preparation, and much more.
