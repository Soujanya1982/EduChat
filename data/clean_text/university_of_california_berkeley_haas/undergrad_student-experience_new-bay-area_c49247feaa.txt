TITLE: San Francisco Bay Area At the Center of Innovation - Undergraduate Program - UC Berkeley Haas

The San Francisco Bay Area is home to companies that are redefining industries and driving global economic growth. When you study at
Berkeley Haas: The Heart of What’s Next
™, you’ll find that opportunities for growth and development are paramount.
A diverse range of industries converge here, from venture capital and digital media to biotech and clean energy. Many of these companies cultivate close relationships with Berkeley Haas and our students to sponsor student-organized events, judge case competitions, provide experiential learning, and offer internships and career development opportunities.
Being a part of a Bay Area program means learning skills that meet the demands of innovation economy employers, while living surrounded by natural beauty and enjoying temperate weather. World-class theater, film, dance, museums, and music are at your fingertips, in addition to easily accessible destinations such as Yosemite, Lake Tahoe, Wine Country, the Monterey Peninsula and 1,200 miles of park trails.
