Undergraduate Office Contacts
The Undergraduate Office is the hub for undergraduate student support in the College of Business. We are the home-base for advising, academic administration, business clubs, and Student to Professional, the Lehigh Business professional development co-curriculum.
We work together as a team within the College and across other Lehigh offices to develop students' planning, communication, and professional skills in ways that complement their academic program and prepare them for future leadership and global impact.
Our passion is to connect every business student to the services and programs he or she needs to get the most from Lehigh and launch to a successful career after graduation.
Location
The College of Business Undergraduate Office is located on the 3rd floor of the Rauch Business Center. We are open from 8:15 a.m. to 4:45 p.m., Monday through Friday. Common forms and other academic programming information are available online via the
