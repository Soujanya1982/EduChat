A Note to Future Applicants
It’s impossible to truly quantify the vibrancy and incredible breadth of backgrounds, experiences, and interests of our community in numbers. The below percentages in no way translate into your individual “chances” of being admitted to Penn.
We are, above all, interested in who
you
are, and the many things that make you unique — something these high-level statistics could never capture!

Activities
Our incoming Quakers participated in a broad range of activities prior to joining Penn. When reviewing applicants, our process is grounded in assessing each individual’s pursuit of knowledge, contribution, and purpose.
