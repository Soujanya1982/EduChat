Uptown Location and Parking
The Freeman School is located at 7 McAlister Drive in the Goldring/Woldenberg Business Complex (GWBC) on Tulane’s Uptown New Orleans campus.  Goldring/Woldenberg Business Complex is the second building on the right from the corner of Freret Street, across from the Lavin-Bernick Center, number 39 on the
Interactive Campus Map
.
*NOTE: McAlister Dr. between Freret St. and Drill Rd. is a pedestrian walkway and is not vehicle accessible.
Guest parking is available at meters (45 minute maximum time limit) on McAlister Drive. Extended parking is available at meters in the Diboll Parking Garage on McAlister Extension near the Reily Center. Permits for parking on McAlister Drive, in the Diboll Garage and elsewhere on the Uptown campus are available through
Tulane Parking Services online
, the ParkMobile app or at the Campus Services office on the first floor of the Lavin-Bernick Center.
