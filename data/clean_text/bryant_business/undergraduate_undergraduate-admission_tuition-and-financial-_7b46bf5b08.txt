Official Cost of Attendance
The U.S. Department of Education requires colleges and universities participating in Title IV Federal Student Aid Programs to disclose the official Cost of Attendance reflecting a more comprehensive cost of attending for one full academic year. The Cost of Attendance includes not only direct/billed costs like (1) tuition and fees, but also includes estimates/allowances for (2) housing and food, (3) books and supplies, (4) transportation, and (5) miscellaneous personal expenses.
