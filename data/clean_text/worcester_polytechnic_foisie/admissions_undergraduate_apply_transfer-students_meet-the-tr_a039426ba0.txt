Meet the Transfer Admissions Team
The WPI Transfer team is here to support you throughout your transfer journey! Our Admissions team will guide you as you explore academic programs, navigate transfer credits, and look to find your community. Whether you’re planning your first visit to WPI or you’re ready to apply, we look forward to connecting with you! If you have any questions, please reach out to
transfer@wpi.edu
and our staff will be happy to assist you!
The Admissions team works closely with our Office of Academic Advising to provide personalized guidance for incoming transfer students. Prospective transfers are encouraged to
schedule a Transfer Tuesday appointment
with an Admissions counselor to learn more about WPI. Sessions are offered virtually on Tuesday afternoons in the fall. Admitted and enrolling students should
schedule an advising appointment
with a transfer academic advisor for support in transitioning to WPI academics.
