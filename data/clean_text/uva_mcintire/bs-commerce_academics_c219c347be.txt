Foundational Core
Foundational Core
The Foundational Core (15 credits) is designed to ensure that all McIntire students build a strong base of analytical, communication, and decision-making skills at the start of their Commerce education. Integrating key business fundamentals in accounting, finance, data analysis, communication, and technology, this sequenced, skills-building progression across the second year ensures that every student is well-prepared for the integrated, team-based consulting projects in their third year.
Integrated Core
Integrated Core
This immersive curriculum during your third year combines all the core disciplines for a wide-ranging, hands-on approach to business education. Taught by a dedicated faculty team, small student groups collaborate to solve real-world business problems that they present to corporate sponsor executives.

Concentrations
Concentrations
By the spring of your third year, you’ll have the broad-based liberal arts foundation and applied business knowledge that will help you choose your concentration or functional area of study. You’ll concentrate on one or two of these areas to hone your skill set and fine-tune your professional goals.
