Transfer Scholarships
Marian University offers scholarships for entering transfer students who have completed at least 12 college-level credit hours at a regionally accredited institution.
These scholarships are available for transfer students entering in the spring or fall semesters.
Transfer student scholarships are granted annually. They are renewable for full-time students who maintain a Marian University GPA of 3.00 or higher.
If you receive an offer of financial aid, any scholarships you are awarded will be indicated in a financial aid award offer.
Eligible applicants receive one scholarship that best fits their achievement.
