Earn Your BS in Business Analytics – A STEM Undergraduate Degree
Prepare for a career at the intersection of business strategy and data science with a Bachelor of Science in Business Analytics from the Lally School of Management at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute. This undergraduate BS in business analytics program is STEM-designated and equips students with both analytical tools and strategic thinking for data-driven decision-making.

FAQs - Business Analytics, B.S.
Is the Business Analytics at RPI STEM-Designated?
Yes, it is STEM-designated, which provides advantages such as extended OPT for international students and a focus on quantitative and technical skills.
What kind of internship opportunities are available?
Students can access internships through RPI's employer network and career services office. Internships often lead to full-time job offers in analytics and consulting roles.
Can I combine the Business Analytics major with another area of study?
Yes! Many students pair it with Computer Science, Finance, or ITWS for a custom interdisciplinary academic experience.
What are the benefits of studying Business Analytics at RPI?
You gain a strong business foundation, real-world project experience, and access to leading employers—all on a technology-focused campus.
