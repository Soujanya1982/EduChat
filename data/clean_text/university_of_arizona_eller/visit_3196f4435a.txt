Planning Your Visit
Eller College Tours
Eller College virtual sessions are held on Tuesdays at 4:00pm Arizona time and focus on our new admissions process, majors, minors, courses, and academic resources available to Eller students. Fridays at 12:30pm we offer in-person building tours led by our Eller Ambassadors, who are current students. Sign-ups for tours may be limited.
Sign up for an Eller Virtual OR IN PERSON EVENT
Take a self-guided virtual Eller tour. Explore McClelland Hall, which houses the Karl and Stevie Eller Professional Development Center, academic advisors, career coaches, global programs, and events and marketing team. Learn more about what Eller undergraduate majors have to offer including the kinds of student organizations, global programs, classes, internships, and career outcomes with each major.
TAKE a virtual self-guided eller tour
If you have questions about these sessions or wish to connect with us, please contact us at
ugrad@eller.arizona.edu
to set up an appointment. Tours are available in person and are available on a first come, first serve basis. Masks are not required in McClelland Hall but if you would like one, we are happy to provide you one!

Directions
From Tucson International Airport
From Tucson International Airport, head north on Tucson Boulevard. Tucson Boulevard begins to turn to the northwest and becomes Benson Highway. As the road begins to curve, watch for a sign that says "Kino Parkway - City Center." Turn right onto Kino Parkway. Kino will eventually become Campbell Avenue. Continue north on Campbell. Take a left on Speedway Boulevard. Take a right on Park Avenue, and park in the parking garage (fee).
From Phoenix
From Phoenix, take I-10 east to Tucson. Exit at at The University of Arizona/Speedway Blvd (exit #257). Head east on approximately two miles. Take a left at Park Avenue, and park in the parking garage (fee).
