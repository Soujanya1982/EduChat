A great way to learn more about the Goizueta experience is by talking to a current student. In addition to our online  and on-campus events, you can also request to chat 1:1 with a student ambassador. Inquire about classes, student life, living in Atlanta, the application process, and much more! If they aren't an expert in your question, our small-by-design community enables them to quickly connect you to someone in the program who is.
