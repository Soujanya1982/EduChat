FAQs MBA in Marketing Analytics
What is the MBA in Marketing Analytics at RPI’s Lally School?
This STEM-designated MBA program combines marketing strategy with AI, big data, and digital tools to prepare students for leadership in data-driven marketing, e-commerce, and digital transformation. This is a concentration in Marketing Analytics.
Is the Marketing Analytics MBA a STEM program?
Yes. Rensselaer’s MBA in Marketing Analytics is STEM-designated, making international students eligible for a 24-month OPT extension in the U.S. after graduation.
How long does it take to complete the MBA with a concentration in Marketing Analytics?
You can complete the program in as little as one year (Fall, Spring, Summer), or choose a more flexible path over three semesters across two academic years.
What skills will I gain from the MBA in Marketing Analytics?
You’ll master skills in:
Predictive analytics and customer segmentation
AI and machine learning in marketing
CRM and marketing automation platforms
SEO, social media strategy, and digital campaign optimization
Behavioral insights and data visualization
What careers can I pursue with this MBA?
Graduates are prepared for high-demand roles such as:
Digital Marketing Strategist
Marketing Data Analyst
Brand/Product Manager
E-commerce or CRM Manager
Customer Insights Specialist
Is prior experience in marketing or analytics required?
No specific background is required, but applicants should have a strong interest in data, digital tools, and marketing strategy. Foundational business or analytics experience can be beneficial.
How do I apply to the MBA in Marketing Analytics?
You can apply through the Rensselaer Graduate Admissions portal. Requirements typically include a resume, transcripts, statement of purpose, and letters of recommendation.
GMAT/GRE scores are optional.
What support is available for international students?
International students benefit from Rensselaer’s extensive support services, including visa and OPT guidance, English language resources, and access to global alumni networks.
What makes RPI’s Lally School a strong choice for marketing analytics?
Lally stands at the intersection of business and technology. Students gain hands-on experience with AI, data science, and digital marketing in a business school embedded within a top-tier STEM institution.
