TITLE: Student goes "Far Out" with Interactive Exhibit | RIT

January 6, 2025
RIT encourages students to engage in interdisciplinary collaboration to bring their passions to life.
Media
Interactive Media
Ally Maus - NMID
(BS ‘23) exemplifies this through her work on “
Far Out
” as a part of the 2023 New Media Capstone. Ally came together with students from the College of Art and Design’s New Media Design program to create an interactive media exhibit that assisted participants in the act of sharing significant memories with the goal of deepening connections within our communities.
