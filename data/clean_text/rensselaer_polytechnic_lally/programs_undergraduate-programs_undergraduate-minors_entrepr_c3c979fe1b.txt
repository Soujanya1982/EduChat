TITLE: Entrepreneurship, Minor | RPI Lally School of Management

Program Overview
Think outside the box and spark change through innovation with a minor that provides an overview of entrepreneurship. This program is designed for Rensselaer students majoring in any other field.
Required Courses:
MGMT 1100 —
Introduction to Management
MGMT 4510 —
Invention, Innovation, and Entrepreneurship
MGMT 4520 —
Introduction to Technological Entrepreneurship
MGMT 4530 —
Starting Up a New Venture
Designated minor advisers can approve course substitutions to meet individual student needs.
