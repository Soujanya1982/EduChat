Applying to NYU is your opportunity to share your story, passions, and potential with a community that values curiosity, creativity, and ambition. Whether you’re a first-year student, transfer, or international applicant, this guide will walk you through everything you need to know — from getting ready to applying, to submitting your materials and tracking your application — so you can put your best foot forward.
Before you Apply
Your application journey: How we review applications
There are no formulas to admissions decisions; NYU seeks students with diverse interests, talents, and goals. Bring your curiosity, creativity, and passion — your story is what makes you stand out!
Stay connected: Use your personal email
Make sure to use a personal email address. We strongly encourage you not to use a school email address to submit your application, as you may miss important updates about your application and admissions decision. Think of it as your direct line to NYU — don’t miss an important message about your future!
Documents: What to submit
Please submit only documents or copies that you can replace, because NYU does not return application materials. Keep your originals safe and focus on submitting your best work.
Supplemental materials: Only what’s required
Only submit artistic materials if your program requires an audition or portfolio. We cannot consider other supplemental materials like research papers or photos. Send only what truly showcases your talents — it helps your application shine.
Application authenticity: Be honest, be you
Ensure your application and all supporting documents are truthful. NYU has a strict policy on application authenticity and working with agents or third parties. Your authentic story is your strongest asset — tell it confidently!
Application fee waivers
We don’t want the application fee to be a barrier. If paying the fee would create a financial hardship, simply request a fee waiver in the Common Application. Students affected by an ongoing humanitarian crisis may also request a waiver by selecting “Impacted by Ongoing Humanitarian Crisis.”

After you Apply
Track your progress: Checking application status
Within three weeks of the application deadline, NYU will confirm receipt of your application and provide instructions to track its status. Stay in the know and in control of your application journey!
Missing materials: Don’t panic
Supporting materials should ideally arrive by the deadline, but late materials can be accepted within the timeframe shown in your portal (typically 1–2 weeks after the application deadline).
