Traveling to Campus
For teams traveling to UT Austin via airport, there are multiple options available to help you navigate from the airport to your hotel and campus. While the hotel does not offer an airport shuttle, participating teams can use rideshare apps (i.e. Uber and Lyft) to get to campus. Teams can also utilize the CapMetro bus service to travel to and from the hotel and campus. For more information, see UT Austin Visitor Resources.
Hotel Information & Getting to Campus
Hilton Garden Inn Austin University Capitol District
The Hilton Garden Inn Austin University Capitol District is located 2 blocks from the NWCC competition site, Robert Rowling Hall (RRH). The hotel has a variety of amenities available for conference guests.
