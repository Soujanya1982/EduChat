TITLE: Research Opportunities - NYU Stern

Research Opportunities
Undergraduate students can participate in a variety of research opportunities with NYU Stern's world-class faculty members, reflecting NYU Stern's ongoing commitment to academic excellence.
Established in 2001, the
Honors Program
pairs high-achieving seniors with faculty members who mentor participants through graduate-level research and thesis development over the course of two semesters.
Through the
Stern Program For Undergraduate Research (SPUR),
students expand their intellectual development through academic research, learning from world-renowned Stern faculty conducting state-of-the-art research projects. NYU Stern students engage one-on-one with Stern faculty to learn research methodologies used in the field and directly support their groundbreaking research.
