MBA Digital and Social Media
Expand your digital knowledge by mastering enterprise-level digital and social media management. You’ll learn to effectively engage target audiences and use data analytics to enhance your impact on organizational objectives. Courses in this specialization are taught by full-time faculty and successful industry practitioners.
Online Format
The courses for the digital and social media major or concentration are offered online. You may take MBA core courses outside the specialization in person or online.
Program Requirements
The
Lerner MBA’s
core business courses and professional development classes give you a solid base of business management knowledge. Then choose a digital and social media specialization option.
Major in Digital and Social Media
The major requires 5 courses and offers an in-depth study of digital media.
Major Courses (15 credits)
Required course:
COMM 702 – Principles of Strategic Communication
Choose 4 from:
COMM 719 – Reputation Management
COMM 731 – Integrated Marketing
COMM 732 – Social Media Campaigns
COMM 733 – Writing for Digital Media
COMM 734 – Digital Media Content Strategy
COMM 735 – Social Media Management
COMM 736 – User Data Analytics
COMM 739 – Topics in Digital Media
COMM 756 – Advanced Strategic Communication Writing
Concentration in Digital and Social Media
The concentration requires 3 courses and offers a broad view of digital media.
Concentration Courses (9 credits)
Required course:
COMM 702 – Principles of Strategic Communication
