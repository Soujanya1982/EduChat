Transfer to WPI: Information for Transfer Students
Still looking for the right college to launch your career? It doesn't matter what school you're currently attending.
If you’re craving a challenge and a community that matches your drive, transfer to Worcester Polytechnic Institute (WPI).
WPI’s hands-on, project-based approach prepares students to thrive in the future of work.
You’ll be out there building, creating, and collaborating on real-world solutions from day one—and you’ll graduate with a portfolio that makes you stand out.
Ready for the nation's most immersive STEM education? Get started by scheduling a campus visit today.

Why Transfer to WPI?
Step into a community built to support your success—and launch your career.
Take two summer classes for free.
To help you stay on track—or even get ahead—all incoming transfer students are offered two free summer courses.
Gain immersive work experience.
Dig into hands-on projects on and off campus, create solutions to important problems, and land an exciting internship in your field.
Stand out early in your career.
Graduate with a distinguished WPI degree and a project portfolio that tells employers you’re ready to excel. Plus, leverage our award-winning career center and active network of alumni across the globe.

Previously Admitted to WPI?
Transfer admission to Worcester Polytechnic Institute (WPI) is
guaranteed
to candidates admitted as first-year students with proof of a 3.0 GPA. Previously admitted transfers are also given very strong consideration.
