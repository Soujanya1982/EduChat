Undergraduate Business Admissions at RPI
Students interested in earning a business degree at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute apply through the university’s Office of Undergraduate Admissions. As part of your application, you can indicate your intended major in the Lally School of Management, including programs such as Business and Management, Business Analytics, Finance, or Marketing & Advanced Computing.
The admissions office provides complete details on application requirements, important deadlines, scholarships, and how to apply.
