FIRST YEAR EXPERIENCE
Start your business education on Day 1 -
Our freshmen take courses including Financial and Managerial Accounting, Business Day 1, Business Statistics and Microeconomics to prepare them for their degree and internships early on.
Direct admission -
Accepted students are admitted directly into the college and have access to coursework and professional skillsets, allowing them to be internship-ready by the end of freshman year.
Hit your career running -
With significant career development curriculum & events, our graduates are ready to excel in their new career after graduation.
New Business Curriculum -
Beginning in Fall 2025, our curriculum will require an Introduction to Applied Artificial Intelligence course, with further AI-focused offerings to come.
