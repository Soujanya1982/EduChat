Emergency Contact Information
It is Roger Williams University’s policy to conduct its operation with the highest regard for the health and safety of its students, employees and the public, and the protection and preservation of property and environment. In the event of an emergency, the University will direct you to an Emergency Preparedness page with details and instructions.
In the meantime, students, faculty, staff, parents and campus visitors are welcome to view the
Roger Williams University Emergency Response Plan
. This document details a coordinated plan of action involving departments campus-wide; it identifies potential hazards and emergencies and provides a clear outline of the overall emergency response organization.
