Business Minor (For Non-Business Majors)
Turn numbers into narratives
Business analytics combines methods from information science, math, and statistics to collect and interpret data. It’s a growing field that companies increasingly rely on to make strategic decisions. Training in business analytics gives you a competitive edge as a professional and leader.
A great complement to any major
The business analytics minor at Gies Business has wide applicability to other areas of business. You’ll gain experience using tools and techniques for:
Predicting business outcomes
Locating where operational problems occur
Noticing patterns in market behavior
Modeling and testing solutions before implementing them
