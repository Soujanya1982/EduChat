Sebastian Perez
McDermott Scholar, Supply Chain Management and Business Analytics

Through this program, I have gained hands-on experience developing research papers and proposals while also applying analytical skills to real-world business challenges. Beyond traditional research, JURS has provided invaluable exposure to case competitions and collaborative projects with industry partners and dedicated team members, giving me practical experience in data analysis, strategic problem-solving, and professional communication. These opportunities have not only strengthened my academic foundation but have also prepared me to confidently bridge the gap between classroom learning and real-world business applications as I continue my academic journey.

Aishah Abdulla

Over the past year, I have worked directly with industry partners like Thermoplan AG, turning classroom concepts into practical solutions and seeing how strategy, data, and law intersect in real decisions. These hands-on projects, along with the network I built, pushed me beyond theory and clarified my interest in applying legal thinking within business environments. Unlike a traditional undergraduate experience, JURS gave me a space to test ideas, collaborate with professionals, and develop a mindset focused on impact rather than memorization.
