Who can sponsor me?
Sponsorship is usually done through a broker/dealer. Contact FINRA® at 800-289-9999 or visit their website at
finra.org
for further information on who can sponsor you.
Which series exams do not require a broker/dealer to sponsor them?
Licenses that do not require a sponsor include the Series 3, 30, 31, 32, 34, 63, 65 & 66.
How do I get into a client readiness learning session?
We provide cohort based client readiness programs to organizations to help expand your client base. Please have your Human Resources representative contact us to set up a session for your organization.
