TITLE: Undergraduate Accounting Pipeline Programs

Undergraduate Accounting Opportunities
Your first step into the accounting world
UNC Kenan-Flagler’s Master of Accounting (MAC) program offers access to meaningful, real-world learning experiences for undergraduate students interested in accounting, finance, and business. These initiatives provide opportunities to gain practical skills, hands-on exposure to accounting and advisory work, and direct connections with MAC faculty, alumni, and employer partners. Through their participation, students build their professional networks, and experience the transformative impact of accounting in today’s business world.
MAC Mentorship Program
Offers UNC‑Chapel Hill undergraduates early, practical exposure to accounting and finance through EY‑supported workshops, mentorship, career‑readiness events, and exclusive access to the MAC community.

Bridging the GAAP
Gives non-UNC-Chapel Hill undergraduates hands‑on exposure to accounting through a MAC faculty designed case competition, firm‑coach support, and an immersive on‑campus presentation weekend.
