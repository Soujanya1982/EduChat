Business Minors
Business essentials for your long-term success
When it comes to supporting long-term career success, the versatility of a business minor can’t be surpassed.
If you have a passion you’d like to turn into a business after graduation, if you’re looking to augment or supplement your liberal arts or other professional degree, or if you’d like to master skills that are attractive to a wide range of employers while leaving you enough time to complete a major or double-major, the UNC Kenan-Flagler business minor programs are a great choice.
Business Administration Minor
Efficient design
You'll gain critical business skills while having enough time to finish your major or double-major.
Interdisciplinary range
The business minor focuses on mastering skills that can be applied to any career trajectory, including the arts, education, health care, and the nonprofit sector.
Focus on business fundamentals
Fundamentals including analytical skills, problem-solving, strategic thinking, communication, leadership and teamwork.
Prior to admission to the business administration minor, you will work on completing general education requirements, which apply to all UNC students, and prerequisites for admission to the minor, many of which satisfy general education requirements.

Curriculum
Business prerequisite requirements (8.5-9.5)
4 core courses (10.5 credits)
2 business electives (4.5 credits)
Business minors may take additional business elective courses, up to six total. All minor requirements must be completed through Kenan-Flagler.
Prerequisite Requirements
STOR 155 or DATA 110 or STOR 120
ECON 101 Introduction to Economics
BUSI 100 Introduction to Business: People, Profits, Planet
Core Courses
BUSI 403 Operations Management
BUSI 406 Marketing
BUSI 408 Corporate Finance
BUSI 411 Strategic Management
Electives
Select one additional 3.0 credit-hour business course and one 1.5 credit-hour business course or combine three 1.5 credit-hour business courses from other BUSI core or BUSI electives.
As a business minor, you can enhance your experience by participating in any of our
undergraduate business global programs
for business credit.

Real Estate Minor
Real-world real estate programming
Leveraging innovative programming, we prepare graduates for a wide array of real estate positions in nearly every corner of the industry.
Career treks
Coordinating with academic coursework, career treks offered throughout the year to multiple cities give access to top real estate employers and projects.
Engaged alumni network
As one of the industry's most engaged alumni networks, our community actively organizes multiple events across major geographies.

Business of Health Minor
Interdisciplinary Learning
Through core and elective courses that cut across numerous academic disciplines, students from a variety of majors will learn together.
Business Fundamentals
Students will develop a solid foundation in core business concepts and skills that are applicable to the healthcare industry.
Professional Network and Industry Connections
Students will be offered opportunities to build a strong professional network and establish valuable connections within the healthcare industry.
Prior to admission to the business of health minor, you will work on completing general education requirements, which apply to all UNC students, and prerequisites for admission to the minor, many of which satisfy general education requirements.
