Business Technology, BBA
Objective
Today’s business and governmental organizations rely heavily upon information management for strategic success and efficient administration.
The Bachelor of Business Administration in Business Technology provides students with key information technology and managerial skills needed in current business environments, for example in technology project management, FinTech, healthcare informatics, or cyber security.
What You'll Learn
In addition to satisfying the requirements of the University’s Cognates Program of General Education and electives to meet the overall 120-credit degree requirement, students pursuing the BBA in Business Technology must complete the BBA Business Core of Required Courses and the specific coursework for the Business Technology area of specialization.
