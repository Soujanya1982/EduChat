Graduate Scholarships
& Financial Aid
Meadows School of the Arts provides a limited number of tuition scholarships and/or graduate assistantships to new graduate students who have been reviewed (through audition, portfolio review and/or interview) by the Meadows faculty and the administration and demonstrate the highest level of achievement in their academic and artistic careers.
