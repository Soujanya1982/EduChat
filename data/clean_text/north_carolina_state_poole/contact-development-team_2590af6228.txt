Contact Us
The Office of Development and External Relations is located across the street from Nelson Hall at 2806 Hillsborough Street. Our goal is to build and strengthen relationships with Poole alumni, parents and partnerships to facilitate philanthropic support to the mission and values of the college. By connecting the passion and interest of our supporters to the college's priorities, we are able to impact the future of business education and research at NC State.
