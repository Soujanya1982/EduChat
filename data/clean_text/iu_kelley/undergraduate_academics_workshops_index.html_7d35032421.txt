Field-specific Workshops set you up for long-term success
Through customized courses, speakers, company visits, networking, and mentoring, you’ll put your classroom learning into practice and make connections in your future field while you’re still in school.
As a Workshop student, you’ll be immersed in trailblazing research and experiential learning that will uniquely prepare you to meet the demands of your chosen industry.
Each Workshop is unique: apply to the one that will help you achieve your career goals.
Review the tables to determine when Workshops are held and when you can apply. Workshops vary on whether you need to be a first-year, second-year, third-year, or fourth-year student when you apply.

Frequently asked questions (FAQ)
Can I apply for Kelley Workshops if I’m not a Kelley major?  Kelley minor?
At this time, only admitted Kelley majors may apply to any of the Kelley Workshops. Kelley minors are not eligible for Kelley Workshops.
Can I apply to more than one Workshop?
Yes, students may
apply
to multiple workshops. However, students may only accept entry into one workshop.
Can I be enrolled in more than one Kelley Workshop?
Students may only enroll in one Kelley Workshop during their time as an IU undergraduate.
Is a Workshop a Student Group (i.e. a student-led club or organization)?
Workshops are not student-led initiatives. Workshops are academic programs run by faculty directors, and include courses taken for credit.
When do I apply for Workshops?
Because each Workshop is unique, the answer varies depending on the Workshop. A majority of Workshops open applications in either fall or spring of a student’s second year at IU. Explore the tables (above) to find details on each individual workshop application timeline.
How do I find which Workshop is the best fit for me? Are there additional ways to learn more about Workshops or chat with current members?
WorkshopFest is held at the beginning of each semester. This is an event with representation and information from every Workshop. Additionally, some Workshops will hold call-out meetings and other recruiting events. Subscribe to the
Kelley Insider
to stay up to date on upcoming Workshop events.
