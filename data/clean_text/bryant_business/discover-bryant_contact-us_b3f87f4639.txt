Need help? We’re here for you.
Bryant University provides multiple ways to connect for inquiries regarding admission, student affairs, and general university operations.
Admission
The admission process at Bryant is deliberately individualized, and we work one-on-one with students throughout the process. If you need to speak with an admission counselor,
please browse the list of counselors
and look for the state (or area) where your high school is located.
Phone: (800) 622-7001 or (401) 232-6100
Email:
admission@bryant.edu
Student Affairs
The Department of Student Affairs facilitates quality co-curricular opportunities that offer purposeful learning. The department staff focuses on career education, campus engagement, campus ministry, counseling services, belonging and well-being, health services, public safety, and residential life.
Phone: (401) 232-6046
You can also visit our information directory or employee directory for detailed contact information and to find specific offices.
