Bauer Visit
Event Details:
Want to learn more about our Nationally Ranked Business Programs? Schedule your prospective visit and feel free to bring the family with you. During your meeting, we will have a short information session that will go over the following: Majors & Specialty Programs, Internship & Career Opportunities, Admission Requirements, and Scholarship Information.
To schedule a virtual or in-person visit:
Please contact:
bmoreno@central.uh.edu
The Office of Admissions is now offering campus visits. Register for an opportunity to receive a live campus tour from a student ambassador and a brief information session from an admissions representative. If you would like to attend this session as well as please RSVP at the following link:
