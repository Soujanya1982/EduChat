TITLE: How to Apply to the Lundquist College | Lundquist College of Business

At the Lundquist College of Business, we offer degree options that cover the world of business. Admissions criteria and paths differ by program, but we've collected everything here to jumpstart your application process no matter the program.
Undergraduate Programs
For those interested in our
undergraduate programs
, the first step is to apply to the University of Oregon. After being admitted to the university, you will follow one of
two paths
to become an
accounting major
or
business major
.
To apply,
visit the UO Office of Admissions
or call (800) BE-A-DUCK (800-232-3825).
Learn About the Two Admissions Paths
Add a Business Minor
For non-business undergraduate students at the University of Oregon, it is also possible to add a minor in business administration. Plus, we offer entrepreneurship, sport business, or sustainable business minors to all students, including to business and accounting majors.
Learn How To Add a Business Minor
