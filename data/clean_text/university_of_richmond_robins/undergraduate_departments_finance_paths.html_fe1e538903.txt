Finance Concentration Sample Paths
While not required, it is recommended that students take FIN 360 in their sophomore year and take FIN 366 and FIN 361 by completion of their junior year. The minimum number of required finance (FIN) units is four. However, it is suggested that students take at least five. Below are a few sample paths a student may take to achieve this.
