Application Process
Application for the undergraduate business program majors is open to Wake Forest second-year students in their Fall semester.
Admission to the School of Business is highly competitive. While the minimum application requirements are outlined below, meeting these criteria does not guarantee admission.
Completed application
Successful completion of the following pre-requisite courses:
>Introduction to Economics (ECN 150) (AP credit may fulfill the Introduction to Economics (ECN 150) course requirement)
>Calculus with Analytic Geometry I (MTH 111) or Calculus with Analytic Geometry II (MTH 112) (AP credit may fulfill the Calculus with Analytic Geometry I (MTH 111) or the Calculus with Analytic Geometry II (MTH 112) course requirement)
>Calculus with Analytic Geometry II (MTH 112) must be taken by Decision Analytics majors
>Introduction to Financial Accounting (ACC 111) at Wake Forest University
>All prerequisite courses listed above must be completed by the end of the fall semester of the applicant’s second year.
Additional details on application requirements can be found in the
