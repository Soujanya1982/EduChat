Undergraduate Management Minor Overview
This minor provides an overview of the management program. It is designed to expose students to the field of management and to provide them with an opportunity to build and enhance their professional and leadership skills through a formal course of study. This minor is only open to students with a declared major in the College of Business and/or students enrolled in either the Integrated Business and Engineering (IBE) or Computer Science and Business (CSB) programs. Lehigh business students cannot receive overlapping credit between College of Business major and minor courses or overlapping credit between College of Business minor courses.
The requirements for the Management Minor: three courses equaling 9 credit hours from the following courses:
MGT 328: Negotiations and Conflict Management
MGT 333: Human Resources Management
MGT 342: Managing in the International Organization
MGT 363: Managing Diversity and Inclusion in the Workplace
