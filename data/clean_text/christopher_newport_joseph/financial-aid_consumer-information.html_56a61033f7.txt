Constitution Day
Constitution Day is September 17 each year and commemorates the signing of the United States Constitution on September 17, 1787. The University library offers many books and electronic resources regarding the United States Constitution. Our librarians and staff are happy to assist you in finding great resources.
Contact them
for more information and assistance.
September 12, 2024 - A
debate
was hosted in Peebles Theatre by the CNU Center for American Studies and students, faculty and staff were welcome to attend.
The Center for American Studies provides an experience that students can attend events surrounding the Constitution. Please visit their
website
for more updated information each year.

University Non-discrimination and Title IX Policy Statement
Christopher Newport University is committed to providing an environment that emphasizes the dignity and worth of every member of its community and that is free from harassment and discrimination in admissions, employment, and education programs or activities based on race, color, religion, sex, national origin, age, disability, genetic information, sexual orientation, gender identity, marital status, military/veteran status, political affiliation,
pregnancy,
or any other status protected by law.
Any person may report a concern (Title IX or equal opportunity) under the policy at any time, including non-business hours, by contacting:

You can register to vote in the state of Virginia online at the following website. You can also download a paper voter registration form. The website also provides information on registration deadlines and the option of same day voter registration, allowing you to submit a provisional ballot.
www.elections.virginia.gov/registration/
Withdrawal from the University

undergraduate catalog
.
