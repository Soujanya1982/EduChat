TITLE: Financial Aid - BYU Marriott School of Business

Overview
Undergraduate students in the BYU Marriott School of Business can receive financial aid from several sources, including federal grants and loans, BYU scholarships, and BYU Marriott scholarships.
Graduate students in BYU Marriott can receive financial aid from a variety of BYU Marriott and program-specific scholarships as well as federal and BYU Marriott loans.
