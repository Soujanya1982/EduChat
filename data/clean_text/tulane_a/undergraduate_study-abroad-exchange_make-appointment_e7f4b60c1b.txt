TITLE: Make an Appointment with Freeman Study Abroad & Exchange Staff

The Freeman Study Abroad & Exchange team members are here to answer questions, offer advice, and help you make the most of your international experience - whether that's as an outgoing study abroad student or an incoming international exchange student. We are happy to meet with you.
Before attending an advising appointment, we ask that you spend some time reading through our programs, policies, and procedures on the
Freeman Abroad website
.
In general, we are able to take appointments Monday through Friday. We also have a walk-in hour from 2:00 - 3:00 pm every weekday except Tuesday. Every Tuesday during the regular semesters, come to see us at
Global Cafe
in the LBC from 2:00 - 4:00 pm.
To make an appointment with an individual staff member, click on the link for the staff member with whom you wish to meet:
Study Abroad (Outgoing) Student Advising: Aaron Forbes
Exchange (Incoming) Student Advising: Lauren Bix
General student advising, Altman Students, agreement management, and program administration: Mary Hicks
For the first available appointment without choosing a staff member, select your appointment type below to find the best time and date for you:

Study Abroad Advising in Person
Incoming Student Advising on Zoom
Incoming Student Advising in Person
If you need same-day assistance or are not able to make an appointment at a date and time that is convenient for you please email
freemanabroad@tulane.edu
so that we can help you accordingly.
