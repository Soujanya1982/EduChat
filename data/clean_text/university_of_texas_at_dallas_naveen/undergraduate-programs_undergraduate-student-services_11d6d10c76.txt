TITLE: Undergraduate Student Services | Contact

JSOM Undergraduate Student Services (UGSS) supports and empowers students throughout their journey at the Jindal School of Management. We help students make informed academic decisions, connect with campus resources, and stay on course for graduation.
Whether you need guidance, have questions, or just want to stop by and chat, we’re here for you. Reach out to us for any questions on your undergraduate journey.
