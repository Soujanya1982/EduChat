TITLE: Declare or Drop Pre-Business

To access the online form to declare the pre-business major:
You must be a current undergraduate student at the University of Oregon.
and
You must not already be a pre-business administration (PBA) major, business administration (BADM) major, accounting (ACTG) major, or a business administration minor.
Proceed to the Declare PBA Form
(You will be prompted to log in with your Duck ID.)
Watch Video Tutorial: How to Change Your Major to Pre-Business
To access the online form to drop the pre-business major:
You must be a current pre-business administration (PBA) major.
You will be able to optionally declare the business minor.
Proceed to the Drop PBA Form
(You will be prompted to log in with your Duck ID.)
