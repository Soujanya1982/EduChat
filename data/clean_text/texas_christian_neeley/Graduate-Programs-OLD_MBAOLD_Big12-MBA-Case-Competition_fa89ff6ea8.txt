Big XII MBA Case Competition
March 26-28, 2026
Join top MBA talent from across the Big XII Conference for a high-impact case competition hosted by the TCU Neeley School of Business.
Apply your intellectual, analytical and creative expertise to a real-world business challenge during the 18th annual Big XII MBA Case Competition. Teams will have one day to develop strategic recommendations and present their solutions to a panel of senior executives and industry professionals from across North Texas.
The competition also offers participants the opportunity to experience TCU, connect with peer MBA programs and engage with business leaders in one of the nation’s fastest-growing markets.
Prizes
First Place:
$6,000 and 4 pairs of boots, donated by Justin boots
Second Place:
$4,000
Third Place

Teams
Each invited Big XII institution may send one team of four full-time MBA students to compete against peer programs from across the conference.
Judges
Senior executives and professionals from a variety of industries across North Texas.
