Internal Audit Certificate
Our Internal Audit (IA) Certificate program is 1 of only 13 programs in the world and 1 of only 8 in the United States to be recognized as a Center of Excellence by the Institute of Internal Auditors.
The objective of the IA certificate is to supply students of all majors with theoretical and practical applications of internal auditing.
Courses offered through the certificate are taught by practitioners in the internal audit field, who bring a wealth of real-world experience to the classroom.
