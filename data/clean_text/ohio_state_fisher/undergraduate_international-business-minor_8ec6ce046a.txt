International Business
The International Business Minor complements a student’s major by providing insights into the global economy, international markets, and cross-cultural management. Students will build awareness of the opportunities and challenges of operating in an increasingly interconnected world and gain valuable preparation for careers in international business and cross-national environments.
No application is required.
Simply notify your college advisor if you intend to pursue the minor.

Frequently Asked Questions
Do I need to apply to the International Business Minor?
No application is needed. Let your college advisor know you plan to pursue the minor. You may also contact Fisher Undergraduate Programs at (614) 292-2715 for support.
Who approves my International Business Minor?
Your college will approve the minor once you’ve met the requirements. Fisher does not require formal approval but can provide a signed program form if needed.
Can I use minor courses for my major or business specialization?
No. Minor courses may not count toward any business core or specialization requirements, except as free electives.
Can I use courses from this minor in another minor?
You must have at least
12 unique hours
in each minor. Check with your advisor for more information.
Can I include foreign language courses in this minor?
No. Foreign language coursework is not applicable to the International Business Minor.
Can I take minor courses pass/non-pass?
No. All courses must be taken for a grade. Pass/Non-Pass courses will not count.
What grades do I need to complete the minor?
You must earn:
C- or higher
in each course
A
2.00 cumulative GPA
across all minor courses
Can I take minor courses before completing prerequisites?
No. All prerequisites must be completed before taking required or elective courses in the minor.
Can I substitute a different course for an elective?
Yes. The course must have a strong international focus and must be approved by an international business faculty member in the MHR department.
Contact
Professor Mona Makhija
for review and approval.
Can I use transfer credit for the minor?
You may apply up to
6 credit hours
of transfer credit toward the minor if the courses are equivalent.
