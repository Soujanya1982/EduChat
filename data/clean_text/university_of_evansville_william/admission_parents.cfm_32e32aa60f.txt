Parents of Prospective Students
At the University of Evansville, we’re thinking about those things, too. Every day, our talented faculty are working as supportive mentors to our students, guiding them through a challenging and collaborative learning process to prepare them for success in their chosen field. Our staff is working alongside them to create a supportive, energetic environment full of exciting opportunities to get involved and become leaders on campus. And our Center for Career Development is working one-on-one with students to connect them to internships, co-op opportunities, alumni networks, and – eventually – jobs that will set them on the path to future success.
That’s why the University of Evansville is more than a great education. It’s a smart investment in your student’s personal and professional success after graduation.
