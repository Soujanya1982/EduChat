Exclusive Benefits for WPI Transfer Partner Schools
Students from any college or university can transfer to WPI and expect clear guidance and support. But only students transferring from our
partner
schools get these added benefits.
Receive a $20,000 transfer scholarship.
Transfer students admitted from a partner school are guaranteed the WPI Transfer Partner Student Scholarship, valued at a minimum of $20,000 each year for up to four years. You may also combine this scholarship with additional aid.
Streamline your admission.
Transfer students from partner institutions are guaranteed admission when they meet the requirements of our transfer agreement.
Focus on your future.
Follow a built-in framework that tells you exactly which courses transfer, how your credits apply to your chosen program, and what steps you can take now to stay on track.
Save time and money.
Complete the recommended background courses for a WPI degree—mapped out in each partnership’s official transfer guide—at your community college or two-year program. Then, once you’re here, take the specialized, upper-level STEM courses only available at WPI.

Transfer Partnership Framework
We have a long history of success with transfer partnerships, starting with
Quinsigamond Community College
here in Worcester in 1989.
Transfer students from partnering institutions are
guaranteed
admission to WPI when they meet the following criteria:
Minimum GPA of 3.3
Associate’s degree in any academic program
Successful completion of one (1) college-level precalculus or calculus I course
Successful completion of one (1) laboratory science course
* If your GPA is 3.0 or higher and you meet all other requirements, we still strongly encourage you to apply!
Why Transfer to WPI?
Join a community built to support your success—and launch your career.
Take two summer classes for free.
To help you stay on track—or even get ahead—all incoming transfer students are offered two free summer courses.
Access financial aid and scholarships.
WPI offers merit scholarships and need-based
financial aid for transfer students
. Students with demonstrated financial need may qualify for additional support through a Global Scholarship. Phi Theta Kappa scholars are guaranteed $10,000 per year.
Gain immersive work experience.
Dig into hands-on projects on and off campus, create solutions to important problems, and land an exciting internship in your field.
Stand out early in your career.
Graduate with a distinguished WPI degree and a project portfolio that tells employers you’re ready to excel. Plus, leverage our award-winning career center and active network of alumni across the globe.
