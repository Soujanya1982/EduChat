Admission Requirements
Our admissions criteria are designed for people with a bachelor’s degree in any area of study.
Admission Criteria
Apply via
PTCAS
.
A completed bachelor’s degree with a minimum GPA of 3.2. There is no specific undergraduate major required.
Completion of all prerequisite courses (8 credits Biology, 8 credits Chemistry, 8 credits Physics, 8 credits Anatomy and Physiology, 3-4 credit Statistics) with a 3.2 overall. All prerequisite coursework must be completed within 10 years of enrollment date and applicants may apply with no more than two "in-progress" prerequisite courses. Final transcripts for all in-progress prerequisites are required prior to starting the program.
Two letters of recommendation, one from a faculty if possible.
We
do not
require the GRE or other graduate admission tests for entry or interview.

Admission Timeline
Rolling admissions; however, we encourage early application due to limited space and assistantships. PTCAS opens on June 15, 2025 to start the program in May 2026.
