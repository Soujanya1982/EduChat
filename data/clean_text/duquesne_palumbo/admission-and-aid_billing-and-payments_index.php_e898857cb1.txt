TITLE: Student Accounts

We are dedicated to helping students and parents understand the billing process and
their financial responsibilities to Duquesne.
Students:
the Tuition Payment Portal is how students access billing statements, make payments,
enroll in a payment plan, add authorized payers and signup for direct deposit refunds.
Authorized Payer:
in order for a parent, guardian or family member to pay a student's tuition bill
or  enroll in a semester-based payment plan, they must be designated as an Authorized
Payer by their student.
Authorized Payers use the link below to access the payment portal.
