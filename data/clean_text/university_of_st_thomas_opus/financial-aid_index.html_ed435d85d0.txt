Financial Aid & Support for Every Student
At the University of St. Thomas, we believe finances should never stand between you and your potential. Our financial aid experts are here to guide you through scholarships, grants, and aid opportunities — every step of the way.
Each program has its own process, so start by exploring the resources that apply to you.
Undergraduate Financial Aid
From
merit-based scholarships
to need-based grants, most undergraduate students receive financial aid — making the cost of a St. Thomas education comparable to many public universities.
Dougherty Family College (DFC) Financial Aid
Our two-year DFC program is designed for affordability. Students receive generous financial support — including free textbooks, meals, laptops, and transit passes — plus dedicated academic and career guidance.
Graduate Financial Aid
Graduate students can explore scholarships, assistantships, and employer partnerships to help offset costs and make advanced study more accessible.
Looking Ahead
A St. Thomas education is an investment in your future — one that prepares you to lead, serve, and thrive. Learn more about our campuses and community.
