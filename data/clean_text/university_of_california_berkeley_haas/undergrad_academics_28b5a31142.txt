TITLE: Academic Resources - Undergraduate Program - UC Berkeley Haas

Haas Gives You the Tools to Make an Impact
Your academic path at Haas will give you the knowledge and understanding of the business landscape and the tools to succeed within it. Our illustrious curriculum offers the flexibility to pursue interdisciplinary study through a
simultaneous degree
, summer minor in sustainable business and policy, or our many
global experiences
. Regardless of the career that you choose for yourself, you will learn the skills necessary to work as part of a team, solve problems, and make an impact. At the end of your academic journey you will earn a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration degree from Berkeley Haas.
To support you throughout your time at Haas, we provide academic advising and tutoring services that can enhance your academic experience. We encourage students to explore the multitude of opportunities and resources that Haas has to offer in order to provide a rich academic experience.
