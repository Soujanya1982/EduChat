campus Visit Grant Opportunity
Prospective first-year and transfer students who attend an official visit through the Admission Office will qualify for a $2,000 annual Visit Grant.
Current high school juniors, enrolling in the fall of 2027, will earn their grant by visiting between Jan. 5 and Nov. 21, 2026. That’s $8,000 over four years!
