Internships and Careers
In addition to the Stern
Professional Development  & Career Education (PDCE) team
, the
NYU Wasserman Center for Career Development
provides individual guidance and an array of services to help you secure internships, on-campus positions, and full-time employment upon graduation.
The Wasserman Center helps you explore career directions and provides support through a host of services and resources that include:
Individual career coaching appointments
Resume and cover letter assistance
Mock interviews
Career fairs
Online job postings and career resources via NYU Handshake
On-campus recruitment
Alumni networking
