Commuter Student Association
The Commuter Student Association (CSA) is a way for commuter and off-campus resident students to get involved in the campus community and stay "in the know!"
Members of the association work together to bridge the gap between students who live off-campus and all the student activities that take place. The main goal is to connect and have fun! CSA hosts a number of events on campus specifically for commuter students, including an annual Thanksgiving lunch, ice cream socials, commuter appreciation days and more. CSA also supports several philanthropies, including Justin’s Wings of Hope and Peace OUTside Campus, through various campus activities.
The association also provides a platform for students to discuss issues that relate specifically to the off-campus student population.
