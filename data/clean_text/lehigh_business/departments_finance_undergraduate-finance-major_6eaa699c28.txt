Undergraduate Finance Major Overview
Lehigh's Finance major builds upon the Lehigh Business integrated core. The curriculum focuses on the private sector involving corporations and financial institutions. Students may choose from a wide range of finance electives. They are encouraged to take courses in related areas such as accounting, computers, economics and quantitative methods.
