TITLE: Financial Aid and Billing Checklist | Ithaca College

Your Checklist
Review this list to make sure you have completed all requirements prior to the start of the academic year. All steps must be completed prior to the start of the semester.
For All Students
Complete your
FAFSA
.
Add authorized payers
to your account.
Set up
direct deposit
in case of any refund.
Review health insurance,
submit a waiver
if applicable.
Review your meal plan assignment if you’re living off campus or in on-campus apartments (Gardens or Circles) and make adjustments if desired.
Complete the
Outside Resources form
if you're receiving any outside scholarships or grants.
New Students
Expand All
Financial Aid
Review and accept
your package in Homer Connect.
Complete Title IV and E-Communications forms in Homer Connect.
If you’re accepting any offered subsidized and/or unsubsidized loans, complete
MPN
and
Entrance Counseling
.
If you plan on taking a PLUS loan, complete an
application
and the corresponding
MPN
and
Entrance Counseling
.
If you’ve been offered TAP as part of your aid package, complete a
TAP application
.
Billing
Review online bill
and confirm that the amount is correct.
Set up
authorized payers
to pay online on your behalf, if applicable.
Enroll in a
payment plan
by the billing due date if you intend to use one.
Contact your 529 company to have them send funds directly to us, if applicable.
Returning Students
Expand All
Financial Aid
Review and accept
your package in Homer Connect.
If you’re accepting sub/unsub loans for the first time, complete
MPN
and
Entrance Counseling
.
If you plan on taking a PLUS loan for the first time, complete an
application
and the corresponding
MPN
and
Entrance Counseling
.
If you’ve been offered TAP as part of your aid package, complete a
TAP application
.
Billing
Review online bill
and confirm that the amount is correct.
Enroll in a
payment plan
by the billing due date if you intend to use one.
Contact your 529 company to have them send funds directly to us, if applicable.
