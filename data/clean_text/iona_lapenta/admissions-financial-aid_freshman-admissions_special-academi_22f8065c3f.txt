Collegiate Science and Technology Entry Program
The Collegiate Science and Technology Entry Program (CSTEP) at Iona University supports undergraduate students pursuing careers in STEM, health, and licensed professions. As an interdisciplinary program, CSTEP brings together students from diverse fields, fostering a collaborative community where students can learn from each other and explore new opportunities. Through mentoring, skill-building workshops, and a growing peer network, CSTEP empowers students to excel academically, engage in research and experiential learning, and achieve their professional goals. We are excited to support our cohort as they pursue their passions and prepare for meaningful careers.
What We Do
CSTEP provides students with guidance, opportunities, and experiences that strengthen academic performance and professional readiness. Students connect with faculty, alumni, and campus partners while developing skills and gaining hands-on experience through research, internships, and service-learning projects. Workshops, conferences, and networking events help students build professional competencies and explore diverse career pathways. By combining mentorship with access to campus resources, CSTEP equips students with the knowledge, experience, and connections needed to pursue their goals.
Program Benefits
Plan Your Path:
Receive personalized academic and career guidance through regular meetings with your CSTEP advisor to set and achieve your goals.
Develop Your Skills:
Participate in workshops and professional development experiences designed to enhance your knowledge, competencies, and confidence.
Engage in Research & Internships:
Gain hands-on experience in your field through research projects and internships that strengthen your resume and practical expertise.
Prepare for Grad School:
Access support for graduate school applications, test preparation, and exam readiness to ensure you are fully prepared.
Network & Connect:
Build lasting relationships with peers, alumni, and professionals through structured networking opportunities and events.
Leverage Campus Resources:
Take full advantage of enrichment programs offered by the Samuel Rudin Academic Resource Center, the Office of Study Abroad & Service-Learning, the Office of Undergraduate Research, and more.

How to Apply
Start Your Application:
Submit the application form to begin the review process.
Share Required Documents:
After we receive your application, we’ll ask you to provide an unofficial transcript (required each semester) and proof of economic need, such as your FAFSA and/or most recent tax return (IRS forms 1040, 1040A, 1040EZ, or 4506). This helps us confirm your eligibility as a first-time CSTEP student.
Connect Through an Informational Interview:
Once your eligibility is verified, we’ll invite you to a brief informational interview to learn more about the program and your goals.
Rolling Acceptance:
Since CSTEP at Iona University offers personalized support to a limited number of students, acceptances are granted on a rolling basis until all spots are filled.
