The Campus You Have Been Searching For
At Christopher Newport University, timeless architecture and coastal beauty come together to create a stunning and welcoming campus that is alive with activities and opportunities. Wander tree-lined walkways, study in bright, inspiring spaces, and enjoy beaches, parks, and vibrant city life just minutes away.

Transfer Requirements for CNU
At Christopher Newport, we look beyond numbers. We consider your academic performance, goals, experiences, and potential to thrive as a Captain.
Competitive transfer applicants typically have 12 or more college credits and a GPA of 2.5 or higher. Completing at least one college-level math and English course is strongly encouraged, and test scores are optional.
We also value leadership, service, personal statements, extracurricular involvement, and your intended major.

Investing in Your Future
At Christopher Newport University, we believe cost should never stand in the way of your potential. That’s why we work hard to make a world-class education accessible and affordable.
Merit Scholarships for Out-of-State Transfer Students
Qualified out-of-state transfer students may receive $3,000–$5,000 in renewable merit scholarships to help offset the higher cost of attendance for non-Virginia residents. Awards are based on academic performance and available funding, and are renewable each year with continued academic success.
FAFSA and Financial Aid for Transfer Students
CNU offers a wide range of financial aid and scholarship opportunities to help make your education more manageable. Submit the Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) early to maximize your eligibility for grants, loans, and scholarships. Learn more on our Tuition and Aid page.
