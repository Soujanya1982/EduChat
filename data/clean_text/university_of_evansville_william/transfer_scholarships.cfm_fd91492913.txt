TITLE: Scholarships - Transfer Admission - University of Evansville

Scholarships
UE recognizes the academic accomplishments of students and rewards your achievements through UE scholarships. Students are considered for scholarships at the time of application for admission.
Transfer Students
Eligible for
transfer academic scholarships
and special add-on scholarships.
First-year Transfer Students
Eligible for
first-year student merit scholarships
. For those who were formerly admitted to UE for the fall semester but didn’t enroll and reapply for the spring semester, UE will typically honor your original first-year student scholarship and financial aid award!
Nontraditional First-year Students
Eligible for
first-year student scholarships
.
Second Degree Students
Eligible for
transfer academic scholarships
(not including add-on scholarships).
Returning Students
Contact Student Financial Services
to determine if your original UE scholarships can be reinstated.
Non-Degree Students
Typically do not qualify for scholarships.
