Application Requirements – Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)
1. What materials are required to apply to Lally School graduate programs?
Applicants must submit an online application, a personal statement, resume or CV, two or Three letters of recommendation, and transcripts. GMAT or GRE scores are optional for most programs. GMATs are required for the Ph.D. program. Please check the
requirements
for each program carefully before submitting your application
2. Is there an application fee for Lally graduate programs?
Yes, the application fee is $75. However, this fee is waived for RPI alumni, current students, employees, and their spouses.
3. Do I need to submit GMAT or GRE scores?
No, GMAT or GRE scores are optional for most programs at the Lally School. Submitting scores may strengthen your application, but they are not required. GMATs are required for the Ph.D. program. We do not require the GRE for the M.S. in Information Technology and those scores will not be considered in application decisions for that program.
4. What should I include in my personal statement?
Your personal statement should outline your academic and professional goals, relevant experience, and why the program is a good fit for you.
5. What GPA is required for admission to Lally graduate programs?
Generally, a minimum GPA of 3.0 is required to apply. For competitive consideration, a GPA of 3.3 or higher is preferred.
6. How many letters of recommendation are required?
You must submit two letters of recommendation from academic or professional references who can speak to your qualifications and potential. Three letters are recommended for the M.S. in Information Technology.
7. What are the resume or CV expectations for the Lally application?
Your resume or CV should highlight your academic background, work experience, research, and relevant achievements aligned with the program you're applying to.
8. What are the requirements for international applicants?
International applicants must submit TOEFL or IELTS scores to demonstrate English proficiency and provide financial documentation early to ensure timely I-20 processing. Please check
Graduate Admissions
for more information on international admission.
9. Can I apply to more than one program at Lally in the same term?
No. You may apply to only one program per term through the online application system.
10. Where do I submit my application for a Lally graduate program?
All applications must be submitted online through the
Rensselaer Graduate Admissions Portal
. Be sure to review deadlines and checklist items specific to your program.
