Apply to study on-campus

Apply for our Online MBA, Online MS in Business Analytics, and Online Graduate Certificate in Business Administration programs.
PlusOne applications
Current Northeastern University undergraduate students apply for our PlusOne MS in Accounting, PlusOne MS in Finance, and PlusOne MS in International Management programs.
Dual degree candidates for the JD/MBA and LLM/MBA programs must apply and gain admission independently to Northeastern Law and the D'Amore-McKim School of Business. You'll enroll first in Northeastern Law before seeking admission to the D'Amore-McKim School of Business.
