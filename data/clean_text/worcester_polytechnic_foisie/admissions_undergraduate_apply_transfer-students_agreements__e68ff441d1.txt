Transfer from Mount Wachusett Community College to WPI
Your Transfer Path from MWCC to WPI
Transfer to WPI with confidence: clear requirements and proven return on investment make your decision easy.
Transferring from Mount Wachusett Community College (MWCC) to WPI is a simple and well-supported process. Our transfer partnership offers defined admissions criteria, substantial financial support, and a tailored pathway designed to help you move forward with confidence.
Through our agreement with MWCC, eligible students receive:
Guaranteed admission to WPI when requirements are met
A minimum
$20,000 transfer scholarship per year
, with additional aid considered
Guaranteed on-campus housing
Two free summer courses (a $5,000 value)
Automatic review for merit scholarships and financial aid
At WPI, you’ll join a nationally recognized STEM university known for our experiential learning and strong career outcomes. Graduates leave with an average starting salary of
$80,294
, proof that a WPI degree delivers long-term value.
Applying to WPI as a transfer student is 100% free. Why wait? Explore your proven transfer pathway from MWCC to WPI below.

$20K Transfer Scholarship. Each Year. Guaranteed.
Transfer students admitted from Mount Wachusett Community College are guaranteed the WPI Transfer Partner Student Scholarship, valued at a minimum of $20,000 each year for up to four years. You may also combine this scholarship with additional aid.
Free Summer Courses for Transfer Students
Take two summer courses for free (a $5,000 value) so you can connect with the campus community and get ready for WPI's famous hands-on projects.
Partnership Framework
Requirements for Admission
WPI has a long history of success with transfer students from partner institutions. Mount Wachusett Community College students are guaranteed admission as transfer applicants when they meet the following criteria:
Minimum cumulative GPA of 3.3*
Completion of an associate’s degree in any academic program
One college-level precalculus or calculus I course
One laboratory science course
* If your
GPA
is
3.0
or higher and you meet all other requirements, we still strongly encourage you to apply!
