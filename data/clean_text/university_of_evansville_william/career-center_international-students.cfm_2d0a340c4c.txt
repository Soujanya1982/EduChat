International Students
International students at the University of Evansville are encouraged to actively prepare for and pursue experiential educational opportunities, such as internships and co-ops, as part of their Evansville Experience. Becoming aware of your internship, co-op, and full-time job opportunities are integral pieces in maximizing the benefit of an education in the United States.
