Full-Time MBA Student Snapshot
No two Olin MBA students have the same story — and that’s exactly the point. Our classes are made up of people from varied professional backgrounds, cultures, and perspectives. What brings them together is a shared drive to strengthen their foundations in business while tailoring their MBA journey to match their ambitions. Here’s a look at a typical Olin MBA cohort, based on the class profiles for our 2025, 2026, and 2027 graduates.
