Early Action Admissions
Early Action is an admissions option for ambitious students who have identified Hult as one of their top-choice schools.
By applying as an Early Action candidate, you’ve indicated your commitment to studying with us, and in return, you’ll be eligible for a number of unique student benefits.
Discover the Benefits
Why apply Early Action?
Exclusive Leadership Summit
All Early Action candidates are invited to a special Leadership Summit, hosted at Hult’s historic Ashridge campus in the UK. This exclusive event takes place in your second year.
Priority access to student housing
Accepted Early Action candidates have the first choice of accommodation available in Boston, London or Dubai and the peace of mind of having secured the perfect place to live sooner.
Enhanced coaching
Every Hult student has a personal development coach. As an Early Action candidate, you’ll connect with yours sooner and benefit from additional coaching sessions over the summer to prepare you for undergraduate life.
Earlier admissions decision
Students who apply Early Action receive priority in the admissions review process, so you'll be among the first to know the outcome of your application. What could be better than knowing you've been accepted to one of your top schools early in the year?
Applying for Early Action
Show your ambition early. By applying through Early Action, you stand out as a proactive, high-potential student ready to take the lead. You’ll be among the first to receive your admissions decision—giving you a head start to secure your place, plan ahead, and unlock priority access to scholarships and housing.
If you already know Hult is where you want to be, Early Action is your chance to make it happen sooner.
