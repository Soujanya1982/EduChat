Ithaca College Visit Grant Details
Newly admitted undergraduate first-year and transfer students who enroll at Ithaca College for 12+ credits starting in fall 2026 are eligible.
Participate in a qualifying on-campus or virtual visit program between January 21 and May 6, 2026.
Confirm your enrollment at Ithaca College and pay your enrollment deposit by May 1, 2026.
Maximum grant amount: $500
The IC Visit Grant will be added to your financial aid package, with the grant disbursing for the fall 2026 semester.
This is a one-time grant.
We know it’s a big decision, so don’t worry! You don’t have to confirm your enrollment until after you visit and have time to consider all your options.
