Received your financial aid letter?
Most incoming and current students will receive a financial aid package that includes a breakdown of their anticipated costs minus financial aid they have been awarded. The information below is to guide students and families in understanding their financial aid letter, calculating their bill, and tips to ensure a smooth start to your semester.

Still have questions?
Our goal is for you and your family to feel supported, prepared, and financially confident as you begin your collegiate journey. If you still have questions after reviewing your financial aid package and the guides above, please schedule a personalized appointment with a member of our team.
