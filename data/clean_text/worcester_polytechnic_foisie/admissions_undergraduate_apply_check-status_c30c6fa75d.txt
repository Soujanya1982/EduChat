Create An Account
Do not attempt to create an account before submitting your application and receiving communications noting the receipt of your application.
This makes sure your application is linked correctly to your portal account.
Please follow these steps to create your account:
Receive an email from WPI Admissions confirming receipt of your application.
Create your account using the username provided in the email. Your username should match the email address used on your application for admission.
Select a password containing at least eight characters. Your password must include both letters and numbers.
Submit your sign-up request, which will then enable you to log in.
