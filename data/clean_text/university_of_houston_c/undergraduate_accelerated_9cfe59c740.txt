Accelerated Pathway Programs (
Accelerated B.B.A./Master of Public Policy
and
Accelerated Pathway B.B.A/Master of Science
) provide academically outstanding students an accelerated pathway to complete an undergraduate and a master’s degree in a reduced period of time. Accelerated Pathway Programs provide undergraduate students permission to take a specified number of master’s level courses as electives toward completion of their bachelor’s degree. The students then apply to the graduate degree program, and if accepted, the previously completed graduate coursework during the senior year is counted as transfer credit toward the completion of the master’s degree.
